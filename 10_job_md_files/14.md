ResourceCo

**The Opportunity**  
We have an exciting opportunity for a highly capable Sustainability Manager to play a key role in shaping and delivering our organisation’s ESG agenda. This is a strategic, enterprise-wide role that will see you partnering across all areas of the business, as well as with customers, regulators and industry bodies, to embed sustainability into how we operate and grow.
This position is ideally suited to someone who thrives in a hands-on, influence-led environment, where success is driven through collaboration, stakeholder engagement and the ability to translate strategy into practical outcomes.
  
**About the Role**  
Reporting into senior leadership, you will act as a trusted advisor and subject matter expert, influencing decision-making and partnering with stakeholders to deliver sustainability initiatives that align with business objectives.
Key responsibilities include:
  * Leading and influencing the development and delivery of the organisation’s ESG strategy 
  * Partnering with operational and commercial teams to embed sustainability into day-to-day decision-making 
  * Shaping and delivering mandatory climate-related disclosures and reporting frameworks 
  * Monitoring and interpreting evolving regulatory requirements, and influencing business responses to ensure compliance and strategic alignment 
  * Building strong, credible relationships with customers, regulators and industry bodies to represent the organisation’s sustainability position 
  * Driving initiatives across circular economy, resource recovery and decarbonisation 
  * Supporting the identification and delivery of sustainability opportunities that create commercial and environmental value 
  * Influencing internal stakeholders to uplift capability and awareness across ESG priorities 


  
**About You**  
You are a strategic thinker who is equally comfortable operating at both a big-picture and delivery level. You bring strong influencing and partnering capability, with the ability to engage a broad range of stakeholders and drive outcomes without direct authority.
You will also demonstrate:
  * Proven experience in sustainability, ESG, environment or a related discipline 
  * Strong understanding of climate reporting frameworks and regulatory environments 
  * Highly developed stakeholder engagement and influencing skills, including engaging with those at a senior executive level
  * Commercial acumen and the ability to align sustainability initiatives with business outcomes 
  * Strong communication skills, with the ability to translate complex concepts into practical actions 
  * A proactive, solutions-focused approach with the ability to work autonomously 


  
**Why Join ResourceCo?**  
This is a unique opportunity to take on a visible, high-impact role within a growing organisation committed to sustainability, circularity and decarbonisation. You’ll have the opportunity to influence meaningful change, partner with a wide range of stakeholders and shape how the business responds to evolving environmental and regulatory expectations.  
In addition, we offer:
  * Secure, full-time position with consistent work
  * Generous Paid Parental Leave
  * Training and development opportunities
  * Supportive team environment
  * Employee Assistance Program (EAP) for you and your family
  * Work in a growing industry focused on sustainability


**Apply now!**

**The Opportunity**  
We have an exciting opportunity for a highly capable Sustainability Manager to play a key role in shaping and delivering our organisation’s ESG agenda. This is a strategic, enterprise-wide role that will see you partnering across all areas of the business, as well as with customers, regulators and industry bodies, to embed sustainability into how we operate and grow.
This position is ideally suited to someone who thrives in a hands-on, influence-led environment, where success is driven through collaboration, stakeholder engagement and the ability to translate strategy into practical outcomes.
  
**About the Role**  
Reporting into senior leadership, you will act as a trusted advisor and subject matter expert, influencing decision-making and partnering with stakeholders to deliver sustainability initiatives that align with business objectives.
Key responsibilities include:
  * Leading and influencing the development and delivery of the organisation’s ESG strategy 
  * Partnering with operational and commercial teams to embed sustainability into day-to-day decision-making 
  * Shaping and delivering mandatory climate-related disclosures and reporting frameworks 
  * Monitoring and interpreting evolving regulatory requirements, and influencing business responses to ensure compliance and strategic alignment 
  * Building strong, credible relationships with customers, regulators and industry bodies to represent the organisation’s sustainability position 
  * Driving initiatives across circular economy, resource recovery and decarbonisation 
  * Supporting the identification and delivery of sustainability opportunities that create commercial and environmental value 
  * Influencing internal stakeholders to uplift capability and awareness across ESG priorities 


  
**About You**  
You are a strategic thinker who is equally comfortable operating at both a big-picture and delivery level. You bring strong influencing and partnering capability, with the ability to engage a broad range of stakeholders and drive outcomes without direct authority.
You will also demonstrate:
  * Proven experience in sustainability, ESG, environment or a related discipline 
  * Strong understanding of climate reporting frameworks and regulatory environments 
  * Highly developed stakeholder engagement and influencing skills, including engaging with those at a senior executive level
  * Commercial acumen and the ability to align sustainability initiatives with business outcomes 
  * Strong communication skills, with the ability to translate complex concepts into practical actions 
  * A proactive, solutions-focused approach with the ability to work autonomously 


  
**Why Join ResourceCo?**  
This is a unique opportunity to take on a visible, high-impact role within a growing organisation committed to sustainability, circularity and decarbonisation. You’ll have the opportunity to influence meaningful change, partner with a wide range of stakeholders and shape how the business responds to evolving environmental and regulatory expectations.  
In addition, we offer:
  * Secure, full-time position with consistent work
  * Generous Paid Parental Leave
  * Training and development opportunities
  * Supportive team environment
  * Employee Assistance Program (EAP) for you and your family
  * Work in a growing industry focused on sustainability
