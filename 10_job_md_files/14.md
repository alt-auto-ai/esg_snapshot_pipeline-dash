### Learn how a merit list or pool may be used
  
Applicants suitable for the role but not offered the position for the current vacancy, may be placed in a merit list or pool. A merit list or pool may be used for up to 18 months.

### ACCC Graduate Program 2027
Adelaide SA, Brisbane QLD, Canberra ACT, Darwin NT, Hobart TAS, Melbourne VIC, Perth WA, Sydney NSW, Townsville QLD
**Is the ACCC Graduate Program on your wish list?**
  

It’s definitely worth considering. As you begin mapping out your career plans for 2027, you’re likely browsing through some of the big employers in the graduate job market. The ACCC offers opportunities that are just as impactful. Take a moment to discover what makes us unique - you might find that we’re exactly what you’re looking for.
  

We are an independent statutory authority that administers the  _Competition and Consumer Act 2010_ and other Acts. We work to promote effective competition and fair trading in the marketplace to benefit consumers, business and the community, and efficiency in the delivery of certain infrastructure services.
  

Our work impacts the lives of nearly every Australian.

**Why the ACCC?**
  

We’re a great place to work because:
  * we are highly regarded for the work we do
  * we work on an array of different and exciting matters
  * we see the difference we are making in the Australian community, particularly through high-profile matters such as the NBN, electricity pricing, fuel, airlines, product safety, scams, agriculture, criminal cartels, consumer data, digital identity and more
  * our culture encourages continuous development and inclusion, and our people are highly engaged and passionate about what we do.


  

If you have tertiary qualifications, and you want the work you do to matter, we believe you could be a good fit and we’d encourage you to apply. The program offers two streams: General and Digital and Data.
  

**The General stream** is open to graduates from a wide range of disciplines that may include, but not limited to, Economics, Law, Finance and Accounting, Business and Government, Arts and Design, and Science or Humanities.
  

**The Digital and Data stream** requires expertise in digital and data disciplines that may include, but not limited to, Information Technology, Data Science, Computer Science, Data Analytics, Mathematics, or Engineering.
  

You will have:
  * the opportunity to progress to an APS5 after completion of the program
  * challenging rotations across the agency with opportunities to try new things
  * ongoing learning and development opportunities
  * flexible working arrangements, including flexible hours and the option to work from home up to 3 days per week
  * regular opportunities to network, share experiences and develop relationships,
  * an entry level program team and assigned buddy
  * a workplace that promotes diversity, inclusion and equal opportunity.


  

**Want even more benefits?**
  

In addition to the benefits of the graduate program, as an ongoing employee of the ACCC you will also be eligible for:﻿
  * an attractive remuneration package, including 15.4% superannuation and annual salary reviews based on performance
  * extensive learning and development opportunities to support your career at the ACCC
    * excellent leave benefits, including:4 weeks annual leave each year (pro-rata for part-time employees) with the option to purchase additional annual leave
    * 18 weeks of paid parental leave for primary caregivers (pro-rata for part-time employees)
    * 8 weeks of paid parental leave for secondary caregivers, building to 18 weeks over the life of the agreement (pro-rata for part-time employees)
    * 6 days leave for First Nations employees (used over 2 years) for cultural and ceremonial purposes, plus 1 day NAIDOC leave
    * 3 days cultural leave per year for essential religious or cultural obligations
    * end of year closedown between the December and January public holiday period
    * flex leave and time off in lieu arrangements.

#### Getting to know the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission
The ACCC is an independent statutory authority that administers the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 and other Acts. The ACCC works to promote effective competition and fair trading in the market place to benefit consumers, business and the community, and efficiency in the delivery of certain infrastructure services. The AER is a part of the ACCC. The AER is Australia's national energy market regulator and has an independent board. The AER shares employees, resources and facilities with the ACCC.
