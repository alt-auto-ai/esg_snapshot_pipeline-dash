Australian Competition and Consumer Commission
### Recruitment Team Leader
Canberra ACT
  * **Do you want to work at an agency where each day reveals new opportunities to work on some of the biggest issues facing consumers and businesses and have opportunities to pursue new and exciting pathways?**
  * **Do you want to work at a place where smart and dedicated people from all walks of life collaborate to work on meaningful matters?**
  * **Do you want to use your APS recruitment expertise and strong customer service to help managers build the skills the AER needs to deliver its strategic plan?**


  

Then this is the place for you! At the AER, we work to make energy consumers better off, now and in the future. We focus on ensuring a secure, reliable and affordable energy future for Australia as it transitions to net zero emissions. Our high performing culture is built on a foundation of care, support and inclusion. It’s why we’re driven by connection and collaboration, sharing our skills, knowledge and support with each other freely and frequently. It’s why we prioritise the wellbeing of our people and empower them to work autonomously, flexibly and courageously. We believe the best version of you is the one that’s true to who you are – and that’s the you we want working alongside us.
  

We value flexibility, this role is available on a full-time, part-time or job-share basis.
  

**Applicants from diverse backgrounds including First Nations peoples, people with disability and those from different cultural backgrounds are encouraged to apply.**
#### The key duties of the position include:
  * Manage the AER Recruitment Team’s day-to-day activities including:
    * Supporting managers to identify and plan their recruitment needs.
    * Advertising and promoting employment opportunities.
    * Administering the AER’s online recruitment system including preparing and receiving applications, supporting managers to record recruitment recommendations, advising applicants of the outcome of their application, preparing and issuing offers of employment, facilitating the onboarding process and maintaining the AER’s position establishment.
    * Facilitating merit lists and transfers with other APS agencies.
  * Support the Assistant Director, Recruitment, Capability and Planning to develop and deliver the Recruitment Team’s work plan.
  * Collaborate with key AER stakeholders to develop, deliver and maintain recruitment plans that will recruit the capabilities the AER requires to deliver its strategic plan.
  * Support, advise and influence employees and managers on recruitment related matters, policies and procedures.
  * Undertake other duties, as required.


#### Eligibility
**About you**
  

In order to succeed in the role, you will have:
  

  * Specialist knowledge of APS recruitment frameworks and related legislation, strategies, policies, guidance, systems and practices.
  * Demonstrated ability to collate and assess information to develop, deliver and maintain recruitment plans.
  * Demonstrated experience providing exceptional client service, preferably in a relevant human resources field.
  * Relevant tertiary qualifications in Human Resources or a related field such as psychology or be working towards, or willingness to gain professional accreditation in Human Resources. 


  

To find out more about us and the role, please read the Candidate Kit.
#### Notes
The salary rates incorporate the agency pay increases which take effective from 12 March 2026 under the ACCC Enterprise Agreement 2024-2027.

#### Getting to know the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission
The ACCC is an independent statutory authority that administers the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 and other Acts. The ACCC works to promote effective competition and fair trading in the market place to benefit consumers, business and the community, and efficiency in the delivery of certain infrastructure services. The AER is a part of the ACCC. The AER is Australia's national energy market regulator and has an independent board. The AER shares employees, resources and facilities with the ACCC.
