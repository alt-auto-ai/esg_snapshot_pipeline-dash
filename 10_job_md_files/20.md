**Ready to Elevate Your Career? Join Us!**
EGA is on the lookout for passionate and talented individuals ready to take their careers to new heights in the dynamic field of environmental consulting. If you are enthusiastic about making a real impact on the environment and are eager to contribute to cutting-edge projects, we want to hear from you!
**About us:**
We are a leading environmental consulting firm specializing in high-end strategic approaches to Contaminated Land Management. Our expertise lies in the assessment, management, remediation, and validation of contaminated land across Australia.
**Job Description:**
We are seeking a motivated Graduate / Entry-Level Environmental Consultant to join our ambitious and diverse Contaminated Land Management team in Sydney. As part of the team, you will be involved in fieldwork, data analysis, technical report writing, and continuous learning under the guidance of our experienced staff.
This is more than just a job; it is an opportunity to contribute to a sustainable future while working in an environment that fosters growth and creativity. 
**Responsibilities:**
  * Conduct fieldwork, including sampling of soil, groundwater, air, soil vapor, and landfill gas. 
  * Analyze data and contribute to technical report writing with support from Senior / Principal staff.
  * Embrace a continuous learning mindset.


**Who you are:**
  * Degree in Science or Environmental Engineering.
  * 0-2 years of relevant work experience.
  * Strong time management, organizational, and problem-solving skills.
  * Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  * Adaptability to the diverse nature of consultancy work.
  * Quick learner with a passion for achieving best practice outcomes.
  * Ability to work well in a close-knit team environment.
  * Strong work ethic with a desire for learning.
  * Friendly, proactive, and self-motivated.
  * Comfortable with travel within NSW and interstate.


**Cultural fit:**
We are looking for a candidate who is motivated, responsible, respectful, and a team player. We take extreme pride in our work and team, offering a fun, flexible work environment you can be proud of, and where you can develop your technical skills and confidence in contaminated land management.
**What You'll Get:**
  * Competitive remuneration package.
  * Flexible work arrangement.
  * Continuous learning and career progression opportunities. 
  * Job satisfaction working within our motivated and enthusiastic team.
  * Opportunity to work on exciting projects across Australia.
  * Mentoring, coaching, and networking opportunities. 
  * Paid industry memberships and attendance at industry-leading events.
  * Social events, team-building activities, and an all-paid weekend away.
  * Brand new office in one of the trendiest suburbs in Sydney!


**How to Apply:**
Ready to be part of our dynamic team in our brand-new office? Please apply through this ad, providing your**CV** and **Cover Letter** explaining why you would be a great fit for our team. Unfortunately, we will not be accepting phone calls regarding this position.
_Note: Applicants must have a valid driver's license and be authorized to work in Australia for consideration._

**Ready to Elevate Your Career? Join Us!**
EGA is on the lookout for passionate and talented individuals ready to take their careers to new heights in the dynamic field of environmental consulting. If you are enthusiastic about making a real impact on the environment and are eager to contribute to cutting-edge projects, we want to hear from you!
**About us:**
We are a leading environmental consulting firm specializing in high-end strategic approaches to Contaminated Land Management. Our expertise lies in the assessment, management, remediation, and validation of contaminated land across Australia.
**Job Description:**
We are seeking a motivated Graduate / Entry-Level Environmental Consultant to join our ambitious and diverse Contaminated Land Management team in Sydney. As part of the team, you will be involved in fieldwork, data analysis, technical report writing, and continuous learning under the guidance of our experienced staff.
This is more than just a job; it is an opportunity to contribute to a sustainable future while working in an environment that fosters growth and creativity. 
**Responsibilities:**
  * Conduct fieldwork, including sampling of soil, groundwater, air, soil vapor, and landfill gas. 
  * Analyze data and contribute to technical report writing with support from Senior / Principal staff.
  * Embrace a continuous learning mindset.


**Who you are:**
  * Degree in Science or Environmental Engineering.
  * 0-2 years of relevant work experience.
  * Strong time management, organizational, and problem-solving skills.
  * Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  * Adaptability to the diverse nature of consultancy work.
  * Quick learner with a passion for achieving best practice outcomes.
  * Ability to work well in a close-knit team environment.
  * Strong work ethic with a desire for learning.
  * Friendly, proactive, and self-motivated.
  * Comfortable with travel within NSW and interstate.


**Cultural fit:**
We are looking for a candidate who is motivated, responsible, respectful, and a team player. We take extreme pride in our work and team, offering a fun, flexible work environment you can be proud of, and where you can develop your technical skills and confidence in contaminated land management.
**What You'll Get:**
  * Competitive remuneration package.
  * Flexible work arrangement.
  * Continuous learning and career progression opportunities. 
  * Job satisfaction working within our motivated and enthusiastic team.
  * Opportunity to work on exciting projects across Australia.
  * Mentoring, coaching, and networking opportunities. 
  * Paid industry memberships and attendance at industry-leading events.
  * Social events, team-building activities, and an all-paid weekend away.
  * Brand new office in one of the trendiest suburbs in Sydney!


**How to Apply:**
Ready to be part of our dynamic team in our brand-new office? Please apply through this ad, providing your**CV** and **Cover Letter** explaining why you would be a great fit for our team. Unfortunately, we will not be accepting phone calls regarding this position.
_Note: Applicants must have a valid driver's license and be authorized to work in Australia for consideration._
