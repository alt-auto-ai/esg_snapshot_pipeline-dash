HOBAN Recruitment has been appointed by Marist180 to conduct the executive search for its next Chief Executive Officer.  
  
**About Marist180**  
For young people experiencing homelessness, trauma, family breakdown, and exclusion from education or employment, Marist180 exists to create hope, dignity and positive change.  
  
Marist180 is a Catholic not‑for‑profit organisation inspired by the Marist tradition and grounded in Catholic social teaching. The organisation is committed to walking alongside young people and families at risk, particularly those who are marginalised and vulnerable.  
  
Operating more than 50 homes and service sites across Sydney, Regional NSW and the ACT, Marist180 supports approximately 1,300 young people and families each year through services including:
  * Residential care and supported independent living
  * Homelessness and family support services
  * Education and employment pathways
  * Culturally responsive and community‑based programs


With a workforce of approximately 500 staff, Marist180 delivers trauma‑informed, strengths‑based services underpinned by compassion, respect, justice and faith‑inspired service.  
  
**The Opportunity**  
Reporting to the Board, the Chief Executive Officer will provide values‑driven, faith‑informed leadership at a pivotal moment in Marist180's journey.  
  
The CEO will lead an organisation‑wide renewal and performance improvement agenda, strengthening governance, rebuilding leadership capability, restoring stability, and ensuring Marist180 is well positioned for its next phase of mission and impact.  
  
This role requires a leader with exceptional communication skills and emotional intelligence, able to engage authentically with staff, young people, Church partners, government, funders and the wider community.  
  
**Key areas of responsibility include:**

  * Providing strong financial stewardship and ensuring long‑term viability
