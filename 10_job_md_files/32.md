Department of Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water (DCCEEW)
### Senior Program Officer
Adelaide SA, Brisbane QLD, Canberra ACT, Darwin NT, Hobart TAS, Melbourne VIC, Perth WA, Sydney NSW
The Clean Energy Investment and Facilitation Division aims to accelerate the deployment of renewable energy infrastructure that benefits all Australians. The Division consists of three branches: Clean Energy Investment, First Nations and Clean Energy Facilitation, and Capacity Investment Scheme Delivery and Governance.
  

The focus of the Clean Energy Investment Branch is to accelerate the roll-out of key energy infrastructure. This includes renewable and clean dispatchable projects through the Capacity Investment Scheme (CIS), and the deployment of transmission through the Rewiring the Nation (RtN) program. It also includes tracking and responding to key systemic blockers to clean energy investment and project delivery. The branch is made up of five teams: 
  * Economic Analysis & Modelling 
  * Clean Energy Investment Policy 
  * Clean Energy Investment Engagement
  * Transmission Projects
  * RtN Partnerships and Governance. 


  

The Rewiring the Nation Office – Partnerships and Governance (RtN P&G) supports major investment in Australia’s electricity transmission infrastructure to help achieve national energy and climate goals. Working with the Clean Energy Finance Corporation, energy market bodies, and state and territory governments, we deliver the $21.2 billion Rewiring the Nation program. Joining RtN P&G means contributing to a significant national initiative that supports Australia’s emissions targets. You’ll work in a flexible, inclusive and collaborative team, engaging across the energy sector and supported by strong leadership, professional development, flexible work options and a focus on career growth and work–life balance.
  

For more information about us please visit our [website](https://www.apsjobs.gov.au/s/external-link?url=).
#### The key duties of the position include:
We are seeking a highly motivated individual that has an interest in the work we do and a willingness to work as part of a team in a fast-paced environment. You will:
  * Contribute to governance and compliance activities within the program or project.
  * Assist with risk management tasks, including identifying, monitoring and helping to manage project risks.
  * Support program reporting processes by contributing to performance updates, status reports and other required reporting products.
  * Contribute to briefing processes, including preparing and coordinating program‑related briefs for senior executives and Ministers.
  * Participate as a member of a fast‑paced team, contributing to day‑to‑day program operations and supporting team priorities.


  

**For detailed information about the job-specific capabilities for this role** , please view the **Job Description** which can be downloaded from our website - the **Apply Now** link will take you there.
#### Eligibility
**Citizenship** - to be eligible for employment with the Department of Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water you must be an Australian citizen.
  

**Security Clearance** - this position requires a **Baseline Vetting** security clearance. You will be required to obtain and maintain a clearance at this level. 
  

**Pre-employment checks** - your suitability for employment will be assessed through a pre-employment screening process. This process includes a requirement to undergo and satisfy a National Police Check, referee checks, character clearance and where required a pre-employment medical assessment, specified mandatory qualification(s) validation and a probation period. 
  

RecruitAbility applies to this vacancy. Under the RecruitAbility scheme you will be invited to participate in further assessment activity for the vacancy if you choose to apply under the scheme; declare you have a disability; and meet the minimum requirements for the job.
