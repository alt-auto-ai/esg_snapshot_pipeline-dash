**About the role**
We are seeking an **Environmental Scientist** to join our team in a full‑time, permanent position. In this role, you will contribute to a diverse range of environmental projects across Australia, and other regions, supporting the preparation of environmental assessments, management plans, audits, and technical reports. 
You’ll work closely with experienced professionals, helping to deliver high‑quality outcomes while developing your technical skills and industry experience. This role offers an excellent opportunity for a junior‑level professional to build capability, gain exposure to varied project work, and play a meaningful part in delivering positive environmental outcomes. 
**What you’ll do:**
  * Contribute to AARC projects across Australia, Southeast Asia, and other regions.
  * Prepare Environmental Impact Statements, Environmental Management Systems, Environmental Authority applications, Environmental Management Plans, and Plans of Operations.
  * Conduct environmental audits and environmental due‑diligence assessments.
  * Provide environmental consulting advice to internal teams and clients.
  * Report progress against timelines and budgets to Managers and Project Managers.
  * Develop reports, figures, and supporting documentation for project deliverables.


**To be successful in this role, you will need:**
  * Hold a tertiary qualification in Environmental Science, Ecology, or a related discipline.
  * Bring 1-3 years of experience in environmental consulting or a related field.
  * Demonstrate strong attention to detail, project management, time management, and organisational skills.
  * Maintain full Australian working rights (mandatory).
  * Hold a valid Australian driver’s licence.
  * Work collaboratively and proactively, with a strong commitment to quality and client service.


**About Us**
APEM Group is leading global environment consultancy providing expert advice to clients in a wide range of industries including water, renewables, infrastructure, marine and ports, transport, construction, and development. Following significant growth journey with operations based in the UK, EU and USA, APEM Group strategically expanded into Australia in early 2024. Our presence in the Australian market will further enhance the aim of expanding on our consultancy services in both the Australian market and wider Asia Pacific region as part of the next stage of our Global growth journey.
As part of the APEM Group, AARC Environmental Solutions provides expert environmental solutions to the resource, critical minerals, and development sectors. Established in Queensland in 1996, AARC has a proven track record of delivering innovative, data and science-led insights to clients across Australia and Asia. We offer a full range of environmental services to support successful and efficient project delivery from planning and approvals through to operations and closure with a strong understanding of regional legislation and regulatory compliance.

**About the role**
We are seeking an **Environmental Scientist** to join our team in a full‑time, permanent position. In this role, you will contribute to a diverse range of environmental projects across Australia, and other regions, supporting the preparation of environmental assessments, management plans, audits, and technical reports. 
You’ll work closely with experienced professionals, helping to deliver high‑quality outcomes while developing your technical skills and industry experience. This role offers an excellent opportunity for a junior‑level professional to build capability, gain exposure to varied project work, and play a meaningful part in delivering positive environmental outcomes. 
**What you’ll do:**
  * Contribute to AARC projects across Australia, Southeast Asia, and other regions.
  * Prepare Environmental Impact Statements, Environmental Management Systems, Environmental Authority applications, Environmental Management Plans, and Plans of Operations.
  * Conduct environmental audits and environmental due‑diligence assessments.
  * Provide environmental consulting advice to internal teams and clients.
  * Report progress against timelines and budgets to Managers and Project Managers.
  * Develop reports, figures, and supporting documentation for project deliverables.


**To be successful in this role, you will need:**
  * Hold a tertiary qualification in Environmental Science, Ecology, or a related discipline.
  * Bring 1-3 years of experience in environmental consulting or a related field.
  * Demonstrate strong attention to detail, project management, time management, and organisational skills.
  * Maintain full Australian working rights (mandatory).
  * Hold a valid Australian driver’s licence.
  * Work collaboratively and proactively, with a strong commitment to quality and client service.


**About Us**
APEM Group is leading global environment consultancy providing expert advice to clients in a wide range of industries including water, renewables, infrastructure, marine and ports, transport, construction, and development. Following significant growth journey with operations based in the UK, EU and USA, APEM Group strategically expanded into Australia in early 2024. Our presence in the Australian market will further enhance the aim of expanding on our consultancy services in both the Australian market and wider Asia Pacific region as part of the next stage of our Global growth journey.
As part of the APEM Group, AARC Environmental Solutions provides expert environmental solutions to the resource, critical minerals, and development sectors. Established in Queensland in 1996, AARC has a proven track record of delivering innovative, data and science-led insights to clients across Australia and Asia. We offer a full range of environmental services to support successful and efficient project delivery from planning and approvals through to operations and closure with a strong understanding of regional legislation and regulatory compliance.

**About the role**
We are seeking an **Environmental Scientist** to join our team in a full‑time, permanent position. In this role, you will contribute to a diverse range of environmental projects across Australia, and other regions, supporting the preparation of environmental assessments, management plans, audits, and technical reports. 
You’ll work closely with experienced professionals, helping to deliver high‑quality outcomes while developing your technical skills and industry experience. This role offers an excellent opportunity for a junior‑level professional to build capability, gain exposure to varied project work, and play a meaningful part in delivering positive environmental outcomes. 
**What you’ll do:**
  * Contribute to AARC projects across Australia, Southeast Asia, and other regions.
  * Prepare Environmental Impact Statements, Environmental Management Systems, Environmental Authority applications, Environmental Management Plans, and Plans of Operations.
  * Conduct environmental audits and environmental due‑diligence assessments.
  * Provide environmental consulting advice to internal teams and clients.
  * Report progress against timelines and budgets to Managers and Project Managers.
  * Develop reports, figures, and supporting documentation for project deliverables.


**To be successful in this role, you will need:**
  * Hold a tertiary qualification in Environmental Science, Ecology, or a related discipline.
  * Bring 1-3 years of experience in environmental consulting or a related field.
  * Demonstrate strong attention to detail, project management, time management, and organisational skills.
  * Maintain full Australian working rights (mandatory).
  * Hold a valid Australian driver’s licence.
  * Work collaboratively and proactively, with a strong commitment to quality and client service.


**About Us**
APEM Group is leading global environment consultancy providing expert advice to clients in a wide range of industries including water, renewables, infrastructure, marine and ports, transport, construction, and development. Following significant growth journey with operations based in the UK, EU and USA, APEM Group strategically expanded into Australia in early 2024. Our presence in the Australian market will further enhance the aim of expanding on our consultancy services in both the Australian market and wider Asia Pacific region as part of the next stage of our Global growth journey.
As part of the APEM Group, AARC Environmental Solutions provides expert environmental solutions to the resource, critical minerals, and development sectors. Established in Queensland in 1996, AARC has a proven track record of delivering innovative, data and science-led insights to clients across Australia and Asia. We offer a full range of environmental services to support successful and efficient project delivery from planning and approvals through to operations and closure with a strong understanding of regional legislation and regulatory compliance.
