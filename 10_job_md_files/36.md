**About the role**  
  
ENV Solutions is seeking a Senior Consultant to join our Gold Coast or Ballina offices. Specialising in Environmental Management for EA / EPL sites, civil and construction projects, you will be leading the delivery of projects in southeast Queensland and Northern NSW. You will also play a key role in mentoring and developing junior team members. The role will be diverse, encompassing project management, field programs, reporting and building client relationships.
Enjoy the benefits of a coastal lifestyle with convenient access to city infrastructure, all while making a tangible impact through your work.
**Role responsibilities:**
  * Management of environmental management projects from scoping and writing fee proposals through to managing team, program, budget, deliverables and invoicing.
  * Develop and Construction Environmental Management Plans (CEMPs) covering noise, vibration, air quality and acid sulfate soils.
  * Lead field programs to assess soil, water, groundwater, air quality, noise and vibration.
  * Write detailed technical reports on investigations and solutions designed for client-specific requirements.
  * Conduct environmental audits and inspections to ensure client adherence to Environmental Protection Licences (EPL) and state planning provisions.
  * Develop client relationships, generate leads and develop service proposals to win new work.


**Experience, skills and qualifications we're looking for:**
  * Bachelor of Environmental Science / Management/ Engineering
  * Current Australian drivers' licence and ability to drive manual 4WD vehicle
  * CPESC accreditation, TMR, TfNSW, environmental auditing experience (desirable)
  * 7-10 years' experience, preferably in a consulting role
  * Experience erosion and sediment control experience
  * Expertise in designing, planning and implementing environmental planning projects, including management of stakeholders
  * Excellent report-writing skills
  * Experience scoping projects and preparing fee proposals.


**What we offer**
At ENV Solutions, we take pride in our positive, close-knit team culture that enables our team to thrive. As a senior consultant, you’ll enjoy:
  * Opportunities to shape your role in a growing, mid-sized consultancy
  * Flexibility to help you balance professional responsibilities with life outside of work
  * A supportive environment that values wellbeing, with access to our employee wellbeing program and related initiatives
  * A collaborative team atmosphere, where your input is valued and regular social catch-ups help keep things connected and enjoyable
  * Competitive salary package in acknowledgement of your qualifications and experience.


**About us**  
  
ENV Solutions is a forward-thinking consultancy delivering sustainable solutions that make a real difference for our clients and the environment. With offices in the Gold Coast, Ballina, Coffs Harbour, and Newcastle, we excel in water treatment, occupational hygiene, environmental assessment, contaminated land management, and more. Recognised as an Employer of Choice in 2024 and honoured with multiple awards - including Engineers Australia Project of the Year (2023) - we foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and professional growth, ensuring you thrive while contributing to a greener future.

**About the role**  
  
ENV Solutions is seeking a Senior Consultant to join our Gold Coast or Ballina offices. Specialising in Environmental Management for EA / EPL sites, civil and construction projects, you will be leading the delivery of projects in southeast Queensland and Northern NSW. You will also play a key role in mentoring and developing junior team members. The role will be diverse, encompassing project management, field programs, reporting and building client relationships.
Enjoy the benefits of a coastal lifestyle with convenient access to city infrastructure, all while making a tangible impact through your work.
**Role responsibilities:**
  * Management of environmental management projects from scoping and writing fee proposals through to managing team, program, budget, deliverables and invoicing.
  * Develop and Construction Environmental Management Plans (CEMPs) covering noise, vibration, air quality and acid sulfate soils.
  * Lead field programs to assess soil, water, groundwater, air quality, noise and vibration.
  * Write detailed technical reports on investigations and solutions designed for client-specific requirements.
  * Conduct environmental audits and inspections to ensure client adherence to Environmental Protection Licences (EPL) and state planning provisions.
  * Develop client relationships, generate leads and develop service proposals to win new work.


**Experience, skills and qualifications we're looking for:**
  * Bachelor of Environmental Science / Management/ Engineering
  * Current Australian drivers' licence and ability to drive manual 4WD vehicle
  * CPESC accreditation, TMR, TfNSW, environmental auditing experience (desirable)
  * 7-10 years' experience, preferably in a consulting role
  * Experience erosion and sediment control experience
  * Expertise in designing, planning and implementing environmental planning projects, including management of stakeholders
  * Excellent report-writing skills
  * Experience scoping projects and preparing fee proposals.


**What we offer**
At ENV Solutions, we take pride in our positive, close-knit team culture that enables our team to thrive. As a senior consultant, you’ll enjoy:
  * Opportunities to shape your role in a growing, mid-sized consultancy
  * Flexibility to help you balance professional responsibilities with life outside of work
  * A supportive environment that values wellbeing, with access to our employee wellbeing program and related initiatives
  * A collaborative team atmosphere, where your input is valued and regular social catch-ups help keep things connected and enjoyable
  * Competitive salary package in acknowledgement of your qualifications and experience.


**About us**  
  
ENV Solutions is a forward-thinking consultancy delivering sustainable solutions that make a real difference for our clients and the environment. With offices in the Gold Coast, Ballina, Coffs Harbour, and Newcastle, we excel in water treatment, occupational hygiene, environmental assessment, contaminated land management, and more. Recognised as an Employer of Choice in 2024 and honoured with multiple awards - including Engineers Australia Project of the Year (2023) - we foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and professional growth, ensuring you thrive while contributing to a greener future.

Enjoy the benefits of a coastal lifestyle with convenient access to city infrastructure, all while making a tangible impact through your work.
**Role responsibilities:**
  * Management of environmental management projects from scoping and writing fee proposals through to managing team, program, budget, deliverables and invoicing.
  * Develop and Construction Environmental Management Plans (CEMPs) covering noise, vibration, air quality and acid sulfate soils.
  * Lead field programs to assess soil, water, groundwater, air quality, noise and vibration.
  * Write detailed technical reports on investigations and solutions designed for client-specific requirements.
  * Conduct environmental audits and inspections to ensure client adherence to Environmental Protection Licences (EPL) and state planning provisions.
  * Develop client relationships, generate leads and develop service proposals to win new work.


**Experience, skills and qualifications we're looking for:**
  * Bachelor of Environmental Science / Management/ Engineering
  * Current Australian drivers' licence and ability to drive manual 4WD vehicle
  * CPESC accreditation, TMR, TfNSW, environmental auditing experience (desirable)
  * 7-10 years' experience, preferably in a consulting role
  * Experience erosion and sediment control experience
  * Expertise in designing, planning and implementing environmental planning projects, including management of stakeholders
  * Excellent report-writing skills
  * Experience scoping projects and preparing fee proposals.
