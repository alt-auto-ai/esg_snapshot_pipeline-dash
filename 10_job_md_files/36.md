## **CEO | Private Equity Backed Scale Up**
Location: Melbourne Reports to: Founder / Sansoni Management Type: Full-Time 
Join a high-growth, founder-led venture with global ambition, validated traction, and strong early momentum.
  * Product launched
  * Beta app live
  * 10,000 users onboard
  * 2,000 deals already flowing through the platform
  * Pre-Seed funding in place, ready for a seed-scale raise


This isn’t a concept. It’s already working. Now we need the right CEO to take it global 
**About the company:** Backed by Sansoni Management, this venture is building a powerful deal engine for entrepreneurs, connecting them with opportunities, services and growth pathways in one ecosystem.
  * Early stage complete with live product in market
  * Strong initial traction and user adoption
  * Positioned for global expansion
  * Focused on turning traction into a category defining business
  * Supported by an established ecosystem


**About the role:** This is a hands-on CEO position leading the next phase of global growth and scale.
  * Work directly with the Founder on execution and direction
  * Scale what’s already working
  * Raise capital to accelerate growth
  * Lead product commercialization
  * Build structure, systems, and team for global expansion


You are responsible for turning early traction into a scalable, repeatable, high-performance business.
  
**What You’ll Do (The Execution)** Lead the next phase of growth and scale
  * Drive capital raising, ideally with exposure to US markets
  * Partner closely with the Founder on execution and direction
  * Build structure, systems and accountability across the business
  * Turn early traction into repeatable, scalable performance
  * Oversee go-to-market and user growth at scale
  * Represent the business with investors, partners and in market


**Who You Are (The DNA)** We are looking for a proven builder who understands what it takes to scale.
  * Experience scaling a product, platform or SaaS business
  * Strong capital raising experience (US exposure highly regarded)
  * Commercially sharp, you understand numbers, not just narrative
  * Comfortable operating in a fast-paced, founder-led environment
  * Able to move between strategy and execution easily
  * A builder. A doer. Someone who gets things done


**Culture** High pace, high-performance environment
  * Founder-led with direct access to decision making
  * Built on accountability and execution
  * Operates with urgency and ambition
  * Focused on building something meaningful at scale


People thrive here because they want to build, not maintain.
  
**Benefits** Not starting from zero, this already has material traction
  * Direct partnership with a high-performing Founder
  * Backed by Sansoni Management and an established ecosystem
  * Real opportunity to build a global, category-leading business 


If you want a polished corporate role, this isn’t it. If you want to build something meaningful at scale, click **APPLY**.

## **CEO | Private Equity Backed Scale Up**
Location: Melbourne Reports to: Founder / Sansoni Management Type: Full-Time 
Join a high-growth, founder-led venture with global ambition, validated traction, and strong early momentum.
  * Product launched
  * Beta app live
  * 10,000 users onboard
  * 2,000 deals already flowing through the platform
  * Pre-Seed funding in place, ready for a seed-scale raise


This isn’t a concept. It’s already working. Now we need the right CEO to take it global 
**About the company:** Backed by Sansoni Management, this venture is building a powerful deal engine for entrepreneurs, connecting them with opportunities, services and growth pathways in one ecosystem.
  * Early stage complete with live product in market
  * Strong initial traction and user adoption
  * Positioned for global expansion
  * Focused on turning traction into a category defining business
  * Supported by an established ecosystem


**About the role:** This is a hands-on CEO position leading the next phase of global growth and scale.
  * Work directly with the Founder on execution and direction
  * Scale what’s already working
  * Raise capital to accelerate growth
  * Lead product commercialization
  * Build structure, systems, and team for global expansion


You are responsible for turning early traction into a scalable, repeatable, high-performance business.
  
**What You’ll Do (The Execution)** Lead the next phase of growth and scale
  * Drive capital raising, ideally with exposure to US markets
  * Partner closely with the Founder on execution and direction
  * Build structure, systems and accountability across the business
  * Turn early traction into repeatable, scalable performance
  * Oversee go-to-market and user growth at scale
  * Represent the business with investors, partners and in market


**Who You Are (The DNA)** We are looking for a proven builder who understands what it takes to scale.
  * Experience scaling a product, platform or SaaS business
  * Strong capital raising experience (US exposure highly regarded)
  * Commercially sharp, you understand numbers, not just narrative
  * Comfortable operating in a fast-paced, founder-led environment
  * Able to move between strategy and execution easily
  * A builder. A doer. Someone who gets things done


**Culture** High pace, high-performance environment
  * Founder-led with direct access to decision making
  * Built on accountability and execution
  * Operates with urgency and ambition
  * Focused on building something meaningful at scale


People thrive here because they want to build, not maintain.
  
**Benefits** Not starting from zero, this already has material traction
  * Direct partnership with a high-performing Founder
  * Backed by Sansoni Management and an established ecosystem
  * Real opportunity to build a global, category-leading business 


If you want a polished corporate role, this isn’t it. If you want to build something meaningful at scale, click **APPLY**.

## **CEO | Private Equity Backed Scale Up**
Location: Melbourne Reports to: Founder / Sansoni Management Type: Full-Time 
Join a high-growth, founder-led venture with global ambition, validated traction, and strong early momentum.
  * Product launched
  * Beta app live
  * 10,000 users onboard
  * 2,000 deals already flowing through the platform
  * Pre-Seed funding in place, ready for a seed-scale raise


This isn’t a concept. It’s already working. Now we need the right CEO to take it global 
**About the company:** Backed by Sansoni Management, this venture is building a powerful deal engine for entrepreneurs, connecting them with opportunities, services and growth pathways in one ecosystem.
  * Early stage complete with live product in market
  * Strong initial traction and user adoption
  * Positioned for global expansion
  * Focused on turning traction into a category defining business
  * Supported by an established ecosystem


**About the role:** This is a hands-on CEO position leading the next phase of global growth and scale.
  * Work directly with the Founder on execution and direction
  * Scale what’s already working
  * Raise capital to accelerate growth
  * Lead product commercialization
  * Build structure, systems, and team for global expansion


You are responsible for turning early traction into a scalable, repeatable, high-performance business.
  
**What You’ll Do (The Execution)** Lead the next phase of growth and scale
  * Drive capital raising, ideally with exposure to US markets
  * Partner closely with the Founder on execution and direction
  * Build structure, systems and accountability across the business
  * Turn early traction into repeatable, scalable performance
  * Oversee go-to-market and user growth at scale
  * Represent the business with investors, partners and in market


**Who You Are (The DNA)** We are looking for a proven builder who understands what it takes to scale.
  * Experience scaling a product, platform or SaaS business
  * Strong capital raising experience (US exposure highly regarded)
  * Commercially sharp, you understand numbers, not just narrative
  * Comfortable operating in a fast-paced, founder-led environment
  * Able to move between strategy and execution easily
  * A builder. A doer. Someone who gets things done


**Culture** High pace, high-performance environment
  * Founder-led with direct access to decision making
  * Built on accountability and execution
  * Operates with urgency and ambition
  * Focused on building something meaningful at scale


People thrive here because they want to build, not maintain.
  
**Benefits** Not starting from zero, this already has material traction
  * Direct partnership with a high-performing Founder
  * Backed by Sansoni Management and an established ecosystem
  * Real opportunity to build a global, category-leading business 


If you want a polished corporate role, this isn’t it. If you want to build something meaningful at scale, click **APPLY**.
