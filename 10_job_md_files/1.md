Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Authority
### ICT Business Systems Analyst - Great Barrier Reef Aquarium
Townsville QLD
**The Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Authority (the Reef Authority)** is a Commonwealth non-corporate entity, and a statutory agency established by the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Act 1975 (Marine Park Act), reporting to the Minister for the Environment and Water. Our objective is the long-term protection, ecologically sustainable use, understanding and enjoyment of the Great Barrier Reef for all Australians and the international community through the care and development of the Marine Park.
  

We are committed to redeveloping the National Education Centre for the Great Barrier Reef and continue to offer people of all ages and abilities the opportunity to access the Reef on land with the world’s largest living tropical reef aquarium.
  

Redeveloping the **Great Barrier Reef Aquarium** will bring numerous benefits to Townsville. The iconic facility will be a place maker in the heart of our CBD, serving as an enduring symbol of Townville’s rich history and international reputation for Reef education and source of great pride for the community. Visitors can look forward to world-class living exhibitions and immersive, interactive experiences that will educate and inspire action to protect the Reef.
  

The project is being designed with operational efficiency and sustainability in mind, ensuring a contemporary facility and strong return on investment for the Australian public.
  

**The Position**
  

**The Aquarium ICT Business Systems Analyst** plays a pivotal role in delivering a comprehensive Business Enabling System to support the aquarium's visitor services, ticketing, bookings, commercial operations, and membership management. This dynamic position will work collaboratively with external vendors and internal stakeholders throughout the procurement, development, and implementation phases to ensure the system meets operational requirements and integrates seamlessly with point-of-sale and CRM platforms.
  

The role requires translating complex business needs into functional system specifications, coordinating user acceptance testing, and ensuring successful deployment across the organisation. Beyond implementation, the Aquarium ICT Business Systems Analyst will provide ongoing system stewardship, including configuration management, optimisation, user training, and support to maximise system performance and value.
  

As the internal subject matter expert, this position will ensure data integrity, system reliability, and compliance with legislative, regulatory, and organisational requirements. The Aquarium ICT Business Systems Analyst will serve as the primary liaison for technical issues and vendor management, while identifying opportunities for continuous improvement. This role offers unique opportunities to shape visitor experience technology and build digital capability within a culturally significant public institution.
  

**About You**
  

  * You will have proven experience in a business systems analyst or similar role, within a complex organisational environment, with a demonstrated ability to manage your own work program and deliver outcomes to required timeframes. 
  * You will have experience supporting the development and implementation of integrated business systems, particularly in commercial or customer-facing operations, with the ability to translate business requirements into functional specifications and support systems through to operational readiness. 
  * You will possess strong stakeholder engagement skills, with a solutions focused mindset and the ability to facilitate collaboration between business units, technical vendors, and project teams. 
  * You will have exceptional attention to detail and analytical capability, with experience in system configuration, testing coordination, and ensuring compliance with legislative, regulatory and organisational requirements. 
  * You will be an effective communicator who can provide technical guidance and user support to non-technical staff; building trusted relationships that enable you to influence positive outcomes based on your expertise and insight.


  

  

#### The key duties of the position include:
Under limited direction, the Aquarium ICT Business Systems Analyst will be responsible for the following key duties:
  

  * Undertake business requirements analysis and translate operational needs into functional system specifications for the Business Enabling System.
  * Coordinate and liaise with external vendors and project teams throughout procurement, development, implementation, and integration phases.
  * Maintain, administer, and optimise system configuration to ensure ongoing performance, data integrity, and alignment with business objectives.
  * Maintain and support integration between ticketing, point-of-sale, CRM, and membership platforms to deliver seamless visitor and commercial services.
  * Coordinate user acceptance testing, system upgrades, enhancements, and maintenance activities with internal stakeholders.
  * Provide expert user support, training, and technical guidance to staff across the organisation.
  * Ensure compliance with legislative, regulatory, and organisational requirements relating to system operations, data governance, and reporting.
  * Maintain comprehensive system documentation, processes, procedures, and performance reporting in line with required standards.


#### Eligibility
**Essential requirements**
  

  * Proven experience in administering and maintaining business/enterprise systems.
  * Must hold and maintain or be able to obtain and maintain a Working with Children Check (Queensland Blue Card). Failure to obtain/maintain a Queensland Blue Card will result in termination of employment.
  * Must comply with the Reef Authority’s Child Safety and Wellbeing Policy. Failure to agree/comply with this policy will result in termination of employment.


  

**Desirable qualifications or equivalent experience**
  

  * Tertiary qualifications in Information Technology/Computer Science/Information Systems or related discipline. 
  * Understanding of business process requirements and systems supporting them.
  * Experience working with software developers or vendors in system implementation projects
  * ITIL Foundations certification.
  * Vendor management experience.


#### Notes

  1. Be an Australian citizen at the time of applying for the position.
  2. Be able to obtain and maintain a Baseline security clearance; failure to do so will result in termination of employment.
  3. Be willing to provide identity documents and undergo an identity pre-employment check through a Document Verification Service if you are deemed to be the successful candidate.
