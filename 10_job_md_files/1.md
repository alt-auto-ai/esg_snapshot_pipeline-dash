Department of Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water (DCCEEW)
### Assistant Director, Systems Administration and Configuration (TechnologyOne)
Canberra ACT
The Finance Division supports the department to be efficient, effective, and financially sustainable through our business partnerships, the Finance Service Charter and our specialist centres of excellence. The division provides financial and management advice and support to the department, the offices of the minister and assistant ministers and to industry stakeholders. The division delivers on business objectives through the provision of high-quality customer service, advice and support and a nationally consistent approach to business, with consideration to the Government’s tight fiscal environment, its significant reform agenda and the department’s imperative to maintain financial sustainability.
  

The Budget Branch manages the processes, procedures and systems to support the development of the Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water portfolio Budget, and seeking of funding from Government. The branch supports the delivery of the Government's priorities by:
  * leading the department's involvement in the Budget Process
  * providing informed and strategic advice to the department's senior executives
  * facilitating timely preparation of fully costed new policy proposals
  * updating and coordinating Budget information, and reporting to internal and external stakeholders
  * responsibly managing the department's Budget (expense and revenue)
  * maintaining the financial management systems that support these and other activities.


  

The Systems Support and Transformation Team provides high-quality customer service, advice and support across the department in relation to its systems for finance, travel and expense management. The team works with internal and external partners to assess issues and enhancement requests, co-design solutions, perform implementation and conduct testing and evaluation.
  

For more information about us please visit our [website](https://www.apsjobs.gov.au/s/external-link?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.dcceew.gov.au%2F).
#### The key duties of the position include:
  * Provide high quality, service focussed customer support, advice and issue resolution to stakeholders across the department in relation to TechnologyOne.
  * Manage the life cycle of TechnologyOne including upgrades in accordance with vendor and ICT change management processes.
  * Identify and implement continuous improvement including the ongoing maintenance of TechnologyOne, performing requirements gathering and business process mapping, producing fit-for-purpose reporting mechanisms.
  * Build and sustain collaborative relationships with staff of the other finance teams to enable them to effectively use TechnologyOne to assist their own stakeholders across the department.
  * Work with team colleagues, maintain the suite of practical system administration and governance documents, including service charters, issue resolution procedures and access and change management policies.
  * Evaluate, interpret, and apply appropriate risk assessments to support the department’s governance, performance and risk framework.
  * Support the Director to manage the Systems Support and Transformation team including mentoring, coaching, training and development.


  

**For detailed information about the job-specific capabilities for this role** , please view the **Job Description** which can be downloaded from our website - the **Apply Now** link will take you there.
#### Eligibility
**Citizenship -** to be eligible for employment with the Department of Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water you must be an Australian citizen.
  

**Security Clearance -** this position requires a Baseline Vetting security clearance. You will be required to obtain and maintain a clearance at this level. 
  

**Pre-employment checks -** your suitability for employment will be assessed through a pre-employment screening process. This process includes a requirement to undergo and satisfy a National Police Check, referee checks, character clearance and where required a pre-employment medical assessment, specified mandatory qualification(s) validation and a probation period. 
RecruitAbility applies to this vacancy. Under the RecruitAbility scheme you will be invited to participate in further assessment activity for the vacancy if you choose to apply under the scheme; declare you have a disability; and meet the minimum requirements for the job.
