**About Lifeline Direct**
Lifeline Direct is a wholly owned subsidiary of Lifeline Australia, operating as Lifeline’s primary service delivery arm for crisis support, mental health, and suicide prevention services across Australia.
Our reach is significant:
  * National delivery of the Lifeline 
  * Local counselling services across 8 Lifeline centres in NSW and Victoria
  * Specialist and Targeted Services (STS): MensLine Australia, Suicide Call Back Service, All Hours Suicide Support, SuicideLine Victoria and bespoke digital services
  * Fee-for-service programs including 1800 Respect and the University Crisis Line
  * Corporate and community training programs
  * 28 retail shops across NSW and Victoria


Our workforce of more than 1,300 people — comprising approximately 500 paid staff and 700+ volunteers — delivers life-changing support to Australians in crisis, every single day.
**About the Role**
Reporting to the Chair of the LLD Board, the CEO is a **transformational leadership role** with a mandate to drive strategic growth, operational excellence, and deeper integration across the Lifeline group.
You will provide direction, purpose and vision to a dedicated executive team and broader workforce, and ensure LLD delivers maximum value through its Strategic Plan. Key areas of focus include:
  * Refreshing and executing LLD’s strategic plan in alignment with Lifeline Australia’s broader strategy
  * Driving financial sustainability and growth, including management of funding agreements and revenue diversification
  * Leading transformational change, including corporate services consolidation with Lifeline Australia
  * Championing innovation in digital mental health service delivery
  * Ensuring a sustainable, high-performing executive team and positive organisational culture
  * Overseeing a profitable retail network of 28 stores
  * Managing governance, risk and compliance frameworks in line with Board expectations
  * Building and leveraging senior relationships with government, funders, peak bodies and community stakeholders
  * Representing LLD externally at conferences, industry events and government forums
