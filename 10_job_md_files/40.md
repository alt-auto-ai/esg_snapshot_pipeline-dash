## Exciting careers start here. 
Science & Engineering is about applying technical expertise to challenges that will shape the future. From climate and sustainability to infrastructure, energy, health and the environment, this work helps organisations respond to some of the most complex and pressing issues facing our world today. 
Starting your career in Science & Engineering at Deloitte means using your scientific or engineering knowledge beyond the lab or classroom. You’ll build experience at the intersection of science, policy and business, developing skills that stay relevant as priorities shift and new challenges emerge. 
## Why bring your Science & Engineering skills to Deloitte? 
Science & Engineering work is practical, interdisciplinary and impact-driven. You’ll work alongside engineers, scientists, sustainability specialists, consultants and clients, applying technical thinking to real-world decisions. 
The work spans climate strategy, decarbonisation, renewable energy, sustainable infrastructure, environmental management and health sciences. Projects often combine deep technical analysis with commercial and regulatory considerations, giving you exposure to how science and engineering inform strategy, investment and policy. 
**As a Graduate, your day could involve:**
  * Analysing scientific or engineering data to inform decision-making 
  * Supporting sustainability, climate or environmental initiatives 
  * Working with teams to design or assess technical solutions 
  * Helping clients understand regulatory, environmental or technical risks 


If you’re motivated by applying technical expertise to challenges that matter, a great role in Science & Engineering could be the perfect career launchpad – so hit apply, and see where your skills could take you! 
## Is Deloitte the right fit for you? 
People who thrive at Deloitte are often: 
  * Curious and keen to apply their technical knowledge in new ways 
  * Comfortable working across disciplines and learning from others 
  * Motivated by solving complex problems with real-world impact 


When you apply, you’ll be able to explore a range of Science & Engineering roles and select your top two preferences, giving you flexibility to start your career in the areas that align most with your interests. 
## What’s included in the Graduate Program? 
  * A permanent, full-time position from day one 
  * Structured training via our Graduate Learning Academy 
  * A dedicated buddy, coach, and mentor 
  * Real client work and career-building projects 
  * Flexible work options and wellbeing support 
  * Access to global career pathways
