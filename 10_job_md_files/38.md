## **Environmental & Sustainability Coordinator | 2027**
Are you a passionate educator committed to shaping the next generation of ecological stewards? **Freshwater Christian College** is seeking a dynamic and visionary **Environmental & Sustainability Coordinator** to lead the pedagogical and curricular integration of sustainability across our Secondary School (Years 7–12).
This is a leadership role of added responsibility, held in conjunction with a classroom teaching position. 
  
**The Role**
As the Environmental & Sustainability Coordinator, you will report directly to the Head of Secondary and work closely with the curriculum and pedagogy team. 
**Key Responsibilities Include:**
  * **Instructional Leadership:** Audit and refine the 7–12 scope and sequence to ensure sustainability is authentically embedded in all Learning Areas, from Mathematics to Humanities.
  * **Student Agency:** Mentor a high-profile student leadership group, empowering them to lead peer-to-peer campaigns and global citizenship initiatives.
  * **Innovative Pedagogy:** Model inquiry-based and service-learning models that challenge students to solve real-world environmental problems.
  * **Theological Alignment:** Weave environmental education together with the College’s Christian values, focusing on the biblical mandate to care for the Earth.


  

**Nature of Appointment**
  * Commencing 15 January 2027, this position is full-time. 
  * This role includes an active classroom teaching component.
  * A dedicated non-contact time allowance and a financial leadership loading are provided in recognition of the added responsibilities of this position.
  * Relocation allowance may also be available.


  

**About You**
We are looking for a collaborative leader who is deeply aligned with the Christian values and educational philosophy of Freshwater Christian College.
**Essential Criteria:**
  * Current **Queensland College of Teachers (QCT)** registration, or the ability to obtain. 
  * Full alignment with the Christian values, virtues, and Statement of Faith of Freshwater Christian College.
  * Demonstrated expertise in the **Australian Curriculum** and successful experience in collaborative secondary environments.
  * Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to communicate effectively with diverse stakeholders.


**Highly Regarded:**
  * Postgraduate studies in Education.
  * Proven success in implementing **Project-Based Learning (PBL)** in a secondary setting.


  

**Why Join Freshwater?**
**Community:** Work in a "village feel" environment where relationships are prioritised, and staff are genuinely supported.
**Purpose:** Know that your expertise directly supports a safe and empowered learning environment. 
**Environment:** Enjoy our unique, lush tropical campus in Cairns.

## **Environmental & Sustainability Coordinator | 2027**
Are you a passionate educator committed to shaping the next generation of ecological stewards? **Freshwater Christian College** is seeking a dynamic and visionary **Environmental & Sustainability Coordinator** to lead the pedagogical and curricular integration of sustainability across our Secondary School (Years 7–12).
This is a leadership role of added responsibility, held in conjunction with a classroom teaching position. 
  
**The Role**
As the Environmental & Sustainability Coordinator, you will report directly to the Head of Secondary and work closely with the curriculum and pedagogy team. 
**Key Responsibilities Include:**
  * **Instructional Leadership:** Audit and refine the 7–12 scope and sequence to ensure sustainability is authentically embedded in all Learning Areas, from Mathematics to Humanities.
  * **Student Agency:** Mentor a high-profile student leadership group, empowering them to lead peer-to-peer campaigns and global citizenship initiatives.
  * **Innovative Pedagogy:** Model inquiry-based and service-learning models that challenge students to solve real-world environmental problems.
  * **Theological Alignment:** Weave environmental education together with the College’s Christian values, focusing on the biblical mandate to care for the Earth.


  

**Nature of Appointment**
  * Commencing 15 January 2027, this position is full-time. 
  * This role includes an active classroom teaching component.
  * A dedicated non-contact time allowance and a financial leadership loading are provided in recognition of the added responsibilities of this position.
  * Relocation allowance may also be available.


  

**About You**
We are looking for a collaborative leader who is deeply aligned with the Christian values and educational philosophy of Freshwater Christian College.
**Essential Criteria:**
  * Current **Queensland College of Teachers (QCT)** registration, or the ability to obtain. 
  * Full alignment with the Christian values, virtues, and Statement of Faith of Freshwater Christian College.
  * Demonstrated expertise in the **Australian Curriculum** and successful experience in collaborative secondary environments.
  * Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to communicate effectively with diverse stakeholders.


**Highly Regarded:**
  * Postgraduate studies in Education.
  * Proven success in implementing **Project-Based Learning (PBL)** in a secondary setting.


  

**Why Join Freshwater?**
**Community:** Work in a "village feel" environment where relationships are prioritised, and staff are genuinely supported.
**Purpose:** Know that your expertise directly supports a safe and empowered learning environment. 
**Environment:** Enjoy our unique, lush tropical campus in Cairns.

This is a leadership role of added responsibility, held in conjunction with a classroom teaching position. 
  
**The Role**
As the Environmental & Sustainability Coordinator, you will report directly to the Head of Secondary and work closely with the curriculum and pedagogy team. 
**Key Responsibilities Include:**
  * **Instructional Leadership:** Audit and refine the 7–12 scope and sequence to ensure sustainability is authentically embedded in all Learning Areas, from Mathematics to Humanities.
  * **Student Agency:** Mentor a high-profile student leadership group, empowering them to lead peer-to-peer campaigns and global citizenship initiatives.
  * **Innovative Pedagogy:** Model inquiry-based and service-learning models that challenge students to solve real-world environmental problems.
  * **Theological Alignment:** Weave environmental education together with the College’s Christian values, focusing on the biblical mandate to care for the Earth.


  

**Nature of Appointment**
  * Commencing 15 January 2027, this position is full-time. 
  * This role includes an active classroom teaching component.
  * A dedicated non-contact time allowance and a financial leadership loading are provided in recognition of the added responsibilities of this position.
  * Relocation allowance may also be available.


  

**About You**
We are looking for a collaborative leader who is deeply aligned with the Christian values and educational philosophy of Freshwater Christian College.
**Essential Criteria:**
  * Current **Queensland College of Teachers (QCT)** registration, or the ability to obtain. 
  * Full alignment with the Christian values, virtues, and Statement of Faith of Freshwater Christian College.
  * Demonstrated expertise in the **Australian Curriculum** and successful experience in collaborative secondary environments.
  * Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to communicate effectively with diverse stakeholders.


**Highly Regarded:**
  * Postgraduate studies in Education.
  * Proven success in implementing **Project-Based Learning (PBL)** in a secondary setting.


  

**Why Join Freshwater?**
**Community:** Work in a "village feel" environment where relationships are prioritised, and staff are genuinely supported.
**Purpose:** Know that your expertise directly supports a safe and empowered learning environment. 
**Environment:** Enjoy our unique, lush tropical campus in Cairns.
