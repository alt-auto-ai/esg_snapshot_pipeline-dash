Australian Competition and Consumer Commission
### Director, Applications Management
Adelaide SA, Brisbane QLD, Canberra ACT, Darwin NT, Hobart TAS, Melbourne VIC, Perth WA, Sydney NSW, Townsville QLD
  * Do you want to work at an agency where each day reveals new opportunities to work on some of the biggest issues facing consumers and businesses and have opportunities to pursue new and exciting pathways?
  * Do you want to work at a place where smart and dedicated people from all walks of life collaborate to work on meaningful matters?
  * Do you want lead, inspire, and manage a diverse team while ensuring high standards in software application development, testing, and project and service delivery?


  

Then this is the place for you! At the ACCC, we’re proud of the impact we can make together. We understand the responsibilities and challenges that come with a fast moving and ever-changing environment. Our high performing culture is built on a foundation of care, support and inclusion. It’s why we’re driven by connection and collaboration, sharing our skills, knowledge and support with each other freely and frequently. It’s why we prioritise the wellbeing of our people and empower them to work autonomously, flexibly and courageously. At the ACCC, we believe the best version of you is the one that’s true to who you are – and that’s the you we want working alongside us.
  

We value flexibility, and all roles can be performed from any capital city in a state or territory or Townsville and are available on a full-time, part-time or job-share basis.
  

Applicants from diverse backgrounds including First Nations peoples, people with disability and those from different cultural backgrounds are encouraged to apply.
#### The key duties of the position include:
  * Manage and mentor staff, fostering high performance, maintaining the team momentum and identifying and removing blockers that inhibit progress.
  * Oversee the development and delivery of technology solutions. Ensure they are user friendly, meet business objectives and align with approved enterprise architecture, design principles, and application roadmaps.
  * Assist in the planning, development, and execution of ICT projects, ensuring they are aligned with budgetary constraints and strategic goals of the ACCC.
  * Engage with internal and external stakeholders and service providers, managing expectations, and aligning processes.
  * Oversee work with other ACCC business areas to clarify requirements and deliver agreed outcomes on time and on budget.


#### Eligibility
About you
  

In order to succeed in the role, you will have:
  

  * Demonstrated experience leading a team or similar role in a fast-paced and large complex people-centred organisation.
  * Demonstrated experience in managing large, diverse, multidisciplinary development teams and driving the technical direction across a portfolio of applications/support services, supporting a range of projects from initiation, inflight and transition to operations, and end state operations.
  * Strong technical expertise in software development, cloud-based platforms, databases, and web technologies with and an understanding of how to drive modern software development practices.
  * Demonstrated ability to drive a software development strategy and roadmap with proven success at delivering complex software development initiatives to meet strategic objectives.
  * Experience in establishing practices within teams to follow Agile and DevOps methodologies using tools like Azure DevOps, Jira or similar.
  * An ability to think strategically, innovate and drive change to improve software development processes to deliver high-quality solutions. 


  

To find out more about us and the role, please read the Candidate Kit.

Under the RecruitAbility scheme you will be invited to participate in further assessment activity for the vacancy if you choose to apply under the scheme; declare you have a disability; and meet the minimum requirements for the job. #### Getting to know the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission
The ACCC is an independent statutory authority that administers the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 and other Acts. The ACCC works to promote effective competition and fair trading in the market place to benefit consumers, business and the community, and efficiency in the delivery of certain infrastructure services. The AER is a part of the ACCC. The AER is Australia's national energy market regulator and has an independent board. The AER shares employees, resources and facilities with the ACCC.
