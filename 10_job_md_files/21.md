## **The Role**
As the Commercial Manager for the Environmental portfolio, you will be responsible for defining and executing portfolio and commercial growth strategies across key segments including water, wastewater, mining, and environmental consulting.
This is a senior leadership position where you will manage a multidisciplinary team across sales and product management. You will be the architect of the portfolio's revenue growth, ensuring that internal and external teams from marketing to technical support collaborate seamlessly to deliver differentiated customer value.
## **Key Responsibilities**
  * **Strategic Leadership:** Define and execute portfolio strategies to drive market share gain in priority sectors and achieve key financial targets.
  * **Team Management:** Lead, motivate, and develop a high-performing team of sales and product management professionals.
  * **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Foster a culture of collaboration across marketing, technical service, and support teams to enhance the customer value proposition.
  * **Change Management:** Act as an engaging and resilient change leader, navigating ambiguity and managing conflict constructively with confidence and composure.
  * **Performance Driving:** Oversee total revenue growth for the portfolio while contributing to the broader strategic success of the global organisation.
