## **About the Organisation**
Our client is a faith-based organisation providing integrated community and aged care services. As the organisation enters a new phase of strategic development, alongside the implementation of the new Aged Care Act and associated reforms, they are seeking an inspiring Chief Executive Officer to advance their mission.
## **The Opportunity**
The CEO provides strategic and operational leadership across all service streams operated in accordance with Gospel values. Reporting to the Board, the CEO will ensure the organisation continues to deliver excellence in care, governance, compliance and financial stewardship.
This role requires a contemporary leader who can confidently lead under the regulatory environments, ensuring:
  * Clear accountability as a Governing Person
  * Strong clinical governance and quality frameworks
  * A culture of safety, transparency and residents' and clients' rights
  * Effective risk management and regulatory compliance
  * Proactive engagement with the stakeholders


The CEO will foster an organisational culture grounded in faith, compassion, empowerment and psychological safety.
**Key Responsibilities**
  * Create governance frameworks that ensure compliance with legislation and regulatory standards.
  * Ensure high-quality, person-centred service delivery across aged care, community care and family and youth services in accordance with relevant Quality Standards.
  * Foster a culture of ethical leadership, pastoral care, safeguarding, continuous improvement and accountability.
  * Lead and implement the organisation’s strategic vision in alignment with Catholic identity and our values.
  * Maintain robust clinical governance, risk management and quality improvement systems.
  * Provide strong financial oversight and ensure long-term sustainability through responsible stewardship of resources.
  * Provide transparent governance, risk and compliance reporting to the Board.
  * Strengthen partnerships with faith and community leaders, regulators, government agencies and community stakeholders.
  * Work closely with the executive team to ensure the successful implementation of the strategy, individual and collective goals, and KPIs.

You are an experienced executive leader aligned with Christian values and committed to compassionate, rights-based service delivery.
You will bring:
  * Ability to lead cultural transformation and build a mission-driven, safety-focused workforce.
  * Demonstrated commitment to, and understanding of Christian faith and values,
  * Demonstrated understanding of the new Aged Care Act, Quality Standards and strengthened regulatory requirements.
  * Exceptional stakeholder engagement and communication skills.
  * Proven financial and commercial acumen across complex funding models, including government-funded programs.
  * Relevant tertiary qualifications in business, health management, governance or a related field.
  * Significant senior leadership experience in aged care or complex regulated human services environments.
  * Strong governance, risk and compliance expertise within regulated environments.
