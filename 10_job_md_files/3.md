### Base Support Officers and Estate Management & Planning Officers
Bandiana VIC, Fishermans Bend VIC, Melbourne VIC, Puckapunyal VIC, Wagga Wagga NSW
#### The key duties of the position include:
  

**The Role**
We are seeking dynamic, solutions focused individuals to join our high performing teams as Base Support Officers and Estate Management & Planning Officers, roles central to keeping Defence sites safe, secure, and effective. As a Base Support Officer, you will excel in stakeholder engagement, anticipate and coordinate base services, and support facilities maintenance, asset improvement, and operational integration. You will build strong relationships across military, civilian, and contractor groups, contributing to infrastructure management, safety, security, and emergency response while supporting daily operations and planning programs.
  

As an Estate Management and Planning Officer, you will play a key role in ensuring the Defence estate is planned, managed, and maintained to meet safety, compliance, and sustainability requirements. You will support infrastructure projects, advise on risks and future needs, contribute to planning frameworks, and work with contractors and planners to deliver strategic estate outcomes in a collaborative environment.
  

**About our Team**
Defence’s primary mission is to protect Australia and its national interests, promote security and stability in the world and support the Australian community as directed by the Government. 
  

The South East Zone is one of three zones within the Service Delivery Division that provide essential, on-the-ground services and support to Defence personnel around Australia. We enable Defence personnel to achieve their mission through delivery of excellent services on Defence Bases and we play a role in helping the Government achieve its strategic objectives.
  

One of the best things about our team is the variety of our work; every day is different, with new challenges to solve and interesting work crossing our desks. To achieve the level of service delivery needed, we work collaboratively with our Defence colleagues and industry partners. Our team works with representatives from industry to foster creativity and explore ideas that improve service delivery for Defence bases.
  

**Our Ideal Candidate**
We are seeking adaptable, solutions focused individuals who remain calm under pressure and demonstrate a strong customer service mindset. A quick thinker, capable of working collaboratively across a range of military, civilian, and contractor stakeholders, while also taking ownership of your responsibilities. The ideal candidate is proactive in managing risks and safety, comfortable in an operational environment, and ready to respond effectively to incidents or emerging issues.
  

Successful applicants will have a proven ability to achieve organisational goals while contributing to a high performance service delivery culture. You will bring well developed communication, organisation, and negotiation skills, along with the ability to influence stakeholders and adapt positively to change. Strong prioritisation skills and the ability to interpret policy into practical outcomes are essential. Most importantly, we are seeking someone with a positive attitude, strong team focus, and the ability to navigate the stresses of a busy role with good humour.

#### Getting to know the Department of Defence
The Department of Defence employs around 18,000 Australian Public Service (APS) employees. Defence APS employees work in a wide variety of critical roles, Australia wide, enabling our three services Navy, Army and Air Force, to do their job. Defence has a range of Australian Public Service (APS) career opportunities that offer interesting, challenging, and unique work. Our workforce includes administrative support, accountants, intelligence analysts, engineers, policy advisors, graphic designers, lawyers, social workers, IT specialists and contract managers to name a few. We offer a rewarding career that includes professional development, work-life balance, recognition, a flexible and supportive work environment and a diverse workforce.
