### Procurement Business Partner
Melbourne VIC
#### The key duties of the position include:
**_About Future Fund_**
At the Future Fund, we’re for future minds – like yours. The new investors and creators who thrive on different thinking and doing it together. Those who glimpse opportunity before it strikes and step up every day to grab it.
The Future Fund is Australia's Sovereign Wealth Fund, managing over $340 billion across seven public asset funds with the same purpose: growing Australia’s future. We are a global investor and work closely with world leading investment organisations to access the best investment ideas.
Where others see challenges, we see more interesting investment experiences. Every person, every skillset, every mindset is an asset. We’ve got every future generation to consider, yet our approach to investing is incredibly pure: we think about it deeply, and daily. Ours is a culture where great people grow the future together, with the rare ability to work collaboratively, think freely, and make a real difference.
Are you Futureminded? Let’s create what’s next.
  

**_The Opportunity_**
  

We are seeking an experienced Procurement Business Partner to lead and deliver high‑quality procurement services across the Agency’s Corporate business units, including People, Culture & Inclusion; Legal; Enterprise Risk; and Corporate Affairs.
  

This is a senior individual‑contributor role for a high‑performing procurement professional who enjoys working closely with the business to solve complex, multi‑dimensional challenges. You will act as a trusted advisor, co‑designing procurement solutions that align commercial outcomes, risk management and compliance obligations with broader Agency objectives.
  

The role plays a critical part in enabling the Agency to focus on its core purpose by delivering value‑driven procurement outcomes, strengthening governance, enhancing service experience and supporting priorities such as sustainability and social responsibility.
  

**_What You’ll Do_**
  

In this role, you will:
  * Partner closely with senior stakeholders across Corporate functions, acting as the primary point of contact between Procurement and the business
  * Lead and support end‑to‑end procurement activities, including sourcing, tendering, negotiation and contract management, in line with Commonwealth Procurement Rules and Agency policies
  * Develop and implement sourcing and commercial strategies informed by spend analysis, business priorities and risk considerations
  * Lead contractual negotiations and provide commercial advice on complex professional services arrangements
  * Manage supplier and contract relationships end‑to‑end, driving performance, managing risk and resolving issues effectively
  * Strengthen procurement governance, compliance and assurance, supporting audit and risk management activities
  * Identify opportunities to streamline and improve procurement and contracting processes while ensuring value for money
  * Define and monitor supplier performance measures and contribute to effective supplier relationship management
  * Collaborate across Procurement, Legal, Finance and Risk to deliver consistent, high‑quality outcomes
  * Promote ethical, sustainable and socially responsible procurement practices, including support for SME and Indigenous supplier participation


#### Eligibility
**_About You_**
  

You are a highly capable, commercially astute procurement professional who brings strong judgement, credibility and a collaborative mindset.
  

You bring:
  * Extensive experience in procurement, contract management and supplier management for complex and high‑value arrangements
  * Proven experience leading strategic sourcing and negotiations, particularly across professional services categories
  * Strong stakeholder partnering skills, with the ability to influence and advise at executive and senior management levels
  * Demonstrated experience managing risk, compliance and governance within procurement activities
  * An analytical and improvement‑focused mindset, using data and insights to uplift procurement capability
  * Experience improving procurement processes, embedding best practice and strengthening transparency
  * The ability to manage multiple priorities simultaneously in a complex, fast‑moving environment
  * A calm, professional and solutions‑focused approach, even under pressure
  * Relevant qualifications (for example CIPS or WorldCC) and experience in a government, financial or investment environment are highly regarded.


Please note that the opportunity is open to Australian citizens only.

#### Getting to know the Future Fund Management Agency
The Future Fund is Australia’s sovereign wealth fund, investing for the benefit of future generations of Australians. The Future Fund Board of Guardians, supported by the Future Fund Management Agency, invests the assets of seven public asset funds: the Future Fund, the Medical Research Future Fund, the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Land and Sea Future Fund, the Future Drought Fund, the Emergency Response Fund and the DisabilityCare Australia Fund. Each fund has an investment mandate that is determined by the Australian Government under legislation. Our role is to generate strong, risk-adjusted returns over the long term.
