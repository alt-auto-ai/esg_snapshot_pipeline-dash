### FAS Industry Engagement Executive Officer and Business Manager (Multiple Roles)
Brindabella Business Park ACT
#### The key duties of the position include:
  

  

**The Role**
Industry Engagement Division is seeking to recruit to the following two positions within their office of the First Assistant Secretary (FAS).
  * Executive Officer 
  * Business Manager


Industry Engagement Division is a fast paced and high profile Division. These are key roles in the running of the Division and require direct engagement with senior leaders. 
  

As an EL 1 Executive Officer your key duties may include:
  

  * Provide high level executive support to the First Assistant Secretary, including oversight of divisional activities and the provision of timely advice to the executive. 
  * Coordinate divisional reporting requirements, and monitor the quality, accuracy, and timeliness of briefing papers and communications materials. 
  * Liaise directly with divisional staff to ensure effective information flow, task coordination, and follow up of priorities. 
  * Streamline the coordination of strategic priorities for the First Assistant Secretary, ensuring alignment across the Industry Engagement Division. 
  * Maintain effective communication with branch heads, line areas, and a range of internal and external stakeholders to support divisional objectives.


As an EL 1 Business Manager your key duties may include:
  

  * Provide critical support to business units across Industry Engagement Division, ensuring effective business operations and process compliance within administrative policy and legislation.
  * Provide comprehensive administrative and operational support, including workforce resource management, records compliance, security, and work health and safety responsibilities. 
  * Compile, analyse, and prepare reports and returns to support business operations and decision making.

**About our Team**
The Industry Engagement Division supports Defence’s vision by enabling access to a strong and secure domestic industrial base with industrial capability, competitiveness, innovativeness and sustainability aligned to Defence priority requirements. The Industry Engagement Division is comprised of 3 branches, with one branch located in the United Sates. 
  

The division is responsible for:
  

  * Implementation of Defence Industry Development Strategy (DIDS)
  * Developing AIC industry analysis systems and tools
  * Supporting the implementation of Australian Industry Capability (AIC) contractual framework
  * Global Supply Chain Program
  * Advice, guidance and mentoring services to small to medium enterprise’s (SME’s)
  * The development and coordination of Defence Industry workforce and skills policy
  * Exposure for Australian defence companies at international trade events
  * Industrial Intelligence Capability Program
  * Maximising Australian Industry Capability opportunities


  

**Our Ideal Candidate**
Our ideal candidate is a capable, adaptable administration professional with a strong understanding of APS governance and corporate service functions, and a commitment to delivering high quality outcomes in a complex, fast paced environment. You will have demonstrated experience providing high level administrative, executive, or business operations support, with the ability to manage competing priorities, exercise sound judgement, and work with initiative and professionalism.
  

You will be confident engaging with senior executives and stakeholders, providing clear, timely, and well considered advice to support effective decision making. Strong organisational and coordination skills will enable you to oversee workflows, coordinate reporting, and ensure briefing materials and correspondence meet required standards of quality and accuracy.
  

You will contribute to effective corporate governance, including financial administration, records management, work health and safety, security, and compliance with APS frameworks. For Administration Manager role, demonstrated leadership capability, people management experience, and a commitment to continuous improvement are essential.
