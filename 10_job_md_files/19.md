$147,769 - $156,144 per annum
Posted 1d ago
[Apply⁠](https://www.seek.com.au/job/90853966/apply)
Save
As Manager, you will bring to the role:
* The ability to provide expert and authoritative advice on policy issues, for internal and external stakeholders and senior management.
* Proven leadership and management skills including leading and prioritising work planning.
* Substantial experience in researching and evaluating options to support policy development and decision making.
* High-level communication and presentation skills including proven experience in engaging with stakeholders and representing the government's interest in a range of forums.
* High-level written communication skills including proven experience in preparing complex briefing material, submissions for consideration at a senior executive level and for Cabinet, Parliamentary and Ministerial processes.
* Knowledge of, or ability to rapidly acquire knowledge of, climate and industry-related policy, programs, laws, agreements and technical matters at all levels.
**Applications to remain current for 12 months.**
**Job Ad Reference: QLD/680510/26**
**Closing Date: Friday, 27 March 2026**

#### Department of Treasury QLD
1.5·[4 reviews](https://www.seek.com.au/companies/department-of-treasury-qld-795763/reviews?jobId=90853966)
Queensland Treasury is dedicated to delivering prosperity for Queenslanders now and into the future. We pursue this through our strategic priorities to:
•support economic growth, job creation and fiscal strength
•improve services for Queenslanders
•empower our people
•ensure safe, productive and fair workplaces.
Our remit encompasses a range of critical functions including:
•managing Queensland's finances
•administering grants and collecting taxes
•providing economic and commercial advice to government
•procuring infrastructure
•keeping workplaces safe and fair through the Office of Industrial Relations.
Key responsibilities
Preparing the state budget
Providing financial and economic advice
Optimising public infrastructure infrastructure investment
Collecting state revenue
Administering grants for first home buyers
Monitoring the performance of the state’s commercial businesses
Keeping Queensland’s workplaces healthy, safe and fair

•improve services for Queenslanders
