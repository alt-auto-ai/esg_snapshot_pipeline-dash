# Job Title: SAP Senior Basis Administrator
**Acknowledgement of Country**
CSIRO acknowledges the Traditional Owners of the land, sea and waters, of the area that we live and work on across Australia. We acknowledge their continuing connection to their culture and pay our respects to their Elders past and present. View our vision towards reconciliation. 
**Role highlights**
  * **Shape & strengthen CSIRO’s SAP landscape, keeping critical systems reliable, secure & high performing**
  * **Be the go to SAP expert, partnering across CSIRO stakeholders to deliver integrated solutions**
  * **Enable smoother, best practice systems that support science at scale**


**About CSIRO**
As Australia's national science agency, CSIRO is solving the greatest challenges through innovative science and technology. Many of our iconic innovations were once considered impossible until someone, just like you, joined us and took on the challenge.

**The opportunity**
As the Senior SAP Basis Administrator with CSIRO, you’ll play a critical role supporting world‑class scientific research by ensuring the stability, performance and future‑readiness of our SAP environments.
Working within the IM&T SAP Solutions Team, you’ll lead the installation, upgrade and maintenance of SAP systems, databases and operating systems, while configuring, monitoring and optimising complex technical landscapes. You’ll collaborate closely with Finance, People and IM&T stakeholders to design and deliver integrated system solutions aligned to CSIRO’s roadmap, streamline the user experience across the organisation, and harness data‑driven insights to support leading practices and processes.
This is an opportunity to apply deep technical expertise in a purpose‑driven organisation making a real impact through science and innovation.
#### The key duties of the position include:
**High-level duties include:**
  * System Landscape Architecture Configuration and Operations including Disaster Recovery Architecture, Design, Validation and Testing 
  * Monitor / maintain / update integration/interfaces with internal and cloud applications
  * SAP Application Security, Security Patch review and management
  * OS and Database level Security, Patch Monitoring, Backup operations and Management
  * Advise on System Architecture / Future state 
  * Team Lead / Mentor activities for the Basis Team


**Role particulars**
**Location and office arrangements:** Canberra (Preferred), Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane
**Salary:** AU$135,571 – AU$158,863 per annum (pro rata for part-time), plus 15.4% superannuation
**Tenure & work schedule:** Indefinite
**Reference:** 103092
#### Eligibility
**As the successful candidate, you will bring:**
**Essential criteria**
  1. Minimum 10 years experience working as an SAP Basis Administrator with demonstrated experience of system architecture, configuration and operations.
  2. Demonstrated experience in stopping/starting of SAP instances/Databases and Operating Systems in Linux environments.
  3. Demonstrated experience in management of Transport Management System including scheduling and executing transport requests in a multi-tier environment.
  4. Demonstrated experience of backup administration and disaster recovery including Database and Operating Systems
  5. Demonstrated experience of system integration administration including design, monitoring, maintenance and updating integrations with internal and cloud applications.
  6. Demonstrated experience of system administration including SAP Kernel upgrades, SAP OSS note implementation, applying SAP Add-ons/Support packs, OS monitoring/patching/upgrades/space/disk management, HANA DB monitoring/patching and upgrades.
  7. Demonstrated experience in SAP batch job creation/configuration/maintenance and management.
  8. Demonstrated experience and knowledge in SAP and Cyber Security including patching reviews and management.
  9. Demonstrated experience performing SAP system copies and data refreshes in a multi-tiered environment.
  10. Demonstrated experience in contributing to the design of future state architecture for on-premise and cloud solutions including S/4HANA, SuccessFactors and Business Technology Platform.
  11. Demonstrated experience in team leadership and mentoring activities.


**Desirable**
  1. Experience with non-SAP middleware including Axway.
  2. Experience with DB variants including MaxDB and PostgreSQL.


For full details about this role, and other criteria, please review the Position Description.
