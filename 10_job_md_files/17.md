**About Grace Group**
We are one of Australia’s premier services organisations with a strong brand and a long history of success.
Our investment in training and development, adoption of advanced technologies and use of innovative processes, make us an industry leader. 
**The power of our brand makes things happen but our people drive our exceptional results!**
**About the Role**
As a critical member of our senior leadership team, reporting to the business Director, this challenging and exciting role will provide you with the autonomy to make your mark on our West Australian business.
Providing general leadership across the site-based sales, customer service and operations teams - your focus will be on growing the business, boosting employee engagement and achieving strong results through service quality and exceptional client relationships.
**About You**
You may be a current Business Manager looking to step into your first senior leadership role and grow with our business, or a seasoned General Manager looking for a change of scenery and a new challenge.
Most importantly, you will enjoy working in a fast-paced and collaborative environment where every day brings new challenges and opportunities to make an impact in our business. 
**Success Factors:**
  * 5+ years business management experience, track record of exceeding sales targets and attracting and retaining client accounts. 
  * Business acumen including polished influencing and negotiation skills.
  * Previous experience in budget management, including full P&L responsibility and business performance analytics. 
  * Advanced communication skills, able to engage with key stakeholders across all business functions including sales, administration and operations. 
  * Customer service orientation, problem solving skills and drive to go above and beyond to deliver client outcomes.
  * Demonstrated success working in a large organisation and partnering effectively with head office corporate support functions to achieve strategic goals.


If you are looking for a role that you can own and know that you can push the performance of our WA business to the next level - apply now to join our team!

**About Grace Group**
We are one of Australia’s premier services organisations with a strong brand and a long history of success.
Our investment in training and development, adoption of advanced technologies and use of innovative processes, make us an industry leader. 
**The power of our brand makes things happen but our people drive our exceptional results!**
**About the Role**
As a critical member of our senior leadership team, reporting to the business Director, this challenging and exciting role will provide you with the autonomy to make your mark on our West Australian business.
Providing general leadership across the site-based sales, customer service and operations teams - your focus will be on growing the business, boosting employee engagement and achieving strong results through service quality and exceptional client relationships.
**About You**
You may be a current Business Manager looking to step into your first senior leadership role and grow with our business, or a seasoned General Manager looking for a change of scenery and a new challenge.
Most importantly, you will enjoy working in a fast-paced and collaborative environment where every day brings new challenges and opportunities to make an impact in our business. 
**Success Factors:**
  * 5+ years business management experience, track record of exceeding sales targets and attracting and retaining client accounts. 
  * Business acumen including polished influencing and negotiation skills.
  * Previous experience in budget management, including full P&L responsibility and business performance analytics. 
  * Advanced communication skills, able to engage with key stakeholders across all business functions including sales, administration and operations. 
  * Customer service orientation, problem solving skills and drive to go above and beyond to deliver client outcomes.
  * Demonstrated success working in a large organisation and partnering effectively with head office corporate support functions to achieve strategic goals.


If you are looking for a role that you can own and know that you can push the performance of our WA business to the next level - apply now to join our team!
