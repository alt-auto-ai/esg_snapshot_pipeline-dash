Department of Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water (DCCEEW)
### Executive Assistant
Perth WA
The Head of Division Executive Office plays a pivotal role in enabling senior executive leaders in Environment Regulation Division to operate at their best. This position leads a high-performing executive support function, ensuring seamless coordination of priorities, communications, and key business activities across the division. Acting as a trusted advisor and central point of alignment, the role drives efficiency, strengthens governance and supports the delivery of strategic objectives in a fast-paced, high-impact environment.
  

For more information about us please visit our [website](https://www.apsjobs.gov.au/s/external-link?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.dcceew.gov.au%2F).
#### The key duties of the position include:
• Provide high-level administrative support to the Head of Division 
• Manage and coordinate complex calendars, appointments and meetings 
• Serve as a primary point of contact between the Head of Division and internal/external stakeholders 
• Coordinate communication across the branches of the division and across divisions to ensure alignment and follow-up 
• Handle sensitive information with discretion 
• Represent the executive professionally in meetings when required 
• Organise executive meetings, including agendas, minutes and action tracking 
• Coordinate divisions events, workshops and leadership meetings 
• Ensure timely follow-up on action items and deadlines 
• Arrange travel logistics, accommodation and itineraries 
• Oversee day-to-day administrative operation the Executive Office 
• Maintain filing systems, records and documentation 
• Assist in planning and executing divisional projects and initiatives 
• Support change management and process improvement initiatives 
• Build and maintain strong working relationships with key stakeholders 
• Coordinate with senior leaders, internal and external stakeholders 
• Facilitate collaboration across teams within the division 
• Provide guidance and support to executive assistants within the division 
• Promote a professional, efficient and positive office and team environment 
• Handle highly sensitive information with integrity and discretion 
• Maintain professionalism in all interactions and communications
  

**For detailed information about the job-specific capabilities for this role, please view the Job Description** which can be downloaded from our website - the **Apply Now** link will take you there.
#### Eligibility
**Citizenship -** to be eligible for employment with the Department of Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water you must be an Australian citizen.
  

**Security Clearance -** this position requires a Baseline Vetting security clearance. You will be required to obtain and maintain a clearance at this level. 
  

**Pre-employment checks -** your suitability for employment will be assessed through a pre-employment screening process. This process includes a requirement to undergo and satisfy a National Police Check, referee checks, character clearance and where required a pre-employment medical assessment, specified mandatory qualification(s) validation and a probation period. 
RecruitAbility applies to this vacancy. Under the RecruitAbility scheme you will be invited to participate in further assessment activity for the vacancy if you choose to apply under the scheme; declare you have a disability; and meet the minimum requirements for the job.
