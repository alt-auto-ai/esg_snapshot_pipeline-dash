### Freedom of Information Officer and Corporate Law Support Officer
Adelaide SA, Brisbane QLD, Canberra ACT, Melbourne VIC, Sydney NSW
  * **Do you want to work at an agency where each day reveals new opportunities to work on some of the biggest issues facing consumers and businesses and have opportunities to pursue new and exciting pathways?**
  * **Do you want to work at a place where smart and dedicated people from all walks of life collaborate to work on meaningful matters?**
  * **Do you want to work in a compact legal team delivering practical, lawful outcomes across corporate governance and FOI?**


  

**Then this is the place for you!** At the AER, we work to make energy consumers better off, now and in the future. We focus on ensuring a secure, reliable and affordable energy future for Australia as it transitions to net zero emissions. Our high performing culture is built on a foundation of care, support and inclusion. It’s why we’re driven by connection and collaboration, sharing our skills, knowledge and support with each other freely and frequently. It’s why we prioritise the wellbeing of our people and empower them to work autonomously, flexibly and courageously. We believe the best version of you is the one that’s true to who you are – and that’s the you we want working alongside us.
  

We value flexibility, this role can be performed at any of the AER offices located in Adelaide, Brisbane, Canberra, Melbourne, or Sydney and are available on a full-time, part-time or job-share basis.
  

**Applicants from diverse backgrounds including First Nations peoples, people with disability and those from different cultural backgrounds are encouraged to apply.**
#### The key duties of the position include:
Freedom of Information (FOI) Officer:
  * Coordinate and process FOI requests in accordance with the  _Freedom of Information Act 1982_ (FOI Act), with guidance from lawyers within the team.
  * Assist with assessing FOI requests, clarifying scope where required, and coordinating searches for relevant documents with AER line areas and the ACCC FOI team.
  * Support internal review and Information Commissioner (IC) review processes, including preparing draft correspondence and assisting with submissions.
  * Liaise professionally with applicants, internal stakeholders, the ACCC FOI team and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).
  * Maintain accurate FOI records, disclosure log entries and file management in accordance with legislative and governance requirements.


  

Corporate Law Support Officer:
  * Provide legal and administrative support, including conducting research on corporate governance, compliance, legal services procurement and Legal Services Directions 2025 reporting, and general corporate and regulatory law matters.
  * Assist with the coordination and delivery of FOI matters, including drafting correspondence and supporting decision-making processes where required.
  * Support the preparation of legal advice, briefs, reports and governance documentation, including researching relevant legislation, case law and policy materials, under supervision.
  * Liaise with internal stakeholders and external agencies to progress corporate governance or regulatory law matters.
  * Maintain accurate records and contribute to effective information and document management practices.
  * Provide administrative support for legal IT applications (e.g. Nuix).


#### Eligibility
In order to succeed in the Freedom of Information Officer role, you will have:
  * Sound understanding of the  _FOI Act 1982_ and the ability to apply statutory timeframes, decision-making requirements and exemptions with appropriate guidance.
  * Ability to coordinate and progress FOI requests accurately and on time, including managing correspondence, supporting document searches and contributing to FOI decisions.
  * Well-developed written communication skills, with the ability to draft clear and accurate FOI correspondence under supervision.
  * Ability to work collaboratively with internal stakeholders and external parties, including applicants and other government and private sector FOI teams.
  * Strong organisational skills and attention to detail, with the ability to manage competing priorities, exercise sound judgement and escalate issues appropriately.


  

In order to succeed in the Corporate Law Support Officer role, you will have:
  * Proven experience undertaking a variety of administrative tasks within a professional office environment.
  * Demonstrated knowledge of legal services procurement processes and the Legal Services Directions 2025, including relevant reporting requirements.
  * Demonstrated initiative and effective organisational skills, including the ability to manage time, prioritise tasks and meet deadlines.
  * Well developed verbal communication skills, with the ability to liaise effectively with staff at all levels in a professional manner.
  * Demonstrated ability to work effectively both independently and as part of a team.
  * Sound understanding of, or the ability to quickly develop knowledge of, government in-house legal or regulatory environments.
  * Experience in providing administrative support for legal IT applications, such as Nuix.


  

To find out more about us and the role, please read the Candidate Kit.

#### Getting to know the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission
The ACCC is an independent statutory authority that administers the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 and other Acts. The ACCC works to promote effective competition and fair trading in the market place to benefit consumers, business and the community, and efficiency in the delivery of certain infrastructure services. The AER is a part of the ACCC. The AER is Australia's national energy market regulator and has an independent board. The AER shares employees, resources and facilities with the ACCC.
