Move With Us is more than a fitness brand, it’s a purpose-led digital wellness business built to empower, connect and motivate women. With a strong subscription model, loyal community and ambitious growth plans, MWU is entering its next evolution and seeking a leader to drive performance, discipline and innovation across the business.  
  
**About the Role**  
Reporting to the Board, the General Manager is responsible for the overall performance, strategic direction and operational effectiveness of Move With Us. Leading a small team of experts, this is a hands-on role that requires someone equally comfortable operating at a strategic level and working in the weeds, driving execution, solving problems and ensuring plans are delivered in practice, not just on paper.  
  
This is a broad and highly accountable leadership role spanning:
  * Strategy and execution
  * Financial performance and reporting
  * Subscription and revenue growth
  * New business incubation
  * Team capability and culture
  * Operational excellence and governance


You will create and propose the strategic direction of MWU, ensuring alignment with the company’s mission and long-term objectives. You will establish a disciplined operating cadence, embed strong performance management frameworks and drive data-led decision making across all functions.  
  
A key focus will be improving business performance management to reach financial goals, strengthening team capability, and ensuring a high-performing and aligned culture across the organisation.  
  
You will also:
  * Own and refine business reporting across daily, weekly, monthly and quarterly rhythms
  * Drive EBITDA, contribution profit and subscription health metrics
  * Identify cost efficiencies without compromising customer experience
  * Translate early-stage ideas into structured incubation plans and MVP launches
  * Lead cross-functional collaboration from ideation to go-to-market
  * Represent MWU externally and champion its brand, values and vision


This role requires a hands on leader who can operate at both strategic and operational levels, steering the business in the most profitable and sustainable direction.  
  
**About You**  
You are an experienced General Manager or senior leader with a strong commercial lens and a background in digital, subscription or SaaS environments.  
  
You bring:
  * Proven experience leading multi-functional teams in a high-growth digital business
  * Strong financial acumen across budgeting, forecasting, EBITDA and ROI
  * Demonstrated capability designing and executing strategic business plans
  * Experience establishing structured KPIs and performance cadences
  * A track record of launching new initiatives or business streams
  * Strong people leadership capability, with experience uplifting team performance
  * Confidence navigating governance, compliance and risk management
  * A passion for health, fitness and community driven brands


You are comfortable making hard decisions, optimising team structure, and holding accountability across all levels of the business. You lead with clarity, authenticity and discipline, and are energised by building high performing teams aligned to a clear purpose.  
  
**Why Join Move With Us?**  
Move With Us is a purpose-led brand with a powerful community at its core. This is an opportunity to step into a pivotal leadership role and shape the next chapter of a growing digital wellness business.  
  
As General Manager, you will also enjoy:
  * Being an integral part of a brand on a mission to empower women globally through health and fitness
  * Spearhead the incubation and launch of new business concepts and revenue streams, shaping the future growth trajectory of Move With Us.
  * A creative, collaborative and high-performing team culture
  * Career development opportunities and ongoing professional growth
  * A competitive salary package commensurate with experience
  * Flexibility to work from home up to three days per week
  * A modern open-plan office in beautiful Burleigh Heads, Queensland
  * Free access to a fully equipped on-site gym
  * Generous discounts to Move With Us and sister brand Crop Shop Boutique
  * Access to a supportive Paid Parental Leave package


**How to Apply:**  
Applying is simple. Submit your resume along with a cover letter. We’re eager to hear from you and look forward to learning how you can contribute to the continued success of the business.
