Department of Defence
### Estate Strategic Planners
Adelaide SA, Brisbane QLD, Canberra ACT, Darwin NT, Hobart TAS, Melbourne VIC, Perth WA, Sydney NSW
#### The key duties of the position include:
**The Role**
Are you ready to play a key role in shaping one of the most complex and strategically important estates in Australia? We are seeking motivated and capable professionals to fill a range of strategic planning roles with responsibility for shaping strategic outcomes and delivering work that has an enduring impact on national capability and operations.
  

In these roles, you will contribute to the coordination and integration of facilities and infrastructure to enable Defence capability now and into the future. You will play a key role in base redevelopment planning, estate change processes and in land use decision making.
  

The work brings together strategic thinking and practical delivery. You will work closely with Defence stakeholders, local government, and other government agencies to integrate infrastructure planning, manage development proposals, and coordinate priorities across complex operational environments. These roles require sound judgement, systems thinking, and the ability to balance capability requirements with safety, sustainability, affordability, and community considerations.
  

If you are motivated by work that matters, enjoy solving complex problems, and want to contribute to outcomes that will shape Defence operations for decades to come, these roles offer a unique and rewarding opportunity.
  

**About our Team**
The Security and Estate Group (SEG) is responsible for developing and sustaining the Defence estate, and serves as the consolidated security and service delivery organisation for Defence. Within SEG, the Estate Planning team plays a crucial role in shaping the future of Defence infrastructure and land use.
  

Our Work Includes:
  * Developing policy for managing the Defence estate;
  * Leading engagement with local governments and external stakeholders;
  * Managing land use matters such as urban encroachment and assessing development applications;
  * Acting as the delegated departmental planning approval authority for infrastructure projects, facility upgrades, and land divestments;
  * Conducting strategic assessments for estate acquisitions, disposals, and leases;
  * Leading resilience and critical infrastructure initiatives across Defence; and
  * Collaborating with other SEG branches, Defence groups and services, and other government agencies to deliver integrated outcomes.


  

While the majority of our team is based in Canberra, we are a geographically dispersed workforce with staff located in Perth, Adelaide, Darwin, Brisbane, and regional New South Wales. This national footprint enables us to deliver regionally informed and strategically aligned estate planning outcomes.
  

**Our Ideal Candidate**
  * Proven ability to deliver strategic studies and planning outcomes.
  * Have experience in estate, infrastructure, planning, policy, engineering, project coordination or a related government or industry environment.
  * Strong stakeholder engagement skills, and an ability to communicate with influence across internal and external networks.
  * Ability to work across multiple stakeholders, including Defence groups and Services, government agencies, local authorities and industry partners
  * Experience in preparing clear, well-structured advice, briefs, and reports for senior leaders to support timely and informed decision making
  * Excellent time management skills and an ability to manage competing demands in a complex environment.
  * A quick learner with a proactive mindset and a desire to grow.
  * A positive and inclusive team player who can work effectively in a geographically dispersed and diverse organisation.


#### Eligibility
**Security Clearance**
Applicants must be able to obtain and maintain a security clearance at “Negative Vetting Level 1”.
RecruitAbility applies to this vacancy. Under the RecruitAbility scheme you will be invited to participate in further assessment activity for the vacancy if you choose to apply under the scheme; declare you have a disability; and meet the minimum requirements for the job.
