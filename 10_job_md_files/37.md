**Environmental Technician – Field Services**  
 _Full-Time | Vehicle Provided | Based in Perth_
Site Environmental & Remediation Services (SERS) is seeking a motivated Environmental Technician to join our Field Services team in Perth. This full-time role offers variety, autonomy, and the opportunity to work on meaningful environmental monitoring and asbestos projects across Western Australia.
Key Responsibilities
  * Conduct routine fieldwork for asbestos assessments and environmental monitoring
  * Maintain and calibrate dust, vibration, and noise monitoring equipment
  * Collect, manage, and report field data with attention to detail
  * Liaise with clients, subcontractors, and internal teams to ensure timely project delivery
  * Undertake equipment troubleshooting and problem-solving in the field


Requirements
  * Physically fit and comfortable working outdoors in variable conditions
  * Competent in Microsoft Office applications and digital field systems
  * Strong communication and organisational skills
  * Capable of working independently and contributing to a small, supportive team
  * Current driver's licence (vehicle provided for work use)
  * Australian work rights are required for this position


Why Join SERS
SERS is an industry leader providing specialist environmental and asbestos services across Australia. You'll join an established team that values safety, teamwork, and professionalism while supporting major infrastructure, construction, and remediation projects.
If you're ready for a field-based role where every day is different, apply now to join our growing Perth team.

**Environmental Technician – Field Services**  
 _Full-Time | Vehicle Provided | Based in Perth_
Site Environmental & Remediation Services (SERS) is seeking a motivated Environmental Technician to join our Field Services team in Perth. This full-time role offers variety, autonomy, and the opportunity to work on meaningful environmental monitoring and asbestos projects across Western Australia.
Key Responsibilities
  * Conduct routine fieldwork for asbestos assessments and environmental monitoring
  * Maintain and calibrate dust, vibration, and noise monitoring equipment
  * Collect, manage, and report field data with attention to detail
  * Liaise with clients, subcontractors, and internal teams to ensure timely project delivery
  * Undertake equipment troubleshooting and problem-solving in the field


Requirements
  * Physically fit and comfortable working outdoors in variable conditions
  * Competent in Microsoft Office applications and digital field systems
  * Strong communication and organisational skills
  * Capable of working independently and contributing to a small, supportive team
  * Current driver's licence (vehicle provided for work use)
  * Australian work rights are required for this position


Why Join SERS
SERS is an industry leader providing specialist environmental and asbestos services across Australia. You'll join an established team that values safety, teamwork, and professionalism while supporting major infrastructure, construction, and remediation projects.
If you're ready for a field-based role where every day is different, apply now to join our growing Perth team.

 _Full-Time | Vehicle Provided | Based in Perth_
Site Environmental & Remediation Services (SERS) is seeking a motivated Environmental Technician to join our Field Services team in Perth. This full-time role offers variety, autonomy, and the opportunity to work on meaningful environmental monitoring and asbestos projects across Western Australia.
Key Responsibilities
  * Conduct routine fieldwork for asbestos assessments and environmental monitoring
  * Maintain and calibrate dust, vibration, and noise monitoring equipment
  * Collect, manage, and report field data with attention to detail
  * Liaise with clients, subcontractors, and internal teams to ensure timely project delivery
  * Undertake equipment troubleshooting and problem-solving in the field


Requirements
  * Physically fit and comfortable working outdoors in variable conditions
  * Competent in Microsoft Office applications and digital field systems

  * Capable of working independently and contributing to a small, supportive team
  * Current driver's licence (vehicle provided for work use)
  * Australian work rights are required for this position
