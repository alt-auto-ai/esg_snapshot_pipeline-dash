**About ACER**
The Australian Council for Educational Research (ACER) is a national, independent, not for profit organisation with a global footprint spanning Australia, the United Kingdom, India, Malaysia and the United Arab Emirates. With more than 440 staff and a diverse portfolio of assessment, research, professional learning and digital product development, ACER continues to evolve to improve learning across the world.
ACER is entering a significant transformation phase involving structural, financial, technological and cultural change as we position the organisation for the decade ahead. We are now seeking an experienced Chief Operations Officer to help shape and lead this next chapter.
**The Opportunity**
Reporting to the Chief Executive Officer, the Chief Operations Officer plays a critical executive leadership role overseeing ACER’s enterprise operations across Finance, Information Technology, Facilities and our global offices. The role ensures ACER has the governance, systems and operational capability required to execute strategy effectively.
The Chief Operations Officer will be a trusted sponsor for major digital, technology, data and operational initiatives, and will work closely with the Chief People and Transformation Officer to modernise ACER’s operating model and strengthen enterprise-wide capability, performance and coherence.
This is a unique opportunity to influence ACER’s long term financial resilience, digital maturity and operational excellence across a complex global environment.
**Key Responsibilities**
  * Provide strategic operational leadership across ACER’s enterprise functions
  * Lead organisational planning, operational performance and governance frameworks
  * Oversee Finance, Information Technology, Facilities, risk, governance and international operations
  * Translate strategy into sustainable financial plans and high-quality business execution
  * Lead major digital, technology, data and operational transformation initiatives
  * Strengthen integration and collaboration across ACER’s offices in the United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates, Malaysia and India
  * Drive process improvement, workflow redesign and organisational scalability
  * Lead financial and commercial analysis for major investment decisions
  * Partner with the Executive Team on strategy and enterprise performance
  * Lead long term facilities and property planning, including capital projects
  * Support workplace strategy to enable current and future ways of working
  * Foster a culture of accountability, collaboration, inclusion and psychological safety


**About You**
  * You are an experienced senior operations executive with expertise in leading transformation in complex environments. You will bring:
  * Extensive executive leadership experience across multi-disciplinary operations
  * Strong governance, strategic planning and organisational performance capability
  * Experience leading financial strategy, commercial analysis and investment evaluation
  * Experience modernising systems and uplifting digital and data capability
  * A successful record of leading large corporate services or shared services functions
  * Excellent communication and stakeholder engagement skills, including at Board and Executive levels
  * Experience working across global or multi-site environments is highly regarded


**Why Join ACER?**
  * Work with a respected international not-for-profit committed to improving learning
  * Lead significant operational transformation with global impact
  * Collaborative, purpose driven and supportive culture
  * Hybrid work arrangements with ability to work from any of ACER’s Australian offices


**Ready to make an impact?**
Apply now and help shape the operational future of ACER. To view the position description and to apply visit: https://careers.acer.org/job-invite/1179/
Applications close: **midnight AEDT, 22 March 2026**

**About ACER**
The Australian Council for Educational Research (ACER) is a national, independent, not for profit organisation with a global footprint spanning Australia, the United Kingdom, India, Malaysia and the United Arab Emirates. With more than 440 staff and a diverse portfolio of assessment, research, professional learning and digital product development, ACER continues to evolve to improve learning across the world.
ACER is entering a significant transformation phase involving structural, financial, technological and cultural change as we position the organisation for the decade ahead. We are now seeking an experienced Chief Operations Officer to help shape and lead this next chapter.
**The Opportunity**
Reporting to the Chief Executive Officer, the Chief Operations Officer plays a critical executive leadership role overseeing ACER’s enterprise operations across Finance, Information Technology, Facilities and our global offices. The role ensures ACER has the governance, systems and operational capability required to execute strategy effectively.
The Chief Operations Officer will be a trusted sponsor for major digital, technology, data and operational initiatives, and will work closely with the Chief People and Transformation Officer to modernise ACER’s operating model and strengthen enterprise-wide capability, performance and coherence.
This is a unique opportunity to influence ACER’s long term financial resilience, digital maturity and operational excellence across a complex global environment.
**Key Responsibilities**
  * Provide strategic operational leadership across ACER’s enterprise functions
  * Lead organisational planning, operational performance and governance frameworks
  * Oversee Finance, Information Technology, Facilities, risk, governance and international operations
  * Translate strategy into sustainable financial plans and high-quality business execution
  * Lead major digital, technology, data and operational transformation initiatives
  * Strengthen integration and collaboration across ACER’s offices in the United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates, Malaysia and India
  * Drive process improvement, workflow redesign and organisational scalability
  * Lead financial and commercial analysis for major investment decisions
  * Partner with the Executive Team on strategy and enterprise performance
  * Lead long term facilities and property planning, including capital projects
  * Support workplace strategy to enable current and future ways of working
  * Foster a culture of accountability, collaboration, inclusion and psychological safety


**About You**
  * You are an experienced senior operations executive with expertise in leading transformation in complex environments. You will bring:
  * Extensive executive leadership experience across multi-disciplinary operations
  * Strong governance, strategic planning and organisational performance capability
  * Experience leading financial strategy, commercial analysis and investment evaluation
  * Experience modernising systems and uplifting digital and data capability
  * A successful record of leading large corporate services or shared services functions
  * Excellent communication and stakeholder engagement skills, including at Board and Executive levels
  * Experience working across global or multi-site environments is highly regarded


**Why Join ACER?**
  * Work with a respected international not-for-profit committed to improving learning
  * Lead significant operational transformation with global impact
  * Collaborative, purpose driven and supportive culture
  * Hybrid work arrangements with ability to work from any of ACER’s Australian offices

ACER is one of the world’s leading educational research centres. Our goal is to support every learner, every learning professional, every learning institution and our learning society through our work.
ACER has built a strong reputation as a provider of reliable support and expertise to education policy makers and professional practitioners since it was established in 1930.
As a not-for-profit organisation, independent of government, ACER receives no direct financial support and generates its entire income through contracted research and development projects and through products and services that it develops and distributes.
ACER has experienced significant growth in recent years and now has more than 380 staff located in Melbourne, Adelaide, Brisbane, Dubai, Jakarta, London, New Delhi, Perth and Sydney.

**About ACER**
The Australian Council for Educational Research (ACER) is a national, independent, not for profit organisation with a global footprint spanning Australia, the United Kingdom, India, Malaysia and the United Arab Emirates. With more than 440 staff and a diverse portfolio of assessment, research, professional learning and digital product development, ACER continues to evolve to improve learning across the world.
ACER is entering a significant transformation phase involving structural, financial, technological and cultural change as we position the organisation for the decade ahead. We are now seeking an experienced Chief Operations Officer to help shape and lead this next chapter.
**The Opportunity**
Reporting to the Chief Executive Officer, the Chief Operations Officer plays a critical executive leadership role overseeing ACER’s enterprise operations across Finance, Information Technology, Facilities and our global offices. The role ensures ACER has the governance, systems and operational capability required to execute strategy effectively.
The Chief Operations Officer will be a trusted sponsor for major digital, technology, data and operational initiatives, and will work closely with the Chief People and Transformation Officer to modernise ACER’s operating model and strengthen enterprise-wide capability, performance and coherence.
This is a unique opportunity to influence ACER’s long term financial resilience, digital maturity and operational excellence across a complex global environment.
**Key Responsibilities**
  * Provide strategic operational leadership across ACER’s enterprise functions
  * Lead organisational planning, operational performance and governance frameworks
  * Oversee Finance, Information Technology, Facilities, risk, governance and international operations
  * Translate strategy into sustainable financial plans and high-quality business execution
  * Lead major digital, technology, data and operational transformation initiatives
  * Strengthen integration and collaboration across ACER’s offices in the United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates, Malaysia and India
  * Drive process improvement, workflow redesign and organisational scalability
  * Lead financial and commercial analysis for major investment decisions
  * Partner with the Executive Team on strategy and enterprise performance
  * Lead long term facilities and property planning, including capital projects
  * Support workplace strategy to enable current and future ways of working
  * Foster a culture of accountability, collaboration, inclusion and psychological safety


**About You**
  * You are an experienced senior operations executive with expertise in leading transformation in complex environments. You will bring:
  * Extensive executive leadership experience across multi-disciplinary operations
  * Strong governance, strategic planning and organisational performance capability
  * Experience leading financial strategy, commercial analysis and investment evaluation
  * Experience modernising systems and uplifting digital and data capability
  * A successful record of leading large corporate services or shared services functions
  * Excellent communication and stakeholder engagement skills, including at Board and Executive levels
  * Experience working across global or multi-site environments is highly regarded


**Why Join ACER?**
  * Work with a respected international not-for-profit committed to improving learning
  * Lead significant operational transformation with global impact
  * Collaborative, purpose driven and supportive culture
  * Hybrid work arrangements with ability to work from any of ACER’s Australian offices
