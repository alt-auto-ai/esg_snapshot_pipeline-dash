# Australian National Scope

**How climate reporting is reshaping CEO and Board accountability**

https://www.bdo.com.au/en-au/insights/esg-sustainability/how-climate-reporting-is-reshaping-ceo-and-board-accountability

**Statutory development – climate reporting governance.-**Australia moves to mandatory sustainability reporting, heightening boards and CEOs' climate accountability.

Instrument: standalone reasonable steps declaration; it requires directors to sign governance disclosures with limited liability protections in the initial period, applying to forward‑looking items such as scenario analysis and Scope 3 emissions, with most disclosures retaining broad protections.

The first three years will see directors sign the standalone declaration and operate under reduced-liability protections for certain forward‑looking items; limited assurance is being phased in and governance, data quality, and reporting controls are under heightened scrutiny, with the government indicating ongoing oversight and potential changes by the end of the initial period.
