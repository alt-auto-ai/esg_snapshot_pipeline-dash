On 4 March the President of the Eurogroup, Kyriakos Pierrakakis will travel to Luxembourg. He will give a keynote speech on the topic of “Digital finance – catalyst for European transformation” at the European Investment Bank (EIB) Group Forum.
During his visit he will meet with the President of the EIB, Nadia Calviño, and the Managing Director of the European Stability Mechanism (ESM), Pierre Gramegna.
> ![<p>Kyriakos Pierrakakis, President of the Eurogroup</p>](
> I am delighted to have this opportunity to address the EIB Forum. This is an excellent occasion to discuss the important subject of digital finance. Digital finance goes hand in hand with and complements the Savings and Investments Union.
> ![<p>Kyriakos Pierrakakis, President of the Eurogroup</p>](
> Kyriakos Pierrakakis, President of the Eurogroup
The EIB Group Forum will be held in Luxembourg from 3-5 March and brings together policymakers, business leaders, and experts to exchange views on investment priorities that support Europe's policies.
The European Stability Mechanism (ESM) provides financial assistance to euro area countries in crisis, acting as a lender of last resort to ensure the financial stability and prosperity of the euro area. The President of the Eurogroup is the Chairman of the Board of Governors of the ESM.
