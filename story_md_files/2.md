Resources Victoria will be promoting the State’s growing minerals sector at the Prospectors & Developers Association of Canada (PDAC) conference next week.

PDAC is one of the world’s largest exploration and mining events, attracting about 30,000 attendees from more than 130 countries.
To be held in Toronto from 2–5 March, the conference will provide a platform for Resources Victoria to speak directly to international mineral investors, explorers and developers about opportunities within the state.
Joining with other geoscience agencies under the banner “Australia Minerals”, Resources Victoria will support efforts to help establish local critical mineral supply chains and promote investment-ready projects.
The visit will support the implementation of the state’s [Critical Minerals Roadmap]( which is helping to establish Victoria as an attractive destination for responsible resource investment and development.
