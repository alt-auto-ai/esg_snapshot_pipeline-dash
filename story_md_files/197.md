We have published version 57 of the [register of solar water heaters](https://cer.gov.au/schemes/renewable-energy-target/small-scale-renewable-energy-scheme/small-scale-renewable-energy-systems/solar-water-heaters/register-solar-water-heaters "Register of solar water heaters"). It is effective as of today, 1 May 2026.
The register lists solar water heaters and air source heat pump models that are eligible for [small-scale technology certificates](https://cer.gov.au/schemes/renewable-energy-target/small-scale-renewable-energy-scheme/small-scale-technology-certificates "Small-scale technology certificates") under the [Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme](https://cer.gov.au/schemes/renewable-energy-target/small-scale-renewable-energy-scheme "Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme").
Model names in the register can differ from the marketed model name. If you can't find a particular model in the register, contact the manufacturer or retailer to find out the name of the model used on the register.
