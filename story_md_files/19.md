#  NSW manufacturers to showcase world-class capability at Australian Manufacturing Week
7 NSW manufacturers will exhibit on the NSW Government stand from 12-14 May 2026.
![](https://www.investregional.nsw.gov.au/sites/default/files/2026-05/At%20the%20stand%20AMW.jpg)
The NSW Government will support seven innovative NSW manufacturers to showcase their products, expertise and cutting-edge capabilities at Australian Manufacturing Week (AMW) 2026 in Brisbane.
Scheduled from 12-14 May, AMW is Australia’s premier event for the advanced manufacturing sector and will bring together manufacturers, technology providers and researchers across the country to highlight the future of Australian manufacturing.
Through a dedicated NSW Government stand, the selected businesses will have the opportunity to connect with new customers, investors and supply chain partners, while demonstrating the depth and diversity of manufacturing capability across metropolitan and regional NSW.
The NSW Government’s participation reflects its commitment to strengthening local manufacturing, supporting businesses to grow, innovate and export. This includes significant reform to government procurement to better support NSW manufacturers and create new opportunities for local industry.
The Government is focused on attracting new investment and supporting reinvestment by highlighting NSW’s strong supply chains, skilled workforce and a connected ecosystem that fosters manufacturer growth and innovation.
Under the [NSW Industry Policy](https://www.nsw.gov.au/departments-and-agencies/investment-nsw/resources/nsw-industry-policy), the Government is working with industry to lift productivity, diversify the economy, build sovereign capability and help manufacturers scale up, adopt advanced technologies, and access new domestic and international markets. 
NSW manufacturers exhibiting on the NSW Government stand at AMW this year include:
**PGSR Pty Ltd, Hunter Region**   
An energy and industrial manufacturer and services provider delivering skilled, reliable and cost-effective services across maintenance, service and 24/7 breakdown support for the energy and industrial sectors.
**Resourceful Living Group, Hunter Region**  
A sustainable manufacturer transforming 100 per cent Australian plastic waste into high-quality recycled materials, furniture and building products, supporting circular economy outcomes across construction and commercial sectors.
**Safe Gauge Pty Ltd, Hunter Region**   
Designer and manufacturer of innovative wireless testing tools used across mining, construction and heavy equipment industries. 
**A &I Coatings, Southern Highlands**  
Manufacturers of high-performance industrial coatings for corrosion protection, flooring, infrastructure, OEM, architectural and commercial applications, with growing domestic and export markets.
**Cochlear Ltd, Macquarie Park**  
A global medical device manufacturer designing and producing world-leading cochlear implant technology, backed by extensive local research and development, engineering and advanced manufacturing expertise in NSW.
**Moxon Industries, Western Sydney**  
An engineering, metal machining and fabrication business servicing rail, infrastructure, mining, defence, food and packaging sectors, with a strong focus on quality, capability and export readiness.
**Novon Lighting, Western Sydney**  
Designers and manufacturers of sustainable, high performance commercial lighting products, using precision engineering and advanced manufacturing to deliver energy efficient solutions.
Businesses were selected through an Expression of Interest process, which invited NSW-based manufacturers, aligned with government priorities, to co-exhibit on the NSW Government stand. 
The stand will also promote broader support available to manufacturers, including access to the Advanced Manufacturing Readiness Facility, skills and training partnerships, innovation programs, and trade and investment assistance.
