The **pilot phase of the WENUS e-Learning platform has been launched**!
This platform has been developed to support vocational education and training (VET) in sustainable wood and timber construction. It integrates circular economy principles and 3D printing technologies into learning activities and training materials.
The pilot platform provides:
  * learning resources on sustainability and circular economy in the wood and timber sector,
  * training materials for VET learners and professionals,
  * practical content related to innovative construction approaches and digital technologies.


During the pilot phase, participants are invited to explore the platform, test the training content and share feedback to support the improvement of the final version of the platform and courses.
**VET educators, learners, professionals and stakeholders interested in sustainable timber construction are asked to contribute by 10 July.**

WENUS is an Erasmus+ project coordinated by Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), together with partners from InnoRenew CoE, Universidad Politécnica de Cartagena, CETEM Technological Centre for Furniture and Wood, and Globalnet.
