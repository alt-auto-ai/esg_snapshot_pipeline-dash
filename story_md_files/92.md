In April 2026, we released the [terms of reference](https://www.dcceew.gov.au/climate-change/emissions-reporting/national-greenhouse-energy-reporting-scheme/review-nger-method-2 "Review of NGER Method 2 for estimating fugitive emissions from open-cut coal mines") for the review of the National Greenhouse and Energy Reporting (NGER) scheme Method 2 for open-cut coal mines.
Facilities use Method 2 to estimate fugitive greenhouse gas emissions from open-cut coal mines in Australia. We want to ensure the method:
  * remains fit for purpose 
  * reflects the most up to date science, data, technologies and practices. 


This review progresses implementation of the Australian Government’s [response](https://www.dcceew.gov.au/about/reporting/obligations/government-responses/2023-cca-review-nger-legislation "Australia Government response to the 2023 CCA review of the National Greenhouse and Energy Reporting Legislation") to the [2023 Climate Change Authority review of the NGER Scheme](https://www.dcceew.gov.au/climate-change/emissions-reporting/national-greenhouse-energy-reporting-scheme/cca-review-nger-legislation "The Climate Change Authority’s review of the National Greenhouse and Energy Reporting legislation: background"). 
We are holding a webinar to share information on:
  * the purpose of the review
  * the development, requirements and implementation of Method 2
  * related compliance, monitoring and enforcement activities
  * the issues the review is addressing
  * how the review will be undertaken
  * how stakeholders can engage with the review.


## Register for the online webinar
