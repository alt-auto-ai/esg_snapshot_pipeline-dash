The European Reuse Barometer is a data-driven initiative assessing the state of reuse & refill across Europe. It provides key insights for companies, policymakers and industry stakeholders working toward a circular economy and tracks the progress of reusable packaging & refill systems operating in Europe.
Reuse systems are scaling across Europe, but reliable data is essential to support this transition. If your business is in the reuse and refill industry, complete the survey and help inform EU and national policy discussions!
