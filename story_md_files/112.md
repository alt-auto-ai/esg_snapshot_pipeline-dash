# Alpha HPA First Project Case Study
  * 10 Mar 2026
  * Case Study


Read the case study to understand why high purity alumina is becoming a cornerstone of the global energy transition—and what it takes to build this capability in Australia. 
![Alpha HPA First Project Case Study mockup on blue background](https://cdn.naif.gov.au/media/lc5nm5a1/alpha-hpa-web-banner.jpg?rxy=0.7354709189497158,0.4904073672004253&width=430&height=365&format=webp&quality=80&v=1dcb0add5a51970&v=1)
Discover how a first‑of‑kind manufacturing project is helping to build Australia’s critical minerals capability.
Gain deeper insight into the global demand for high purity alumina, the role HPA plays in the energy transition, and how Alpha HPA is scaling low‑carbon, advanced processing in regional Queensland. You’ll also see how industry clustering in the Gladstone State Development Area, renewable electricity and proprietary technology are supporting efficiency and security of supply.
Market and sector insights are combined with practical risk learnings and detail on how an emerging‑industry project was financed, staged and delivered—offering lessons that can be applied across critical minerals and advanced manufacturing projects.
