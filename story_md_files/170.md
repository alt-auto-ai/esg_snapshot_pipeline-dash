The Q1 2026 Quarterly Carbon Market Report (QCMR) data workbook is available now.
Forecasts and commentary on these data will be included in the full QMCR, expected by mid June 2026.
We publish the QCMR data workbook to provide the Australian carbon market access to information sooner.
When the QCMR is published, the data workbook will be updated with further data and charts that appear in the report.
Explore the [Q1 2026 QCMR data workbook](https://cer.gov.au/document/qcmr-data-workbook-march-quarter-2026 "QCMR data workbook - March quarter 2026").
