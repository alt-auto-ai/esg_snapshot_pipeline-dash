# APRA releases Monthly Authorised Deposit-taking Institution Statistics for April 2026
Friday 29 May 2026
[Print](https://www.apra.gov.au/news-and-publications/apra-releases-monthly-authorised-deposit-taking-institution-statistics-for-72)
The Australian Prudential Regulation Authority (APRA) has released its Monthly Authorised Deposit-taking Institution Statistics (MADIS) publication for April 2026.
The April 2026 publication is available on the APRA website at: [Monthly Authorised Deposit-taking Institution Statistics publication](https://www.apra.gov.au/monthly-authorised-deposit-taking-institution-statistics "Monthly Authorised Deposit-taking Institution Statistics")

##  Fact sheets and checklists
To help you understand and get ready for Payday Super, we've developed the following resources.
  * [Key changes to super guarantee (PDF, 175KB)This link will download a file](https://www.ato.gov.au/api/public/content/2fb02c05407142a2a416ae2eb2308dc5?v=da9af6bd) fact sheet compares the super systems that apply before and after 1 July 2026, so you can see what's changing.
  * [Qualifying earnings (QE) (PDF, 164KB)This link will download a file](https://www.ato.gov.au/api/public/content/62742ead21de47fabb842d9a36b66ad2?v=a1d1cc85) fact sheet explains what you need to know about QE. For more detailed information, see [What payments are qualifying earnings](https://www.ato.gov.au/businesses-and-organisations/super-for-employers/payday-super/paying-super-on-payday/what-payments-are-qualifying-earnings).
  * [SuperStream changes (PDF, 155KB)This link will download a file](https://www.ato.gov.au/api/public/content/abd07e72b374403bbfe756df339cf21c?v=b860634e) fact sheet explains the changes happening to SuperStream.
  * [Payday Super checklist for employers (PDF, 199KB)This link will download a file](https://www.ato.gov.au/api/public/content/50c53f9b799c4c24b19d8c148a298551?v=22ad05c5) includes information on when and how to prepare for Payday Super.
  * [Small Business Superannuation Clearing House (SBSCH) checklist (PDF, 771KB)This link will download a file](https://www.ato.gov.au/api/public/content/73bd8eac60b94af491397ade39fd91cf?v=30826897) explains how to transition from the SBSCH.
  * [Payday Super – easier to read](https://www.ato.gov.au/about-ato/using-our-website/easier-to-read-information/payday-super-easier-to-read) Easier to read information about Payday Super changes for employees.
