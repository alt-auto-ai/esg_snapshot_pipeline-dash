# APRA releases Monthly Authorised Deposit-taking Institution Statistics for April 2026
Friday 29 May 2026
[Print](https://www.apra.gov.au/news-and-publications/apra-releases-monthly-authorised-deposit-taking-institution-statistics-for-72)
The Australian Prudential Regulation Authority (APRA) has released its Monthly Authorised Deposit-taking Institution Statistics (MADIS) publication for April 2026.
