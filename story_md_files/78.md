#  EOI to exhibit at Australia’s Premier Food Industry Trade Show are open! 
The NSW Government is proud to participate in Fine Food Australia 2026 from 31 August to 3 September 2026 at the Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre.
![](https://www.investregional.nsw.gov.au/sites/default/files/2026-05/20240903P_InvestmentNSW_FineFoodFair_0141-PRINT.JPG)
As the largest food industry trade show in the Southern Hemisphere, [Fine Food Australia](https://www.nsw.gov.au/regional-and-primary-industries/regional-business-and-economy-nsw/fine-food-australia-2026) attracts more than 26,000 trade visitors from over 50 countries each year, offering unmatched exposure to buyers, distributors and industry leaders.
Showcase your brand in the Flavours of Regional NSW / Tastes of Regional NSW zone.
The Flavours of Regional NSW / Tastes of Regional NSW zone celebrates the state’s diverse food and beverage manufacturing capabilities while highlighting government support for industry growth.
We are inviting Expressions of Interest (EOI) from Aboriginal and regional NSW food and beverage businesses to co-exhibit within the dedicated Tastes of Regional NSW stand.
What’s on offer:  
• Opportunity to exhibit within a premium, government-supported showcase  
• Exposure to thousands of national and international trade buyers  
• Inclusion in a curated display highlighting regional excellence and innovation  
• Networking opportunities across the global food and beverage sector.
Up to 15 businesses will be selected through a competitive EOI process.
This initiative is led by Regional Economic Development within the Department of Primary Industries and Regional Development.
[Submit your EOI today](https://www.nsw.gov.au/regional-and-primary-industries/regional-business-and-economy-nsw/fine-food-australia-2026) to be part of this exciting opportunity to elevate your brand on a national and global stage.
Spaces are limited, so don’t miss your chance to represent the best of regional NSW at Australia’s premier food industry event.
Please note: Successful applicants will be required to contribute $500 towards participation to attend a series of pre-event online Trade Show Readiness workshops on 1, 6, 15 and 22 July 2026.
We look forward to you taking part in this exciting opportunity to showcase the tastes of Regional NSW.
