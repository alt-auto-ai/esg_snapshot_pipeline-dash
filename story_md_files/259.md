# Have your say on the Berwick-Cranbourne, Clyde-Five Ways and Pattersons Roads Intersection Upgrade
Share
[Back to Newsfeed](https://bigbuild.vic.gov.au/news)
Planning is underway for an upgrade of the Berwick-Cranbourne Road, Clyde-Five Ways Road and Pattersons Road intersection in Clyde.
The upgrade will replace the existing roundabout with traffic lights to improve safety.
## Have your say
To help inform our planning we want to hear your views on safety, traffic flow and how you use the intersection.
To have your say, click the button below by **Sunday, 31 May 2026**.
[Give feedback ](https://engage.vic.gov.au/bciu)
## Meet the project team
You can also chat with the project team, provide feedback about the intersection or ask question about the upgrade on:
  * [Saturday 23 May 2026 - Shopping on Clyde, 280 Berwick-Cranbourne Rd, Clyde VIC 3978](https://bigbuild.vic.gov.au/community/community-events/roads/berwick-cranbourne-road,-clyde-five-ways-road-and-pattersons-road-intersection-upgrade-meet-the-project-team).


Drop in any time between **10am and 3pm.**
## Next steps
Feedback received will be used to inform our planning and construction approach for the intersection upgrade.
We expect initial planning work will be complete in mid-2027.
Construction timelines will be known once planning has progressed further.
