#  Response to the Jobs and Skills Australia Act (2022) Review
JSA Commissioner Barney Glover has welcomed the Review of the Jobs and Skills Australia Act (2022) report, which has been tabled in Parliament.
Watch below as the Commissioner provides his thoughts on the Review.
JSA has today provided a response to the Review as an important milestone in our service offering to our stakeholders and partners alike.

The Review was tabled with the House of Representatives on 5 March 2026.
