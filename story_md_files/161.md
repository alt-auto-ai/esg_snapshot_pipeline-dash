#  Federal Budget provides funding for continuing measures
Date
13/05/2026 - 12:00pm
On Tuesday 12 May 2026 the Treasurer, the Hon Dr Jim Chalmers MP, delivered the 
  * $40.8 million over two years from 2026–27 for the _Attorney-General’s Portfolio – additional resourcing_ measure; 
  * $14.6 million over four years from 2026–27 ($3.7 million per year ongoing beyond the forward estimates) for the _Securing the National Disability Insurance Scheme for Future Generations_ measure;
  * $4.2 million in 2026–27 for the _Supporting Border Security_ measure; and
  * $0.2 million in 2026–27 for the _Home Affairs – additional resourcing_ measure.


The CDPP thanks the Australian Government, the Attorney-General and agency partners for their support in the form of this continued funding which is an acknowledgment of our work as the peak agency in the Commonwealth criminal justice system.
