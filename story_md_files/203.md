#  Resources to enhance RTO capability in supporting VET students with disability and mental health 
26 May 2026
![RTO resources available – Supporting students with disability and student mental health](https://www.dewr.gov.au/sites/default/files/styles/article_sm/public/images/2026-05/Banner%20%E2%80%93%20Resources%20to%20enhance%20RTO%20capability%20in%20supporting%20VET%20students%20with%20disability%20and%20mental%20health.png?itok=JHtxk_JJ)
We have released guidance materials to help registered training organisations (RTOs) support and respond to VET students who require additional support.
These resources are designed to assist building capability in small to medium RTOs, with the aim of helping to improve inclusivity in policy design and procedures for students with disability and to support student mental health. The resources include:
  * guides
  * templates
  * practical illustrations
  * other supporting resources.


You can access these resources on the [Resources for training providers](https://www.dewr.gov.au/skills-information-training-providers/resources-training-providers) web page.
