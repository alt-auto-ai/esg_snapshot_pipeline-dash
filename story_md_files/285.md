The IEEE 10th International Forum on Research and Technologies for Society and Industry (RTSI 2026) will include a [special session](https://2026.ieee-rtsi.org/track-list/) on  _AI-powered innovations for circular economy: Advancing sustainable recycling_.
It will explore how AI, robotics and data-driven technologies can support efficient waste management, material recovery and circular economy solutions.
They are**calling for contributions** **from academia and industry** on topics including:
  * AI-based waste sorting and recycling
  * Robotics for automated disassembly
  * Computer vision for recycling systems
  * Edge AI for real-time processing
  * Predictive analytics and recyclability assessment
  * Circular supply chains and industrial applications


**Submission deadline: 5 𝐉𝐮𝐧𝐞**
**Acceptance notification: 15 June**
You can find all the [details about submissions here](https://2026.ieee-rtsi.org/initial-paper-submission/).
RTSI 2026 will take place from 16–18 August at Aalto University in Espoo, Finland.
