# NSW Threatened Species Scientific Committee final determinations for May 2026
08 May 2026 
News Scientific Committee Threatened species Animals and plants News and media releases
The NSW Threatened Species Scientific Committee assesses which species are eligible for listing as threatened species.
Three plants have been listed as threatened species.
## NSW Threatened Species Scientific Committee final determinations
### _1. Uromyrtus australis_
The tree _Uromyrtus australis_ A.J.Scott has been listed as a critically endangered species.
_Uromyrtus australis_ is endemic to New South Wales where it is restricted to the South Eastern Queensland bioregion.
More information on the species can be found in the Committee’s reasons for final determination and conservation assessment report using the Common Assessment Method: [_Uromyrtus australis_](https://www.environment.nsw.gov.au/topics/animals-and-plants/threatened-species/nsw-threatened-species-scientific-committee/determinations/final-determinations/uromyrtus-australis "Uromyrtus australis A.J.Scott – critically endangered species listing | Final determination 2026").

### 2. _Eidothea hardeniana_
The tree _Eidothea hardeniana_ P.H.Weston & Kooyman has been listed as a critically endangered species.
_Eidothea hardeniana_ is endemic to New South Wales in the South Eastern Queensland Bioregion.
More information on the species can be found in the Committee’s reasons for final determination and conservation assessment report using the Common Assessment Method:[_Eidothea hardeniana_](https://www.environment.nsw.gov.au/node/72116) _._

### 3. _Pherosphaera fitzgeraldii_
The shrub _Pherosphaera fitzgeraldii_ (F.Muell.) F.Muell. ex Hook.f., has been listed as a critically endangered species.
_Pherosphaera fitzgeraldii_ is endemic to the upper Blue Mountains, New South Wales.
More information on the species can be found in the Committee’s reasons for final determination: [_Pherosphaera fitzgeraldii_](https://www.environment.nsw.gov.au/topics/animals-and-plants/threatened-species/nsw-threatened-species-scientific-committee/determinations/final-determinations/pherosphaera-fitzgeraldii "Pherosphaera fitzgeraldii \(F.Muell.\) F.Muell. ex Hook.f. – critically endangered species listing | Final determination 2026") _._
