Prefer a face-to-face chat? Come meet the Fisheries Queensland team on the ground.
We’re heading out to boat ramps, tackle stores and fishing competitions to share information about how your catch counts and how the [Qld Fishing 2.0 app]( works.
You'll be able to:
  * chat directly with our team
  * learn how the [Your Catch Counts program]( supports sustainable fishing
  * get hands-on help with the Qld Fishing 2.0 app
  * ask questions and share feedback.


Whether you’re new to the app or just want to understand how your data makes a difference, come and have a yarn with us.
We look forward to seeing you at one of these events, because your catch really does count.

  * Cairns: 13–15 March 2026
