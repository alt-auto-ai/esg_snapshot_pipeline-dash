#  Applications for the ECESP's 2026 annual conference networking village are now open!

The [European Circular Economy Stakeholder Platform](https://circulareconomy.europa.eu/platform/en/about-platform) will hold its annual conference on **22-23 April** in Brussels.
This year's conference will focus on the upcoming Circular Economy Act and provide a high-level platform to discuss its ambitions, scope and policy direction. It will situate the CEA within the broader EU framework, investment needs and global developments.
This two-day event will be held in the Commission's Charlemagne building on the morning of 22 April, and in the EESC's Jacques Delors building from that afternoon.
The Platform is launching a **call for expressions of interest in its always successful networking village**. The projects must be clearly circular and both the submission and the booth must be in English.
This is a space located in the Jacques Delors building, where exhibitors can showcase their circular solutions to a wider audience. It will be open to participants from the afternoon of the first day of the conference.
Do you have a hands-on circular project you would like to promote, or an innovation you would like to share that could spark a conversation on enhancing the single market for circular products and services?
Tell us more by [filling in this form](https://ec.europa.eu/eusurvey/runner/ECESP_annual_conference_2026_networking_village) and submitting it by **12:00 CET on 24 March**.
The ECESP Secretariat will assess the projects and contact the successful candidates on **8 April**.
Find more information about the conference on the [ECESP website](https://circulareconomy.europa.eu/platform/en/ecesp-annual-conference-2026-competitive-and-fair-circular-europe-ambition-heart-single-market).
