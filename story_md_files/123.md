#  Webinar: Higher Education Outcomes
On 27 February, Jobs and Skills Australia hosted a webinar to discuss how we can support students moving from study into the workforce. The panel shared insights from the recently published [Higher Education Outcomes - Exploring Administrative Data Report](https://www.jobsandskills.gov.au/publications/higher-education-outcomes-exploring-administrative-data "Higher Education Outcomes – Exploring Administrative Data"), including: 
  * evidence-based insights into how graduates move from study into work
  * what administrative data reveals that adds to the evidence base
  * the impact of higher education across disciplines on employment and income.


Watch the webinar and read the report for further insights.
