# AASB May 2026 Board Meeting Public Agenda and Papers out now
**Friday, May 1, 2026**
The [public agenda](https://aasb.gov.au/media/e12aopyw/aasbpublicagendam220_14may26.pdf) and [public papers](https://aasb.gov.au/board-centre/current-board-papers/) for AASB Board Meeting 220, to be held on 14 May 2026, are now available.
The Board will discuss the following agenda items:
  * Presentation and Disclosure in Financial Statements of Superannuation and Not-for-Profit Entities
  * AASB Agenda Consultation
  * Post-implementation Review – Leases 
  * Climate-related Financial Disclosures.


Public attendance is by videoconference – to attend, please register by **COB** 12 May 2026 by emailing 
  * Name
  * Entity
  * Email address 
  * The agenda number(s) for the session(s) you wish to observe.
