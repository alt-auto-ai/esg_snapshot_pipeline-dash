# MRIWA RESEARCH SHOWCASE 2026
9 March 2026
Intelligent Transitions: The Role of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning in Building a Sustainable Resource Future
## Join us at the MRIWA Research Showcase 2026!
This event will showcase **cutting-edge research and emerging applications of AI and machine learning** across the mining and minerals sectors, highlighting their role in shaping a sustainable future. Explore current innovations, future possibilities, and the associated risks and opportunities—fostering a shared vision for strategic research and collaboration. From smarter exploration to predictive maintenance and sustainability breakthroughs, discover how technology is transforming resources. Network with leading researchers, innovators, and industry experts driving the next era of mining innovation.
