**6 Mar 2026**
Acknowledging the outstanding contributions made by Victorian women. 
Victoria is celebrating **International Women’s Day** (IWD) by highlighting the achievements and leadership of women in workplaces and communities.
Celebrated every year on 8 March, International Women’s Day recognises the achievements of women worldwide.
This year’s theme,  _Balance the Scales_ , encourages action to create fairer opportunities for all.
Below is a list of some of the ways businesses can be involved in International Women’s Day:
  * share staff stories to raise the profile of women who are leading in your business or community
  * join local initiatives that celebrate women’s achievements
  * participate in local art or storytelling projects that recognise and celebrate women
  * create opportunities for professional development for women in your business or community.


Together, we can help strengthen equality, visibility and representation for women across the state.
## 2026 Victorian Honour Roll of Women Nominations are open
Do you know an extraordinary Victorian woman? Celebrate their contribution by nominating them for the [2026 Victorian Honour Roll of Women.](https://www.vic.gov.au/honour-roll-women)
The Honour Roll is open to:
  * Women who have made a difference to their communities and have spearheaded change.
  * Women who are history heroines, local champions, trailblazers, emerging leaders or change agents.


If this sounds like you or someone you know, nominate now. Self-nominations are also encouraged. Nominations are open until Friday 20 March 2026.
