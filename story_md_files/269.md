# Susie Kropman Joins the Blue Economy CRC
Susie Kropman Joins the Blue Economy CRC
![](https://blueeconomycrc.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Welcome-Susie-Kropman-1-1-1024x1024.png?x99404)**On behalf of the Blue Economy CRC Board and Executive team, we are delighted to welcome Susie Kropman to the team as the Director, Blue Policy and Planning.**
Susie brings a wealth of experience to the team with a foundation in marine science and more than two decades shaping complex ocean, energy and environmental policy at senior levels of the Australian Government. Her leadership in policy areas including offshore renewable energy, ballast water reforms, Australia’s first oil-spill response, shipping environmental issues, and marine park management has grounded her in the practical realities of ocean governance, environmental risk and regulatory reform.
Susie’s extensive experience managing stakeholder dynamics, negotiating domestic and international agreements, international diplomacy, and translating complex science and industry information into workable policy and regulatory pathways will be a true asset to the work of the BE CRC.
Susie is motivated by creating policy frameworks that enable sustainable economic opportunity whilst safeguarding marine ecosystems. Her goal is to ensure the Blue Economy CRC has maximum impact to grow the ocean economy, sustainably.
> “I am so excited to be joining the Blue Economy CRC team! This is a crucial time for the CRC and I look forward to working with the team to maximise the long term legacy and impact of the CRC. I’m grateful for this opportunity to help shape an integrated, commercially viable and environmentally responsible future for Australia’s ocean economy.”
Susie will lead our existing Blue Policy and Planning team in Tasmania and New Zealand, providing program-wide policy oversight, translating research into regulatory frameworks and stakeholder engagement into development consent, and driving strategic leadership on the CRC’s policy and planning agenda.
Please join us in welcoming Susie to the team!
