# Newsletter May 2026
  * 11 May 2026
  * Newsletters


The May edition of NAIF Impact showcases our latest investments, project milestones, and community outcomes shaping growth across northern Australia.
Inside, you’ll read about our latest investments, supporting delivery of more affordable housing in Townsville and student accommodation in Darwin. 
Keep informed about our recent project milestones and review our most recent financial performance metrics in our quarterly summary. Explore available career opportunities and find out about upcoming events we will be attending throughout the north.
