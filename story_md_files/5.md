# Grant submissions closing soon
27/05/2026
Applications for the $1 million Advancing Antimony Grants program close at 5pm on Tuesday 23 June.
Applications for the $1 million [Advancing Antimony Grants program](https://resources.vic.gov.au/critical-minerals/advancing-antimony-grants-program-guidelines) close at 5pm on Tuesday 23 June.
The program is encouraging the minerals sector to explore the technical and commercial aspects of how to best establish the state’s first critical minerals processing facility.
Victoria’s significant mineral deposits mean the state has the potential to be a global major player in ethically produced antimony.
Resources Victoria is guiding efforts to develop a local supply chain to boost the state’s advanced manufacturing, support regional economic development and potentially create new well-paid local jobs.
