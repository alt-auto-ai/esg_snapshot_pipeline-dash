Join the Australian Government graduate program STEM stream for a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience and contribute to creating a better future for all Australians. 
Our department coordinates graduate recruitment as the lead agency for the Australian Government Graduate Program STEM stream. 
When you apply to this stream, you can be considered for graduate roles across participating government departments and agencies, including our department. Last year, we ranked #2 among government graduate employers as a result of feedback from our grads.
During the application process, you’ll be able to choose which participating agencies you’d like to be considered for. This is your chance to nominate to work with us.
