# ASIC cancels AFS licence of Global Pacific Solutions Group Pty Ltd
Published 26 May 2026
ASIC has cancelled the Australian financial services (AFS) licence of Global Pacific Solutions Group Pty Ltd (AFS licence 514164). The licence cancellation took effect on 20 May 2026.
The licence was cancelled because Global Pacific Solutions Group Pty Ltd is no longer carrying on a financial services business.
Under s915B(3)(a) of the Corporations Act, ASIC may cancel an AFS licence held by a body corporate if the body ceases to carry on a financial services business.
Global Pacific Solutions Group Pty Ltd may apply to the Administrative Appeals Tribunal for a review of ASIC’s decision.
## Background
Global Pacific Solutions Group Pty Ltd has held AFS licence 514164 since 24 February 2019.
