# 2025-26 Premier’s Awards for Health and Medical Research winners and finalists announced
News
**Published:** 21 April 2026 
![The 5 winners for the 2025-26 Premier’s Awards for Health and Medical Research](https://djsir.vic.gov.au/__data/assets/image/0007/2472631/PAHMR26-award-winners.jpg)
Congratulations to the winners and finalists of the 2025-26 Premier’s Awards for Health and Medical Research
The awards recognise the extraordinary work of Victoria’s emerging leaders in health and medical research.
This year’s awards winners are:![Dr Brooke Patterson at the 2025-26 Premier’s Awards for Health and Medical Research](https://djsir.vic.gov.au/__data/assets/image/0005/2472629/PAHMR26-Dr-Brooke-Patterson.jpg)Dr Brooke Patterson at the 2025-26 Premier’s Awards for Health and Medical Research
  * Dr Brooke Patterson, Clinical Researcher Award and Premier’s Award for Health and Medical Research Excellence
  * Dr Karyn Ferguson, Aboriginal Researcher Award
  * Dr Ebony Monson, Basic Science Researcher Award
  * Dr Catherine Jan, Health Services Researcher Award
  * Dr Hannah Jarman, Public Health Researcher Award.


These exceptional early-career researchers are expanding what is possible in healthcare and shaping the future of our world-class medical research sector.
The groundbreaking work of this year’s finalists and award winners spans fields including:
  * virology
  * maternal and child health within Aboriginal communities
  * advances in treating orthopaedic injuries
  * using AI to detect glaucoma
  * the impact of social media on body image and eating disorders.


Thanks to the Australian Society for Medical Research for partnering with us to shine a spotlight on these outstanding leaders in their field.
  
Read more at the [Premier’s Awards for Health and Medical Research](https://djsir.vic.gov.au/medical-research/sector-support/premiers-awards) website.
