# Shop local and win in Mordialloc
Share
[Back to Newsfeed](https://bigbuild.vic.gov.au/news)
Works on the [Mordialloc Level Crossing Removal Project](https://bigbuild.vic.gov.au/projects/level-crossing-removal-project/projects/mordialloc) continue, we're running a Spend and Win competition to support nearby traders and encourage the community to continue shopping local.
From **Monday 27 April to Sunday 24 May** , spend $10 in store with participating local traders in Mordialloc and you could win a $200 weekly prize voucher.
Simply fill out a physical form at a participating trader after your purchase, and you’ll be entered into the weekly draw.
Winners will be announced every week, so make sure to stay tuned!
Thank you for continuing to shop local while we remove the [McDonald Street, Mordialloc and Station Street, Aspendale](https://bigbuild.vic.gov.au/projects/level-crossing-removal-project/projects/mordialloc) level crossings and build a new Mordialloc Station. From **21 May** , we’ll open the brand new Mordialloc Station and trains will travel over the new rail bridges.
[Read the terms and conditions](https://bigbuild.vic.gov.au/library/level-crossing-removal-project/lxrp/terms-and-conditions) for more information.
