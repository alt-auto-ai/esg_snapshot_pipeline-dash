## What is the eLearning course about? 
The new eLearning course on ‘Military Mobility and the use of Form 302’**** provides a comprehensive overview of the customs procedure applicable to the movement of military goods across EU borders.
The course explains how Form 302, a specialised customs document, developed in the framework of the Union Customs Code (UCC), supports simplified, secure, and coordinated customs for non-commercial military movement.
