[Have your say(external link)](https://consult.dcceew.gov.au/oei-framework-2025-26-review-charging-arrangements) on the 2025-26 review of charging arrangements for the Offshore Electricity Infrastructure (OEI) framework.
We are reviewing the Cost Recovery Implementation Statement (CRIS) for the OEI framework to ensure the charging arrangements remain fit for purpose for industry.
The review will consider:
  * Assumptions on licence numbers including feasibility licences, research and demonstration (R&D) licences, and transmission and infrastructure licences (TIL)
  * licence costs recovered through Commonwealth levies 
  * temporary financial relief for OEI licence holders
  * overall expenditure to administer the OEI Act framework effectively and efficiently over the next 3 years.


The CRIS supports the administration and regulation of the OEI framework.
Have your say before 5pm AEST Monday 25 May 2026.
