#  Applications are now open for the 2027 Australian Government Graduate Program
Date
06 March 2026

In addition to the [Finance Graduate Program](https://www.finance.gov.au/about-us/careers/entry-level-programs/graduate-program), the Department of Finance is coordinating graduate recruitment as part of the Australian Government Graduate Program (AGGP) and is the lead agency for the 
Graduates with an interest and/or a degree in finance, commerce, business, accounting or other related disciplines are invited to apply to the 2027 Australian Government Graduate Program [Accounting and Financial Management Stream](https://www.finance.gov.au/about-us/careers/entry-level-programs/accounting-and-financial-management-stream).
The Australian Government Graduate Program is for recent graduates who are eager to strengthen their professional skills while working on projects that make a difference to the Australian community.
With one application, you could receive opportunities from multiple departments and agencies – all you have to do next is decide which is the best fit for you!
As an Accounting and Financial Management graduate, you'll work in collaborative teams and be mentored by a senior qualified accountant to build your skills in a diverse range of areas. You’ll also be supported and encouraged to undertake further learning, development and study.
At the conclusion of your program, you’ll be ready to continue your journey in the Australian Public Service. There’s a lifetime of career opportunities waiting for you in the Australian Government.
**!** Applications close on Monday 13 April at 11.30pm AEST.
