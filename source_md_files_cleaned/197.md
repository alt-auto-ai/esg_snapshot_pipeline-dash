We have published version 57 of the [register of solar water heaters]( "Register of solar water heaters"). It is effective as of today, 1 May 2026.
The register lists solar water heaters and air source heat pump models that are eligible for [small-scale technology certificates]( "Small-scale technology certificates") under the [Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme]( "Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme").
Model names in the register can differ from the marketed model name. If you can't find a particular model in the register, contact the manufacturer or retailer to find out the name of the model used on the register.
