View the Q3 2025-26 snapshot including updated financial data for committed loans, public benefit, jobs, Indigenous engagement, and project pipeline.
During this quarter, we announced a $72m loan to QPM Energy for the Isaac Power Project in Moranbah and $70m to Campus Living Villages for the Student Accommodation Project in Darwin. 
Our pre-ID pipeline remains strong with 8 projects in due diligence and 12 at the active enquiry stage. Indigenous procurement reached a cumulative total of $269.5 million across our projects since inception.
This snapshot provides detailed data on our financial support across sectors, project locations, Indigenous engagement outcomes, project pipeline and cumulative financial performance achieved across our infrastructure portfolio.
