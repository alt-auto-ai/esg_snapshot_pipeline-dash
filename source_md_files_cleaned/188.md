5 March 2026
The Australian Government has awarded a new contract to Toll Remote Logistics Pty Ltd to provide essential air freight services to Norfolk Island until 30 June 2029, following a recent competitive procurement process.
Maintaining reliable freight connectivity remains a key priority for the government, recognising the island’s unique geographic circumstances and reliance on air services for the delivery of essential supplies.
Securing this new arrangement with Toll Remote Logistics Pty Ltd supports the ongoing delivery of essential and time-sensitive freight to the island, providing greater certainty for residents, businesses and the wider community.
The government is committed to supporting and strengthening the resilience of the Norfolk Island economy through ensuring air freight services remain adaptable to changing community needs.
The government will continue to work closely with Toll Remote Logistics Pty Ltd and the Norfolk Island community to ensure services remain reliable, responsive and fit for purpose.
