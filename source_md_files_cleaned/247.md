Nominations are now open for the 2026 Commonwealth Procurement and Contract Management Awards for Excellence.
The awards recognise the professionalism and capability of procurement and contract management professionals in the Australian Public Service.
All Commonwealth entities are eligible to nominate for the 2026 awards in one or more of the following award categories:
  * Contract Management Capability
  * Collaboration and Engagement with Industry
  * APS Leader in Procurement and/ or Contract Management.
Nominations close on **19 June 2026**. More information about each category and how to nominate is available on the [2026 Commonwealth Procurement and Contract Management Awards for Excellence]( page.
