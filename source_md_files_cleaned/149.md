First Nations businesses are invited to apply now for the 64th Australian Export Awards.
The First Nations Exporter Award is an opportunity for First Nations businesses to receive recognition for their international success. 
At a challenging time in global trade, Australia’s First Nations exporters remain remarkably resilient. The awards provide an opportunity for recognition for Australian exporters who are successfully navigating these challenges and are Australia’s longest running export awards program.
Eligible First Nations businesses for the First Nations Exporter Awards can be operating in any industry and must have been exporting for 3 years or more.
