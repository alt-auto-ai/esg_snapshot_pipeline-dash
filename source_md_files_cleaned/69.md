Spokespersons for Parliament and for the political groups will hold a briefing on the 9 - 12 March plenary session, on Friday at 11.00 in Parliament’s Anna Politkovskaya press room.
WHEN: Friday 6 March at 11.00
WHERE: Anna Politkovskaya press room in Brussels and via 
Key topics next week include:  
  * Debate on the US-Israel military operation against the Iranian regime and on the 19-20 March EU summit.
  * Tackling Europe’s housing crisis: debate and vote on the recommendations by Parliament’s special committee on housing
  * Debate and vote on an EU single market for defence
  * Debate on the Commission’s energy package and the need for clean, independent and secure energy
  * Debate and vote on an update of the Package Travel Directive to protect travellers
  * Debate on unleashing the single market’s full potential, including for financial services
  * Child sexual abuse online: vote on prolonging an exemption to privacy legislation allowing for the voluntary detection of child sexual abuse material online
  * Protecting copyrighted work in the age of AI: vote
  * MEPs to mark International Women’s Day
  * Formal sitting with the Prime Minister of Armenia, Nikol Pashinyan
  * Announcement of the first laureates of the European Order of Merit
Interpretation of the press briefing will be available in English and French.
You can follow it live from 11.00 on Friday in Parliament’s Anna Politkovskaya press room or via Parliament’s [webstreaming ]( [EbS](
