We have approved Renewable Electricity Guarantee of Origin (REGO) certificates, the first for the scheme. 
The REGO was launched as part of the [Guarantee of Origin (GO) Scheme]( "Guarantee of Origin Scheme") in November 2025.
The first business to receive approved REGO certificates is Ecovantage, a registered participant that creates certificate projects that reduce energy or carbon emissions.
