**Friday, March 6, 2026**
The [public agenda]( and AASB Board Meeting 219, to be held on 19 March 2026, are now available.
The Board will discuss the following agenda items:
  * Not-for-Profit Private Sector Financial Reporting Framework (Tier 3)
  * Conceptual Framework – NFP Amendments 
  * Post-implementation Review of AASB 1060 and AASB 2020-2
  * Tier 2 – Translation to a Hyperinflationary Presentation Currency
  * AASB 1060 Review
Public attendance is by videoconference – to attend, please register by **COB** 16 March 2026 by emailing 
  * Email address 
  * The agenda number(s) for the session(s) you wish to observe.
