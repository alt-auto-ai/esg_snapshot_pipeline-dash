**29 May 2026**
The Department of Transport and Planning have developed a new resource to support planners and heritage practitioners navigate key processes for applying a Heritage Overlay: the Victorian Local Heritage Guidelines.
  * Part A explains the heritage criteria and, the assessment and documentation process.
  * Part B explains how to apply the Heritage Overlay.
