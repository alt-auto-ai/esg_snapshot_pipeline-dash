There's no doubt about it, our website looks very different and is structured differently to the past one, which is a great thing for you, our users. Finding the information you need quickly and intuitively using the top navigation bar is now a breeze as it takes you directly into core content.
The biggest change you'll experience is that information has been collected together around subject topics. We have removed outdated and infrequently accessed content and focused our attention on the core information our users want.
The modern design is fresh and clean and importantly is responsive to mobile users, who make up an ever increasing number of our visitors.
As you take the time to explore the new site, let us know if you come across anything that isn't quite right. It could be a small thing or a big thing (although hopefully not), either way we'd like to know so the site works and looks its best.
Keep an eye out for upcoming improvements as we continue tweaking the site to best serve you now and into the future.
