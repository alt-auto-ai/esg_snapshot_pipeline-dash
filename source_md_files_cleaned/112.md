The General Manager has issued a statement regarding the resignation of Mark Irving KC as Administrator of the Construction and General Division (C&G Division) of the CFMEU, effective 1 June 2026.
In the statement the General Manager explains that he will seek the views of registered organisations, peak councils and industry participants to identify a suitable person with the skills and experience to replace Mr Irving KC. This is a significant appointment which requires the General Manager to consider:
  * his obligations under the _Registered Organisation’s Act_
  * the tasks that remain to be completed under the Scheme of Administration and the 
  * the remaining issues to be addressed within both the C&G Division and the wider building and construction industry.
