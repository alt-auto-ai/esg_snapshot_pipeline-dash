Planning is underway for a future upgrade of Point Cook Road, between Jamieson Way and Dunnings Road in Point Cook, to improve safety, and increase road capacity to cater for population growth.
To help inform our planning, we’d like to hear your views on safety, traffic flow and what we should consider in our planning. To have your say, click the button below by **Sunday 10 May**.
[Give feedback](
You can also chat to the project team, provide your feedback or ask us a question about the upgrade at:
  * Sanctuary Lakes Shopping Centre, 300 Point Cook Rd, Point Cook VIC 3030 - **Saturday 2 May, 10am to 3pm**
Initial planning work will be completed in early 2027 and will be used to inform government decisions on future funding.
