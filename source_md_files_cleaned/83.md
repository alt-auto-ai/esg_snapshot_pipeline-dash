On 27 February, Jobs and Skills Australia hosted a webinar to discuss how we can support students moving from study into the workforce. The panel shared insights from the recently published [Higher Education Outcomes - Exploring Administrative Data Report]( "Higher Education Outcomes – Exploring Administrative Data"), including: 
  * evidence-based insights into how graduates move from study into work
  * what administrative data reveals that adds to the evidence base
  * the impact of higher education across disciplines on employment and income.
