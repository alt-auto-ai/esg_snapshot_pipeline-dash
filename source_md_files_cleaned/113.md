Read our case study on the Kimberley Cotton Gin, for sector‑specific insights into large‑scale cotton processing in northern Australia.
Discover how a single infrastructure project can unlock an entire regional industry. 
This case study gives you deeper insight into the market opportunity for Australian cotton, the evolution of cotton production in the Ord Valley, and the conditions required to make broadacre agriculture viable in the north. You’ll also see how clean energy, export pathways and collaboration across partners are shaping a more resilient, future‑focused supply chain.
This case study offers sector insights, risk learnings and details on how the project was financed and delivered in a remote environment, that other agriculture and regional infrastructure projects can apply.
