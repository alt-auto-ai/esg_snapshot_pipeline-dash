Eligible automotive businesses can now [register their interest(external link)]( to host public electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure at their premises.
As part of the Dealership and Repairer Initiative for Vehicle Electrification Nationally (DRIVEN) Program, the DRIVEN Charger Grant Stream provides $20 million to fund the purchase and installation of public EV fast charging stations at eligible:
  * automotive dealerships
  * service centres
  * EV repairer sites.
The grants stream aims to:
  * deliver more public fast EV charging stations across Australia
  * support automotive dealerships and EV repairers to diversify their business
  * facilitate partnerships between Australian automotive dealerships or EV repairers and EV charging businesses.
If you are considering EV charging infrastructure at your site(s), [register your interest(external link)]( on our website. Grant applications close **Friday, 28 August 2026**.
The register:
  * is free
  * does not lock you into anything
  * helps connect suitable sites with EV charging infrastructure installers and operators.
You do not need to be the business hosting the charger to apply for a grant. Anyone can apply if they meet the requirements and the charger will be installed at an approved site.
