News
**Published:** 1 June 2026 
Rinnai Australia is expanding its Braeside advanced manufacturing facility, which will secure 110 local jobs and many more across the supply chain.
The boost in R&D and manufacturing capabilities at the world-class facility will drive innovation across the company’s product range. This will increase their capacity to develop new:
  * hot water technologies.
It will also scale-up the design and manufacture of energy-efficient appliance solutions. This will help advance Victoria’s clean energy transition.
Rinnai employs 442 staff across its 3 Victorian sites. The company is a major supplier of heating, cooling and hot water appliance solutions. They deliver high-quality products and services to local and global markets.
The company recently participated in our [Digital Jobs program]( The program helped staff build advanced digital and AI skills to strengthen manufacturing capability and support high-skilled jobs in Victoria.
Victoria has the largest manufacturing workforce in Australia, employing more than 260,000 people.
The [Victorian Industry Policy]( and the $150 million [Victorian Investment Fund]( are helping to grow the state’s industrial base. It secures investment in targeted areas like advanced machinery manufacturing, creating jobs and opportunities for Victorian businesses and workers.
