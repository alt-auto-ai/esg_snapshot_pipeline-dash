The Communiqué further welcomes the establishment by the Hub of the Network of Climate Vulnerable Countries affected by Conflicts or High Levels of Humanitarian Needs and welcomes the first set of pilot projects developed under the Hub partnership with The Gambia, Guinea-Bissau, Iraq and Chad and with the support of the GCF.
