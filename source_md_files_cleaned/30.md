  * Critical Minerals and Gold guide available in Japanese, Korean and Indonesian
26 May 2026
The Critical Minerals and Gold in the Northern Territory 2026 documents the Territory’s resource endowment for up to 21 global critical minerals, and exploration potential for a further 14 critical minerals.
The guide includes resource inventories for [aluminium (as bauxite)]( bismuth, [cobalt]( [copper]( iron ore]( [lead]( [lithium]( "lithium"), [graphite]( [magnesium]( [manganese]( [molybdenum]( [nickel]( [phosphate]( [silver]( [rare earth elements]( [titanium (as ilmenite)]( [tungsten]( [uranium]( [vanadium]( [zinc, and]( [zirconium (as zircon)]( guide now also includes gold, making it a comprehensive inventory of the resource potential of the NT.
To find out more and download the guide, visit [NT Critical Minerals and Gold guide | Resourcing the Territory](
