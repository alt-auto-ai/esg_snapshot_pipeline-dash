The Australian Energy Market Operator **(AEMO)** advises under clause 3.15.21(f) of the National Electricity Rules that Photon Energy SGA Pty Ltd (ABN 29 645 266 173) **(Photon Energy)** has been suspended from all trading in the National Electricity Market **(NEM)** with effect from 00:00 hours Australian Eastern Standard time on 7 May 2026.
Photon Energy is registered in the category of Integrated Resource Provider as a Small Resource Aggregator in the NEM.
Photon Energy is ineligible to trade or enter into any transaction in the NEM until such time as AEMO notifies Photon Energy and all other Registered Participants that the suspension has been lifted.
