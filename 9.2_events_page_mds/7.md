## About this talk
Investors are focusing on understanding companies’ exposure to ESG risks and opportunities, driving growing demand for trusted sources of ESG data and measurement. This session examines how FTSE Russell applies a transparent, rules based approach to ESG measurement, supporting integration of ESG considerations into portfolio analysis. The session will also feature corporate case studies to illustrate the application of FTSE ESG Methodology in real world. Key discussion topics: • FTSE Russell’s ESG measurement methodology • Corporate case studies
