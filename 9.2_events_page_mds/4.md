Overview
Adelaide’s premier Fleetwood Mac tribute band, The MAC' brings to you a high energy, foot stomping, sing-along show to remember!
Adelaide’s latest & greatest Fleetwood Mac tribute band, hailing from the McLaren Vale wine region, The MAC' brings to you a 1970’s and 80’s California sound experience, sharing the songs and stories behind some of the greatest songs in the history of rock and roll. The MAC deliver a high energy, sing along, foot stomping show that will have the dancefloor jumpin’ from the first note. Performing the bands classic hits across their Platinum selling studio albums onward as well as several mega hit songs from Stevie Nick’s exceptional solo career. An incredible show for all fans of all ages!
Enjoy the best panoramic ocean views in Adelaide entertaining your guests at our beachfront Bar & Bistro. Established in 1955 with nothing more than a humble tin shed in the sand dunes, we at West Beach Surf Life Saving Club have grown into a pillar of our community and a leader in lifesaving excellence, inclusion, and sustainability. From nurturing the next generation of lifesavers to creating one of the most thoughtfully designed clubhouses in the country, we strive to lead with heart, vision, and impact.
