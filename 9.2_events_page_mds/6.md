#  Webinar: AGM readiness in a digital age 
3 June 2026  2:00pm - 3:30pm 
National, Virtual Event 
Member $0
Non-Member $45
[Register](https://www.governanceinstitute.com.au/events/e10536-webinar-agm-readiness-in-a-digital-age/)[Become a member](https://governanceinstitute.com.au/join-now/)
This webinar will focus on AGM preparation for company secretaries and governance professionals, recognising that the AGM remains one of the most visible and highest risk moments in the governance calendar. As expectations around transparency, engagement and accountability continue to rise, technology and AI are playing an increasingly important role in how AGMs are planned, conducted and managed.
The session will explore key AGM content, themes and expectations, including what boards, shareholders and regulators are currently focused on, alongside practical considerations such as agenda setting, disclosures, shareholder engagement, resolutions, and managing complex or contentious issues. Particular attention will be given to the use of technology in AGMs, including hybrid and virtual meeting formats, digital voting, real time shareholder questions, record keeping and cyber considerations.  
  
Drawing on recent AGM experience, the webinar will also examine how emerging tools, including AI enabled document review, drafting support, meeting preparation and issue tracking, can assist company secretaries and governance teams to prepare more effectively, identify risks earlier and respond more confidently to stakeholder scrutiny. The session will highlight common challenges, emerging trends and lessons learned, and provide practical insights that participants can apply immediately to strengthen AGM readiness while maintaining appropriate governance, oversight and control.
## What's in it for you:
Learning aims and objectives
• Clarify current AGM expectations of boards, shareholders and regulators   
• Support effective AGM preparation and risk management for company secretaries and governance teams   
• Explore how technology and AI can enhance AGM readiness, efficiency and oversight   
• Identify practical ways to use digital tools for agenda planning, disclosures, voting and engagement   
• Highlight emerging AGM trends, challenges and lessons learned from recent meetings   
• Equip participants with practical insights they can apply immediately in the AGM cycle
