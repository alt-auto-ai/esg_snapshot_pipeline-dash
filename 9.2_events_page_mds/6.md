## **Speakers highlights**
#### Jo Seiler, CEO Bali Operation, Siloam Hospitals and Professor and Dean of Nursing and Midwifery, Charles Darwin University
**![](https://www.governanceinstitute.com.au/app/uploads/2025/12/Elizabeth-A.jpg)**
Jo Seiler is a prominent figure in the healthcare sector, serving as the CEO of Bali Operation, Siloam Hospitals, and as the Professor and Dean of Nursing and Midwifery at Charles Darwin University. Her professional journey is marked by significant contributions to nursing leadership, healthcare administration, and clinical education.
At Charles Darwin University, Jo Seiler has been instrumental in fostering excellence in nursing education and research, aligning with the university's mission in the Northern Territory. Her academic leadership includes curriculum development and academic advising within the nursing and midwifery programs.
Jo Seiler's expertise extends to the strategic direction of organizations, ensuring strong corporate governance and adherence to best industry standards. Her role at St John Ambulance NT involves overseeing clinical performance and governance, ensuring patient safety and quality.
Jo Seiler's work has also been recognized in international health service provision, where she has initiated partnerships to improve clinical care standards in developing countries. Her contributions to nursing education and healthcare management are recognized by her peers and the academic community.
#### Chair: Ross Springolo FGIA FCG, Company Secretary, Northern Australia Infrastructure Facility and State Chair, SA/NT State Council, Governance Institute of Australia 
![](https://www.governanceinstitute.com.au/app/uploads/2025/10/Ross-Springolo.jpg)
Ross has 25 years of experience working in governance related roles predominantly in tertiary education, not-for-profit entities and in corporate Commonwealth entities. Ross is the Chair of Melaleuca Refugee Centre and a former non-executive director on FCD Health Limited, SATAC Limited, Northern Centre for Contemporary Inc and the Top End Hospital Network Governing Council.
