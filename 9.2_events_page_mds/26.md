Overview
Western Dairy invites you to take part in our 27th annual Dairy Innovation Day field day.
This event is the largest gathering of the Western Australian dairy industry stakeholders and show cases the range of farm and dairy production innovations and advances in a farm setting.
In providing this as a free event, Western Dairy relies primarily on sponsorship from the commercial sector and we encourage all attendees to support those companies that support us in delivering relevant and timely information to dairy producers.
Dairy Innovation Day 2026 will be hosted by West & Haggerty on Noalimba Farms at 3141 Wests Road, Capel WA 6271.
The Dairy Innovation Day program for 2026 will focus on the theme of ****“Future Farming Systems”.****
Join us at the end of the day to celebrate our vibrant and innovative industry at the Western Dairy Gala Dinner at the Sanctuary Golf Resort in Bunbury.
