## About this talk
Watch the recording of our insightful fireside chat with Martyna Wojciechowska (EcoVadis) and Patrik Åslin (Handelsbanken) as they discuss the evolving ESG regulatory landscape and its impact on procurement. In this session, you'll gain valuable insights on: Understanding the new and upcoming ESG regulations and how they impact procurement Practical steps to ensure compliance and drive sustainability across the supply chain How to measure and track sustainability performance in supplier networks
