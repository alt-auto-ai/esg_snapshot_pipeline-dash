For many directors, especially those newer to the boardroom, it’s not the formal governance model that creates difficulty, but the human dynamics that sit underneath it.
Explore the practical realities of executive board dynamics — how trust is built (and lost), how constructive challenge lands, and how directors can contribute to strong outcomes without slipping into management. You’ll leave with sharper instincts for reading the room, asking better questions, and strengthening the board–executive partnership.

#### This year’s study highlights:
  * Increased governance workload– with implications for the role of the non-executive director and their relationship with the CEO and management 
  * Evolving discussions around director remuneration and board succession planning
  * Profitability challenges for many sub-sectors
  * Challenges and opportunities in measuring organisational purpose
  * Heightened accountability for care governance, quality and client outcomes
  * Emerging governance risks and opportunities across cyber security, artificial intelligence and climate governance
