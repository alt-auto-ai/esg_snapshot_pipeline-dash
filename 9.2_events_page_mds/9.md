#  Townsville Members & Friends Coffee Catch-up 
12 June 2026  7:30am - 9:30am 
Coffee Presto, 103 Bowen Road, Rosslea (Townsville)  QLD 
Member $0
Non-Member $0
[Register](https://www.governanceinstitute.com.au/events/e10253-townsville-members-friends-coffee-catch-up/)[Become a member](https://governanceinstitute.com.au/join-now/)
Join us for a relaxed morning of coffee, conversation and connection with fellow governance professionals in Townsville. 
Please note that **attendees will need to purchase their own food and drinks.**
This informal catchup will also be an opportunity to meet senior Governance Institute staff and members of the Queensland State Council.
**_Please register as your seat is not reserved until you have received a confirmation email._**
## **What is in it for you?**
  * A relaxed environment for members to meet and build professional relationships.
  * Exchange of ideas and experiences related to governance and industry trends.
  * Opportunities for collaboration and mutual support among members.


## Who should attend?
We welcome Governance Institute members and friends from all sectors and industries.
