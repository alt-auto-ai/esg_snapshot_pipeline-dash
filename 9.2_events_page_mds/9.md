##  About 
International development co-operation is at a turning point, with major structural changes. This is a moment for renewal of purpose, priorities and power dynamics. Geopolitical and economic priorities are shifting, public resources for development are tightening, and delivering impact has become a defining test of legitimacy. Meanwhile, demands are rising for development co-operation that promotes trade, investment, and security, leading to inclusive economies, peace, and shared prosperity.
This conference will focus on action, connecting geopolitical realities with development priorities and translating vision into practical strategic directions. Development co-operation leaders will leave with renewed purpose, clearer priorities and actionable pathways for impact. 
Join political leaders, senior policy makers, thought leaders, private sector players, and civil society actors from across the world. The agenda includes keynote speeches, panel discussions, and interactive roundtables, where participants will share visions for the future, challenge assumptions, and identify practical ways forward, shaping the development policies at the frontlines of humanity’s greatest challenges.
