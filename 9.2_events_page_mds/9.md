#  Transforming Transportation 2026: Powering Jobs and Growth

As the world navigates a complex landscape shaped by economic shifts, external shocks and the rise of AI, the 23rd edition of [Transforming Transportation](https://www.wri.org/initiatives/transforming-transportation) will emphasize the pivotal role of transport in catalyzing development that creates jobs, builds resilience and reaches everyone.
Co-hosted by WRI Ross Center and the World Bank, Transforming Transportation 2026 is a comprehensive, week-long program anchored by two main forum days, March 10 and 11, with additional technical sessions and partner meetings throughout the week.
Guest speakers will address a range of critical topics, including mobilizing private sector capital for transport and jobs, building resilient transport systems for reliable connectivity, shaping the future workforce through digitalization and electrification, and more. For virtual attendees, all main forum sessions will be available to livestream in English.
