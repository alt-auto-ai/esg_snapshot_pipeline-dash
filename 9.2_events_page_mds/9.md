#### Join us for this exciting VIC Fellows Luncheon, featuring keynote speaker, Sally Capp AO MAICD. This exclusive event is designed to offer our esteemed Fellows a unique opportunity to expand their professional network, gain valuable insights, and celebrate their significant contributions to the Victorian business and director community.

**Networking Opportunities:** Connect with fellow AICD Fellows, an elite group of influential leaders and contributors. This luncheon offers a rare chance to engage with peers, share experiences, and build meaningful relationships thatcan enhance your professional journey. 
**Inspiration and Insight:** Hear from Sally Capp AO MAICD, former Lord Mayor of Melbourne discussing aspects of leadership during a crisis and ways in which directors can manage their duties whilst balancing the needs and expectations of multiple stakeholders.
**Recognition and Celebration:** Celebrate the achievements of our Fellows and acknowledge their unique contributions to the community. This event is a testament to the stature and influence of AICD Fellows, and we take pride in recognising your role in shaping the future of business and governance in Victoria.
