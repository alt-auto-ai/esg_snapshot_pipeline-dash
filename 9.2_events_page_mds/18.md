Overview
Join us for an eye-opening event where real-life stories of survival will keep you on the edge of your seat.
Some people ****chase**** purpose. Others are ****thrown**** into it.  
  
In this powerful live talk and book launch, Dr Kaushik Sridhar shares how a philosophy he calls '****Follow Your**** Dot' helped him survive and make sense of life’s most extreme moments - from being ****kidnapped**** by Hezbollah, surviving a near ****plane crash**** , and staring down the ****barrel of a gun**** three times… to rediscovering purpose, peace, and clarity in the aftermath.  
  
This is not just a story about resilience - it’s about perspective, courage, and what happens when you choose to see every challenge as part of your path.  
  
Join Kaushik for a purposeful, emotive evening of storytelling, reflection, and connection - a safe space where life’s hardest questions meet their most human answers.
### ****What You’ll Experience****
● A deeply personal story of survival and transformation
● Practical insights on finding purpose in adversity
● Connection over canapés and conversation
