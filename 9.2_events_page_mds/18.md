****Celebrating the best of local business!****
The ****MCCC Business Awards**** celebrate and showcase the exceptional talent, innovation, and impact of local businesses and community contributors across the City of Melville and City of Cockburn. These awards recognise businesses and individuals who go above and beyond in their industries, demonstrate leadership in their communities, and contribute to a thriving local economy.
  * ****14 Awards Across Industry, Impact & Pinnacle Categories**** – including Retail, Hospitality, Innovation, Sustainability, and Business of the Year.
  * ****Unrivalled Exposure**** – finalists and winners featured across MCCC, City of Melville, and City of Cockburn channels.
  * ****Gala Night Celebration**** – an evening of awards, networking, canapés, and celebration at the South of Perth Yacht Club.


The MCCC Business Awards bring together business leaders, government partners and industry stakeholders for a night recognising excellence in local enterprise.
