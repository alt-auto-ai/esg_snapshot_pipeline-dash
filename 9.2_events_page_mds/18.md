# Albury-Wodonga Directors' Briefing: Boardroom Culture and Organisational Impact
30th April 2026 
Albury, NSW
#### The culture around the board table plays a critical role in how well a board performs. Trust, openness, accountability and respectful challenge all shape the quality of discussion, decision making, and ultimately, the direction of the organisation.

Join us at this Directors’ Briefing as we hear from leading directors and experts as they discuss:
  * How board culture influences performance and governance outcomes 
  * What a healthy, high-performing board dynamic looks like
  * The board’s role in setting the tone for the whole organisation
  * How to spot when culture might be holding your board back — and what to do about it


This also presents a valuable opportunity to engage with peers and strengthen your connections within the director community.
