Overview
An interactive workshop helping organisations apply Doughnut Economics to strategy, culture, and decision-making within ecological limits.
## 🍩 Doughnut Design for Business™
****From Doughnut Economics to organisational strategy****
Doughnut Design for Business™ is a facilitated workshop that helps organisations move from inspiration to implementation.
Using the globally recognised ****Doughnut Economics framework**** , teams explore how their organisation can thrive by meeting social needs ****while operating within ecological limits**** , and what this means in practice for strategy, culture, operations, and organisational resilience.
Doughnut Design for Business™ is an official, practitioner-led workshop tool of ****Doughnut Economics Action Lab (DEAL)**** , developed in response to ****growing demand**** from organisations seeking ****practical ways to apply********Doughnut Economics thinking********inside********real business contexts****.
## 🌍 Why Ecological Limits Matter for Business
The ****Planetary Boundaries framework**** - which underpins the Doughnut’s ****ecological ceiling**** - shows that climate change is only one of nine Earth-system boundaries, with seven already in overshoot.
This points to a broader operating context in which climate, resource use, and social factors increasingly interact in ways organisations are seeking to understand in relation to ****risk, resilience, and long-term viability****.
Doughnut Design for Business™ supports organisations to engage with these realities as ****strategic and operational considerations****.
It is a hands-on, collaborative design process focused on real ecological and social considerations, constraints, and ****future possibilities****.
## 💡 What the Workshop Does
Using DEAL’s authorised Doughnut Design for Business™ methodology, participants work together to:
  * Understand Doughnut Economics as a ****21st-century compass**** for organisations
  * Explore ****social shortfall**** and ****ecological overshoot**** , and how the organisation relates to the Doughnut’s social foundation and ecological ceiling
  * Examine how ****purpose, governance, ownership, finance, and incentives**** shape what change is possible, using the Five-Layered Deep Design of Business framework
  * Generate ambitious, organisation-specific ideas for becoming more ****regenerative and distributive by design,**** in line with the principles of Doughnut Economics.


Sessions can be tailored to support sustainability strategy, ESG conversations, leadership development, innovation, or culture change.
## 💬 What Teams Gain
Teams leave with Doughnut-specific insight and capability, including:
  * A shared understanding of how ****planetary boundaries and social foundations**** reshape organisational success
  * Fluency in ****regenerative and distributive thinking**** , and how this differs from green-growth approaches
  * Greater confidence navigating ****21st-century********ecological, social, and economic considerations****
  * Strategic insight into where current business design may be enabling - or constraining - ****emerging economic alternatives**** and organisational expectations


Rather than another disconnected initiative, Doughnut Design for Business™ provides a ****coherent framework for action**** already being used by cities, communities, and organisations globally.
## 👥 Workshop Format
****Two formats are available:****
  1. Introduction to Doughnut Economics (2 hours)
  2. Doughnut Design for Business™ workshop (5–6 hours)


****Group size:**** Up to ****20 participants per session****. Can be delivered as ****single sessions or a series**** across multiple teams.
****Delivery:**** On-site or off-site by arrangement. Micro and small businesses may collaborate to form a ****shared workshop group**** (with a suitable venue).
****Locations:**** Local, regional, and interstate delivery available.
