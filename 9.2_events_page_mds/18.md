Organized in collaboration with key industry partners, the **EDA convention** will bring together professionals from the demolition, decontamination, and recycling (DDR) sectors from all around the globe. 
Managing the risks and controlling large complex projects and contractors has challenges. The ERM team is happy to engage with you to address these challenges. Planning and implementation of appropriate processes and controls is key in order to assess the impacts, develop planning and bid documents, support contractor bidding processes. We can either provide turnkey management of the removal or support portions of the effort including supervision, safety, waste, and environmental management during the project implementation.  
  
We have an experienced team providing a source of trained and experienced partners, demolition managers, field safety officers, asbestos certified personnel, and tank system removal personnel. Let’s chat! 
Come and have a chat with us!
