Overview
Visitors are welcome on Sunday 3rd May from 2-4pm for a showaround the Moora Moora Community Cooperative.
Join a tour of the property, meet some of the members and residents of the community and discuss Moora Moora and how we work together to manage our community and property.
Please meet at the Octagon (the brown timber building visible from the front gate). 
Arrive 1:45 for tour commencing at 2pm.
Parking is located on the north side of the building.
We are open for tours on our visitor days, held on the first Sunday of every month at 2 – 4pm (except on days of Total Fire Ban).
