#### About the Tech Governance Forum
The annual Tech Governance Forum was created in 2025 in response to the fast-changing nature of technology-related risk, governance and compliance.
With a dedicated focus on tech issues like cybersecurity, data governance and Artificial Intelligence, this event supports board members and senior executives to confidently navigate this complex area.
The forum features key presentations by subject-matter experts immersed in the world of tech and innovation, multi-topic roundtables, real-world case studies and time to network with peers.
Join your fellow leaders for the Tech Governance Forum 2026 in the heart of Brisbane or watch the livestream from anywhere in the world.
