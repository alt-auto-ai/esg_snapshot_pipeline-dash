Overview
Discover simple, practical ways to measure and reduce your carbon footprint in this interactive workshop for everyday sustainable living.
Are you curious about your carbon footprint and the impact your daily choices have on the planet?
Whether you’re new to sustainability or already making small changes, this workshop is designed to support everyone.
Join us for an interactive workshop where you'll discover what a carbon footprint is, why it matters, and how your everyday actions contribute to climate change. You will explore practical and achievable ways to measure and reduce your personal impact by looking at key areas such as transport, home energy use, and lifestyle habits.
You'll leave with practical steps to help make your everyday choices more sustainable.
This workshop is hosted by the City of Subiaco in partnership with Switch Your Thinking.
Light refreshments will be provided. We hope to see you there!
