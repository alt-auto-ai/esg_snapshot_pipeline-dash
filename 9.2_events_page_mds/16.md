The war in the Middle East and the resulting fuel supply crisis is a stark reminder of the vulnerability of many of Australia’s globalised supply chains. Australian boards are seeking to navigate the current crisis while also building resilience across other key supply chains. 
A panel of senior directors and experts will examine:
  * Immediate steps that have been taken by Australian organisations to respond to fuel and energy supply disruptions and significant price increases;
  * Board identification and oversight of other vulnerable supply chains that underpin the business operations of Australian organisations; and
  * Proactive steps that Australian organisations can take to build supply chain resilience.
