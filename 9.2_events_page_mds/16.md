# Embedding organisational resilience amidst continuous change
[See the program](https://www.governanceinstitute.com.au/governance-and-risk-management-forum/governance-and-risk-management-forum-agenda-qld/#grmf-program)
The challenge for boards and management in a time of continuous change is the imperative to embed resilience at the very core of the organisation. But resilience is often a defensive risk mechanism that comes into play after the fact and is often driven by the strategies of yesterday. 
How then do we shift from a reactive approach to an adaptive culture that integrates resilience into every part of the organisation? How do boards and management look beyond the past and the present to anticipate the new rhythms and turns, that will help the organisation to navigate future turbulence with agility and skill? Because as Peter Drucker famously said “The greatest danger in times of turbulence is not the turbulence; it is to act with yesterday’s logic.” 
Join us for the 2026 Governance and Risk Management Forum where we reflect on and share the practical strategies on the culture and the systems and processes that will shape and define resilience and drive continuous growth and innovation amidst volatility and change.
We will examine how leaders can balance courage with prudence to take advantage of the opportunities that volatility offers the brave; while ensuring you stay true to your purpose and values. How do we enhance board and stakeholder engagement to ensure alignment during crisis? What are the strategies for risk leaders to build the resilience not only of systems and processes but also of their people?
## What is in it for you?
Over the two days you will have the opportunity to hear from our panel of speakers and to engage in active discussions with your peers on some of the practical strategies that can be adapted to your own settings. While day one focuses on governance excellence, day two will unpack the strategies to embed resilience at the core of your risk management practice, culture and framework. The program will feature the popular annual feature sessions, the company secretaries’ session, the state of the economy, the CRO panel.
