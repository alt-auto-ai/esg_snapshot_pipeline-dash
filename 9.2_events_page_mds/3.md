### About the Australian Governance Summit
The Australian Governance Summit brings together the nation’s most influential directors and governance leaders. Over two immersive days, they’ll explore topical issues and challenges shaping change across Australia. 
The Summit offers a unique opportunity to strengthen your governance expertise, broaden your knowledge, and amplify your leadership impact. Beyond the keynotes and expert insights, it’s where you can build connections and be part of a valuable community of like-minded peers who support professional growth. You’ll also hear from thought leaders, get access to roundtables and practical tools needed to stay ahead of emerging trends and shape the next decade of directorship. Join us in-person or virtually.
