#  Crop protection training series with Fresenius 2026
##  Online
Add to Calendar 
ERM experts will lead two upcoming online training sessions in collaboration with Akademie Fresenius. The sessions will focus on genetic toxicology in chemical safety assessment and the environmental fate and behaviour of plant protection products (PPP) in Europe. Drawing on deep regulatory and scientific expertise, ERM will provide practical insights to support robust, compliant decision‑making across the product lifecycle.
##  Training sessions Hide
#####  Environmental Fate and Behaviour Evaluation of PPP in Europe 
Get a fundamental understanding of environmental fate that is essential for exploring and comprehending the assessment of e-fate for crop protection active substances and its application in the registration process under Regulation 1107/2009.
Obtain more information and register here: 
#####  Genetic Toxicology in Chemical Safety Assessment 
Gain essential knowledge of genetic toxicology across sectors including chemicals, plant protection products, biocides, cosmetics, food and feed. This virtual intensive session covers assay conduct, data generation and evaluation, regulatory genotoxicity tests, DNA damage, repair and in vivo approaches.
Obtain more information and register here: 
##  ERM experts Hide
![Damian Bowen](https://www.erm.com/globalassets/damian-bowen.jpg?width=450&quality=10)
##  Damian Bowen 
Technical Director 
Damian has in excess of 20 years toxicology experience, with more than 5 years spent study directing a wide variety of GLP and non-GLP in vitro and in vivo genetic toxicology assay endpoints. Damian gained his undergraduate degree in pharmacology from the University of Sunderland, with postgraduate degrees in toxicology (MSc) and biochemistry and intensive care medicine (DPhil).
Damian has worked at major contract research companies in the world, where he worked with various clients as a study director. He has extensive experience running and supervising genetic toxicology studies, advising clients on their ongoing projects and interpretation of guidelines. He has authored over 300 confidential reports and numerous conference posters and written about refinement, reduction and replacement of existing genotoxic animals models. His work has been cited by United Kingdom Committee on Mutagenicity (COM), EFSA, ECHA, OECD and ICH.
Damian has moved away from the study directing environment, working as the Technical Director of Toxicology at ERM, providing expert toxicological advice for the agrochemical, biocidal and REACh industries along with human risk assessment for pesticides (operator, worker, resident), biocides (various biocidal products including long lasting insecticidal nets, mosquito space spray formulations).
Damian was appointed to the editorial advisory board for the Journal of Applied Toxicology in 2018.

##  Vera Papasova 
Managing Technical Consultant 
Vera Papasova is a Chemist with almost 12 years of experience in Environmental Fate. She joined ERM (UK) in 2022 as a Senior Consultant. Throughout her career, she has developed extensive expertise in environmental fate assessment and regulatory dossier preparation across Europe, bringing together her laboratory background as a Study Director and her consultancy experience.
