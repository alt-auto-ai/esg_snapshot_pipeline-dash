## About this talk
This webinar explores how financial innovation is shaping the path toward a more sustainable and resilient future. This webinar brings together insights from finance and industry to examine how evolving instruments- such as blended finance, transition finance, and risk-sharing mechanisms - can unlock investment at scale and accelerate real world impact across sectors and geographies. Participants will gain a practical understanding of what is changing in the finance landscape, why traditional approaches are no longer sufficient, and how new solutions can better align capital with long term transition goals. Through expert perspectives and real world examples, the session will highlight strategies across sectors for mobilizing capital, managing risk, and bridging the gap between ambition and implementation.

Across African cities and globally, nature-based solutions (NBS) and green-gray infrastructure (GGI) interventions are changing the landscape of climate resilience. When done right, these projects can protect people from climate shocks, enhance biodiversity, and improve socio-economic and health outcomes. WRI, under its [Green-Gray Infrastructure Accelerator](https://www.wri.org/initiatives/green-gray-infrastructure-accelerator) , is bringing a cross-sectoral approach to NBS integration and fostering peer learning exchanges across African cities. However, accelerating NBS and GGI in the Global South requires context-specific approaches, not one-size-fits-all models from the Global North.
In this webinar, WRI teams from Brazil, India and Indonesia will share lessons on designing and financing NBS and the strengths of south-south collaboration. From implementing 30 projects in [Indian](https://www.wri.org/outcomes/indian-cities-pioneer-nature-based-solutions-and-inspire-national-climate-action) cities to structuring finance-ready urban initiatives in [Brazil](https://cities4forests.com/story/nbs-accelerator-brazil-phase-two-announcement/) and replicating accelerator models in Indonesia, speakers will highlight what works—and why. Join us to explore how cities across the Global South are building more resilient, equitable futures through locally grounded, collaborative approaches to green-gray infrastructure.
_This webinar is part of WRI Africa’s Green-Gray Infrastructure Accelerator Webinar Series, which draws on the experience of Cities4Forests, Urban Water Resilience, and partners from across the continent to illuminate best practices for planning, financing, implementing and institutionalizing natured-based solutions for urban climate resilience._  _Watch the recordings of our previous webinars on inclusive implementation of NBS_[ _here_](https://hub.wri.org/events/2025/8/bridging-nbs-policy-practice-finance-africa) _and_[ _here_](https://hub.wri.org/events/2025/9/social-inclusion-community-ownership-successful-nbs-adoption) _; and on policy integration here (_[_English_](https://hub.wri.org/events/2025/11/urban-greening-moving-practice-policy) _|_[_French_](https://hub.wri.org/fr/events/2025/11/vegetalisation-urbaine-de-la-pratique-la-politique) _) and_[ _here_](https://hub.wri.org/events/2026/3/scaling-green-infrastructure-resilient-rwanda) _._
