Overview
Bring in your energy bill and have it reviewed to check if you're on the cheapest plan for you.
Bring in your energy bill for expert advice on the most cost-effective energy plan for you. Learn about available concessions and supports, receive some energy-saving tips and a free 28-page Electrify Your Home Planner.
30-minute timeslots available. Book online or enquire in-branch.
Presented by volunteers from the Macedon Ranges Sustainability Group.
