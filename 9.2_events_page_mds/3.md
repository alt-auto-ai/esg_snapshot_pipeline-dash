# WA Forum 2026
15th May 2026 
Burswood, WA
#### Preparing leaders for WA’s next chapter
This year’s WA forum presents – Steering Through the Storm: Responsible Governance, Technology and the Productivity Imperative. 
With WA entering a new chapter fuelled by strong economic momentum, regulatory reforms, Productivity Commission initiatives and emerging technologies like AI and cyber. Now is the time to explore what these shifts mean for organisations and leaders.
