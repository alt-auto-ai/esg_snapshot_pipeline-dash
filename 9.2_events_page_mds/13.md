30 April 2026  7:30am - 9:30am

Member $0
[Register](https://www.governanceinstitute.com.au/events/e10245-sydney-members-friends-coffee-catch-up/)[Become a member](https://governanceinstitute.com.au/join-now/)

Opportunities for collaboration and mutual support among members.

We welcome Governance Institute members and friends from all sectors and industries.
