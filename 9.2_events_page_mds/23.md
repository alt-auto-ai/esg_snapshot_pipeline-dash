## People Planet Pint and The Circular Economy Club Present ****Waste'a'thon****
  

Get ready for an exciting in‑person event where ****fun meets sustainability****! Join us for ****Waste’a’thon**** , proudly brought to you by ****People Planet Pint**** and the ****Circular Economy Club****.
At Waste’a’thon, you’ll join a team—or bring your own—to ****race against the clock**** as you transform real‑world case studies from their ****current state**** to a vision of ****zero waste to landfill****.
Expect an evening packed with:
  * 🧠 ****Collaboration and problem‑solving****
  * 🎲 ****Curve balls**** that test your creativity
  * 🎤 ****Pitching sessions**** to a panel of expert judges
  * 🏆 ****Awards**** for the most innovative circular ideas


This event is generously supported by ****Griffith University**** , our event partner.
Come for the challenge, stay for the impact—be part of a community driving real change toward a circular future!

At Waste’a’thon, you’ll join a team—or bring your own—to ****race against the clock**** as you transform real‑world case studies from their ****current state**** to a vision of ****zero waste to landfill****.
Expect an evening packed with:
  * 🧠 ****Collaboration and problem‑solving****
  * 🎲 ****Curve balls**** that test your creativity
  * 🎤 ****Pitching sessions**** to a panel of expert judges
  * 🏆 ****Awards**** for the most innovative circular ideas


This event is generously supported by ****Griffith University**** , our event partner.
Come for the challenge, stay for the impact—be part of a community driving real change toward a circular future!
