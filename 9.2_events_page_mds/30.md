WORKSHOP SERIES FOR WOMEN IN SMALL BUSINESS TO ACHIEVE SUCCESS & AVOID BURNOUT.
Building a business can feel exciting, fulfilling… and at times, incredibly overwhelming.
You’re wearing all the hats. Making constant decisions. Trying to stay visible, motivated and financially sustainable, while also managing life behind the scenes.
And sometimes, you simply need space to pause, reset and reconnect with WHY you started in the first place.
If you’re craving support, clarity, confidence and genuine connection with other women navigating similar challenges, this workshop series is for you! 🌱
---
****Introducing the 'Mindset For Success' Workshop Series****
A monthly interactive workshop experience designed specifically for women in the early stages of starting and growing sustainable, profitable businesses from the ground up.
This is not about hustle culture or pretending to have it all together.
It’s about creating strong foundations through mindset, self leadership, community support and purposeful growth.
Together, we’ll explore practical tools, mindset shifts and meaningful conversations to help you grow both personally and professionally, while keeping the spark alive behind the business you’re building.
Because long-term success starts from within.
---
****Our Workshop Journey Together:****
****Tuesday 2nd June → Confidence & Self-Belief****  
Learn how to back yourself, quiet self-doubt and strengthen confidence in your business decisions.  

****Wednesday 1st July → Overcoming Overwhelm****  
Discover practical mindset tools and sustainable habits to reduce stress, create focus and avoid burnout.  

****Tuesday 4th August → Sustainable Success Habits****  
Build supportive routines, stronger boundaries and a business approach that feels aligned, energising and achievable long term.
