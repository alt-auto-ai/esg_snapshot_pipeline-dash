An AMPA Trimester 1, 2026 music theatre showcase
Join us for an unforgettable evening of musical theatre at -
"CHANGE!"
The world is in a strange place: war rages, conflict - both physical and verbal - permeate our culture. Discourse has been replaced by anger and a failure to listen. The real question is: is this anything new?
  

Theatre has always been a catalyst for Social and Political change. Whether through the works of Bertolt Brecht, or a young Jonathan Larson, people who tell stories have never failed to try and leave the world all the better for their art. In this Trimester’s Music Theatre Production you will hear young people sing of all sorts of freedom. You will hear them sing about loss, about abuse, but you will also hear them sing for the hope that’s to come. They will reach out to you and ask you to see them.
  

Come on down on****Friday********1st of May, at AMPA’s Tom Mann Theatre.****
__(This Trimester we will be offering two showtimes:___****6PM****_ __&___****8PM****_ __)__
  

"CHANGE!" promises to be a treat for all music theatre enthusiasts with an AMPA twist.
  

Grab your friends, family and enjoy the show!

An AMPA Trimester 1, 2026 music theatre showcase
Join us for an unforgettable evening of musical theatre at -
"CHANGE!"
The world is in a strange place: war rages, conflict - both physical and verbal - permeate our culture. Discourse has been replaced by anger and a failure to listen. The real question is: is this anything new?
  

Theatre has always been a catalyst for Social and Political change. Whether through the works of Bertolt Brecht, or a young Jonathan Larson, people who tell stories have never failed to try and leave the world all the better for their art. In this Trimester’s Music Theatre Production you will hear young people sing of all sorts of freedom. You will hear them sing about loss, about abuse, but you will also hear them sing for the hope that’s to come. They will reach out to you and ask you to see them.
