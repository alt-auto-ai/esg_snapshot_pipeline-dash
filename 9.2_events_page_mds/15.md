# Spotlight Session | Sustainability Reporting Community of Practice
21 May   
2026
Online   
1.00pm - 2.30pm (AEST)
![](https://unglobalcompact.org.au/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/SRCoP-May.png)
## Event Details
Spotlight sessions within the SRCoP series are designed to provide deeper technical insights through longer-form presentations delivered by subject-matter experts, industry leaders and regulators. These sessions focus on building knowledge and capability across key sustainability reporting topics, offering participants access to practical guidance, emerging practices and evolving regulatory expectations.
While the primary focus is on learning and capacity-building, each session also includes opportunities for peer exchange through facilitated breakout rooms and a Q&A segment. Participants are encouraged to come ready to engage with new information, ask questions, and contribute to collaborative dialogue that supports stronger reporting practices across the community.
_**About the Sustainability Reporting Community of Practice**_
_To help Australian businesses navigate evolving sustainability reporting standards and expectations the UN Global Compact Network Australia (UNGCNA) hosts a Sustainability Reporting Community of Practice (SRCoP), in collaboration with the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX) and Chartered Accountants Australia and New Zealand (CA ANZ)._
_This community of practice provides a collaborative and trusted environment where members can access insights, share challenges and explore solutions to meet both mandatory and voluntary reporting requirements._
_[Find out more and how to join here](https://unglobalcompact.org.au/communities-of-practice/sustainability-reporting-community-of-practice/). _
