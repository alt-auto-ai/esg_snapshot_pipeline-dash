Get ready for Climate Fresk - A collaborative, game-based workshop helping teams build climate science literacy, connection and purpose.
### 🌿 ****Climate Fresk – Solve the Climate Puzzl********e****
  
Experienced by ****more than two million participants worldwide**** , Climate Fresk is a collaborative, game-based workshop that helps teams build a shared understanding of climate change and its implications for organisations, communities, and decision-making.
Grounded in climate science and designed for participation, Climate Fresk strengthens ****climate literacy**** , improves ****internal communication**** , and helps teams align around the challenges and opportunities of a rapidly changing world.
Delivered as a stand-alone session or as part of a broader sustainability or strategy program, Climate Fresk supports organisations to move beyond surface-level awareness toward ****informed, collective action****.
### 💡 ****What It Is****
A ****three-hour, team-based experience**** where participants work together to solve the climate puzzle.
Using ****42 illustrated cards grounded in IPCC science**** , teams map the cause-and-effect relationships driving climate change and its impacts on ecosystems, societies, economies, and organisational contexts.
Participants leave with a clear, shared understanding of:
  * how the climate system works
  * why it is changing
  * how climate risks and transitions connect to their organisation, sector, and communities


Sessions can be tailored to align with your organisation’s sustainability initiatives, risk conversations, or internal communication priorities.
### 💬 ****What Teams Gain****
✅ ****Climate literacy**** - a shared, science-backed understanding of climate change and its drivers.
✅ ****Confidence**** - clearer language and framing to discuss climate issues across teams, clients, and leadership.
✅ ****Connection**** - a highly collaborative experience that builds trust, empathy, and collective intelligence.
✅ ****Direction**** - a stronger foundation for informed decision-making and meaningful climate action.
### 👥 ****Workshop Format****
****Duration:**** 3 hours.
****Group size:**** up to ****16 participants**** per session. Can be delivered as ****single sessions or a series**** across multiple teams.
****Delivery:**** On-site or off-site by arrangement. Micro and small businesses may collaborate to form a ****shared workshop group**** (with a suitable venue).
****Locations:**** Local, regional, and interstate delivery available.
****Facilitator:**** Tom Foster, founder of EcoProsper Consulting and a ****certified Pro Climate Fresk facilitator****.
