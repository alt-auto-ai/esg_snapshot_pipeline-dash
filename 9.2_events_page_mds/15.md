#  WBCSD Annual Meeting 
Start date 
27 April, 2026 
End date 
30 April, 2026 
Location 
Montreux, Switzerland 
Share:
[Register here (members-only)](https://flagshipevents.wbcsd.org/am26/)
[About the meeting](https://events.wbcsd.org/annual-meeting-26/)
Businesses are navigating a world that is rapidly changing and presenting numerous challenges. In that context, we believe that effective leadership, collaboration, and careful implementation will lead to progress. To support this much needed collaboration and implementation, starting this year, WBCSD will convene one [Annual Meeting](https://flagshipevents.wbcsd.org/am26/) in Montreux for both Council Members and Liaison Delegates, as the central forum for strategic dialogue and peer exchange across our membership; combining strategy and implementation power to accelerate decisions, identify potential constraints and turn sustainability goals into real business value.
Structured around three tracks – Executive, Practitioner and ROI – the program brings together C-suite leaders, technical experts and strategy owners to navigate complexity, foster collaboration and demonstrate clear business value.
Join fellow senior executives in Montreux, Switzerland (27–30 April) to shape business-led solutions, influence implementation at scale, and translate sustainability into measurable performance for your company. Members are encouraged to visit our member [**website**](https://flagshipevents.wbcsd.org/am26/) to register (contact [**here** ](https://events.wbcsd.org/annual-meeting-26/)to find out more about the Meeting.
