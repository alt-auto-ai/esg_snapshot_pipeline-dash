Directors have a critical role to play when their organisation is facing a crisis. However, we find ourselves in an environment where it feels as though everyone wants to be “outraged”.
Social media, traditional media, politicians, commentators – everyone is looking to “cut through” and outrage is their chosen method. The result is that crisis management has become significantly harder and much more problematic. 
Join us at this Directors’ briefing and hear from John Rawnsley GAICD on how to manage the waters when a crisis hits.
