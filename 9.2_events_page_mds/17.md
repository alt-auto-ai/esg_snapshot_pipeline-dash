Overview
Break free, rewire your mind & create real change. A hands-on 2.5 day workshop hosted by Sue Bromley, certified NCS Consultant
Dr Joe Dispenza's CHANGE YOUR MIND...CREATE NEW RESULTS
Dr Joe Dispenza is a world renown speaker, lecturer and New York times best selling author has developed a unique program, Change Your Mind…Create New Results, for individuals interested in using neuroscientific principles to optimise their mind and therefore their life.
The Change Your Mind…Create New Results program guides participants through a peronalised process using Dr Joe’s models and tools to change their thinking and create the results they desire, because if you believe your thoughts have anything to do with your destiny then we must master our thoughts, feelings and actions to be the creators of our lives and not the victims of it.
Armed with these tools, people are increasing their effectiveness in every area of their life with clear measurable outcomes and manifesting the life they desire.
Based on the latest research in the science of change, Sue Bromley brings to people the information and tools necessary to transform themselves. This interactive 2.5 day course is based on the principles of Dr Joe Dispenza’s groundbreaking work and his scientific research on personal transformation..
In this two-day course, Sue will provide brain-based, outcome-driven methodologies and frameworks that will help participants facilitate positive change and live more effectively.
CHANGE YOUR MIND...CREATE NEW RESULTS is the only global training designed by Dr Joe Dispenza.
Participants of this course will receive:
• CHANGE YOUR MIND...CREATE NEW RESULTS Workbook written by Dr Joe Dispenza.
• CHANGE YOUR MIND...CREATE NEW RESULTS 30 Days to Genius Journal, a powerful guide for the 30 days following the completion of the course.
• Audio downloadable files of all videos shown of Dr Joe Dispenza during the course.
• Audio downloadable files of two meditations shared during the course, for your personal use.
(Please note these workbooks and meditations are not available for purchase at any other course or event.)
Some of the knowledge obtained through this course:
• What is change?
• How to implement a simple formula to begin making changes in your life
• How to think greater than your body
• How to think greater than your environment
• How to think greater than time
• How to move from living in survival to living in creation
• Understanding your 3 brains that allow you to go from Thinking to Doing to Being and how to access your success “sweet spot”
• How to change your brainwaves for optimal performance
• How to design your successful future self
• How to create your personal 30 days to Genius plan
This workshop will run over a Friday night and Saturday and Sunday at Ngutungka Henley in their state of the art new community centre. Lunch and refreshments will be provided.
You will walk away with all the integrative tools and meditations to make this part of your life and begin to conciously create life the way you want it to be.
