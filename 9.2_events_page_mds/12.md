#  WBCSD Connect at Ecosperity Week 2026 
Start date 
18 May, 2026 
End date 
21 May, 2026 
Location 
Singapore 
Share:
Asia sits at the center of the global sustainability transition. As economies across the region pursue growth, industrial transformation, and energy security, they must also navigate rising climate risk, nature loss, and increasing expectations from capital markets and regulators. 
At **Ecosperity Week 2026** , WBCSD will convene its global and regional member community alongside policymakers, financial institutions, and ecosystem partners to accelerate practical, business-led solutions for Asia’s transition. 
WBCSD Connect in Singapore will focus on aligning finance, data, and innovation to: 
• Strengthen long-term resilience and competitiveness 
• Scale decarbonization across value chains 
• Mobilize capital toward high-integrity climate and nature solutions 
• Integrate physical and transition risks into financial decision-making 
• Enable an inclusive, people-centered transition across APAC 
Through a curated series of roundtables, workshops, leadership dialogues, and public sessions, we will move from ambition to implementation — turning complexity into coordinated action.

**About Ecosperity Week 2026**
Ecosperity Week is Temasek’s annual flagship sustainability event. Temasek is a global investment company with a net portfolio valued at S$434 billion (US$324 billion) as at 31 March 2025. 
Themed “Asia’s Race towards 2030: Powered by Innovation, Driven with Intent”, Ecosperity Week 2026 will convene decision makers, innovators and partners to advance system level solutions that can accelerate Asia’s transition towards a future that is both dynamic and sustainable. Ecosperity Week 2026 takes place from 18 to 21 May 2026 in Singapore.
