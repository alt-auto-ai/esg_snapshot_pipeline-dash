## **Web Description**
Join us for an afternoon of networking and conversation at our Governance Institute of Australia’s Member Exchange. This event is a perfect opportunity for members to connect over a casual lunch. Whether you're looking to share insights, discuss industry trends, or simply enjoy good company, this gathering is designed to foster meaningful connections within our community. 
Please note this event is self-funded and attendees will cover their own lunch and coffee/drink.
Members are encouraged to purchase their lunch and bring it into the session ready to commence at 1pm.
##  **What is in it for you?**
  * A relaxed environment for members to meet and build professional relationships. 


  * Exchange of ideas and experiences related to governance and industry trends. 


  * Opportunities for collaboration and mutual support among members. 


## **Speaker Highlights**
**Chair: Kim Daire, Sustainability Governance Expert, Subcinctus Consulting**
**![](https://www.governanceinstitute.com.au/app/uploads/2026/03/Kim-Daire-Sustainability-Governance-Expert-Subcinctus-Consulting.jpg)**
Kim Daire is an experienced sustainability and climate governance professional. Her experience spans the public, private and NFP sectors and includes climate mitigation, climate risk and resilience, sustainable water management, sustainability and stakeholder engagement. She first started specialising in climate risk and resilience in 2012. Kim is the former Non-Executive Director and Treasurer of the Climate and Health Alliance and a graduate of the GIA. Kim has a strong interest in management of change, and the barriers and enablers that enable corporations to turn strategy into implementation.
