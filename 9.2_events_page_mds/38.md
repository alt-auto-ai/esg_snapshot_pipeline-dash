Overview
Join our workshop to discuss how to install a rooftop solar power system on your home or business and take advantage of new incentives.
Over 1530 local households across Woollahra area have installed rooftop solar systems, including many in our heritage conservation areas.
Join our free workshop for tips on installing solar and batteries for your home or business. Find out how a solar system can help reduce your energy bills and carbon pollution.
In this workshop you will learn:
  * Where solar panels can be placed on your roof
  * How to select quality solar and battery products
  * What to look for in a solar quote
  * What to look for in an installer
  * How the new NSW government incentives work
