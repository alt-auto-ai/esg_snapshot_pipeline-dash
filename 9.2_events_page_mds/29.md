Overview
We're back! Join us for a roundtable navigating the contexts and impacts of Australia's identity and systems to co-create the future.
Welcome to Reapra Roundtable, a platform for discursive discussion navigating the roots that run deep in Australia's identity and cultural landscape, the social and economic systems that support it, and how we might choose to interact with it to co-create generational impact.
Bring your lived experience and curious minds and join our roundtabl-ists from many worlds to discuss not just societal problems, but the underlying tensions and the overwhelming need for long term thinking.
Learn stories of lived experience, explore systemic problems and grapple with other perspectives.
  

The event will loosely be divided into:
  1. The development and effects of Australia's identity and cultural landscape over time and the resulting systemic and social challenges.
  2. How these challenges invite us to co-create responses and cultivate generational impact for long term wellbeing.


  

See you there!
