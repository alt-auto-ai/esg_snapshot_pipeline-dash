# Data ↔ Power Melbourne
##  Understanding the evolving risks and opportunities of data centre energy

Recent events reveal a critical disconnect; energy providers and data centre operators are "talking past each other." While the energy sector explores grid support and project underwriting, operators remain focused on immediate power access and energy security.
**Data Power** is your chance to ask the experts the burning questions you have. It’s a safe space to learn about data centre energy use, where growth is happening and what it will mean for those working in both energy and technology.
Improve your understanding of the data centre sector, the evolving policy landscape and the technical realities behind the market opportunity.
