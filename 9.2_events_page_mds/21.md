Overview
Practical tools to protect your health, mindset, and business sustainability
Running a business can be rewarding—but it can also be relentless. ****Building Resilience: Wellbeing for Business Owners**** is a practical, empowering session designed specifically for small business owners, sole traders, and managers who are feeling the pressure of day-to-day operations and constant responsibility. This workshop goes beyond theory, offering evidence-based tools to help you recognise the early signs of stress and burnout, strengthen your mental and physical resilience, and protect both your health and your business sustainability. You’ll gain a clear understanding of what resilience really means in the context of business ownership, create a personalised wellbeing toolkit you can rely on long after the session ends, and learn about local and professional support services available to you. If you’re ready to feel more in control, energised, and equipped to thrive—not just survive—this session will give you the strategies to make it happen.
