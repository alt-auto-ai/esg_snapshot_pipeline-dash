# Brisbane Directors’ Workshop: Finding Board Positions
13th May 2026 
Brisbane, QLD
#### Are you prepared for your first or subsequent board position?  
Applying for a director role is different to applying for your executive roles. Being prepared, developing a director career path and actively mapping out your plan to find board appointments are essential for success.
### When
Wednesday, 13th May 2026 
8:45 am to 2:00 pm AEST

### CPD Points
This event will earn you 5 CPD points
This workshop will provide you with practical tools to assist you with the recruitment process when applying for a director position.
Join our experienced directors as they share their knowledge on:
  * How they obtained their first directorships
  * Obtain guidance on the director recruitment process
  * How to make your director CV stand out from other applicants
  * Tips and tricks on the interview process for Non-Executive Directors 


As part of the workshop there will be a dedicated session on “How to write your Director CV”. We encourage all event attendees to bring with them on the day their current executive and non-executive director curriculum vitae.
Take advantage of this opportunity to network with your peers and position yourself for the next big step in your career.
The workshop will formally conclude at 1.00pm which will be followed by lunch and networking.
