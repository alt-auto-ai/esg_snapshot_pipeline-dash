Overview
This hands-on workshop will teach you about making quick pickles to preserve your summer harvest.
Ever wanted to say money by preserving food while it is in season and cheap? Or have you ever grown too many vegetables that you don't know what to do with them?
This hands-on workshop will teach you about making quick pickles to preserve your summer harvest. Come and pickle carrots, cucumber, zucchini and more (60-90mins). Afternoon tea is also provided. Concession tickets are available on request
