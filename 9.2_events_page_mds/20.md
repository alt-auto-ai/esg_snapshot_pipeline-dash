Overview
Interest in electric vehicles (EVs) is skyrocketing and many people who are considering the switch have questions about the ins and outs of driving an EV, especially if you live in Yarra.
Come along for an informative session hosted by Yarra Council to hear from experts and local EV owners about:
  * How EVs are better for the environment and for total cost of ownership
  * General considerations when choosing an EV
  * Overcoming range anxiety
  * The basics of charging
  * Experiences from EV owners in Yarra who can’t charge at home
  * Charging from home: do you need to install an EV charger?
  * Charging in an apartment block
  * How Yarra is helping residents with resources and expansion of the public charging network


Whether you’re EV-curious or ready to make the switch, this is your chance to get clear, practical answers from people who’ve already made the journey. Join the conversation, hear real local experiences, and leave feeling confident about what going electric in Yarra could look like for you.
