## Swell Sessions at The Scarborough | Outdoor Cinema Fundraiser  

Salty films, clifftop views, and a cause worth getting behind.
Join us this May as we fire up the outdoor screen for a run of surf-inspired films, all in support of Surfers for Climate.
We’ll also be hosting a special panel discussion with Matt Loft and Josh Kirkman, sharing insights from the film and the fight to protect places like Scott Reef.
With thanks to Capital Brewing, your ticket includes a free schooner on arrival, plus every Capital beer purchased on the night helps fund real action for our coastlines.
  
****Film Screenings****
  * ****Thoughtful Larrikins****  
An original Surfers for Climate film exploring the intersection of surf culture, community and climate action.
  * ****Wild Waves Forever - Save Scott Reef****  
A compelling short documentary following the fight to protect one of Australia’s most pristine and threatened marine environments.
  * ****Fight for the Bight Series****  
A selection of short films from the World Heritage campaign to safeguard the Great Australian Bight.


  
****Guest Speakers****
****Josh Kirkman****  
 __Chief Executive Officer,____Surfers for Climate__
Josh brings a unique blend of climate communications expertise and deep roots in the surf community. With a background in Nordic cleantech investment and a decorated career in competitive bodyboarding, he’s passionate about mobilising the surf community to create real political and environmental change.  
  
****Matt Loft****  
 __Camera Operator & Editor, Kingtide Productions__
Based in Thirroul, Matt works across film and television, including __Mark Berg's Fishing Addiction__. He features in the Scott Reef film and brings a grounded, firsthand perspective from his travels to the reef.

## Swell Sessions at The Scarborough | Outdoor Cinema Fundraiser  

Salty films, clifftop views, and a cause worth getting behind.
Join us this May as we fire up the outdoor screen for a run of surf-inspired films, all in support of Surfers for Climate.
We’ll also be hosting a special panel discussion with Matt Loft and Josh Kirkman, sharing insights from the film and the fight to protect places like Scott Reef.
With thanks to Capital Brewing, your ticket includes a free schooner on arrival, plus every Capital beer purchased on the night helps fund real action for our coastlines.
  
****Film Screenings****
  * ****Thoughtful Larrikins****  
An original Surfers for Climate film exploring the intersection of surf culture, community and climate action.
  * ****Wild Waves Forever - Save Scott Reef****  
A compelling short documentary following the fight to protect one of Australia’s most pristine and threatened marine environments.
  * ****Fight for the Bight Series****  
A selection of short films from the World Heritage campaign to safeguard the Great Australian Bight.


  
****Guest Speakers****
****Josh Kirkman****  
 __Chief Executive Officer,____Surfers for Climate__
Josh brings a unique blend of climate communications expertise and deep roots in the surf community. With a background in Nordic cleantech investment and a decorated career in competitive bodyboarding, he’s passionate about mobilising the surf community to create real political and environmental change.  
  
****Matt Loft****  
 __Camera Operator & Editor, Kingtide Productions__
Based in Thirroul, Matt works across film and television, including __Mark Berg's Fishing Addiction__. He features in the Scott Reef film and brings a grounded, firsthand perspective from his travels to the reef.

## Swell Sessions at The Scarborough | Outdoor Cinema Fundraiser  

Salty films, clifftop views, and a cause worth getting behind.
Join us this May as we fire up the outdoor screen for a run of surf-inspired films, all in support of Surfers for Climate.
We’ll also be hosting a special panel discussion with Matt Loft and Josh Kirkman, sharing insights from the film and the fight to protect places like Scott Reef.
With thanks to Capital Brewing, your ticket includes a free schooner on arrival, plus every Capital beer purchased on the night helps fund real action for our coastlines.
  
****Film Screenings****
  * ****Thoughtful Larrikins****  
An original Surfers for Climate film exploring the intersection of surf culture, community and climate action.
  * ****Wild Waves Forever - Save Scott Reef****  
A compelling short documentary following the fight to protect one of Australia’s most pristine and threatened marine environments.
  * ****Fight for the Bight Series****  
A selection of short films from the World Heritage campaign to safeguard the Great Australian Bight.


  
****Guest Speakers****
****Josh Kirkman****  
 __Chief Executive Officer,____Surfers for Climate__
Josh brings a unique blend of climate communications expertise and deep roots in the surf community. With a background in Nordic cleantech investment and a decorated career in competitive bodyboarding, he’s passionate about mobilising the surf community to create real political and environmental change.  
  
****Matt Loft****  
 __Camera Operator & Editor, Kingtide Productions__
Based in Thirroul, Matt works across film and television, including __Mark Berg's Fishing Addiction__. He features in the Scott Reef film and brings a grounded, firsthand perspective from his travels to the reef.
