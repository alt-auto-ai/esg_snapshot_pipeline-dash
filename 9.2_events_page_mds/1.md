ISO and the Greenhouse Gas Protocol are pleased to invite you to the second webinar in the COP Action Agenda series on harmonizing global carbon accounting standards, with a focus on product-level accounting.
Join us on 24 April at 9:00am EST/3:00pm CEST to hear why product-level accounting is critical in enabling transparency, comparability and informed decision-making across value chains and explore the latest developments in this space.
The session will also provide insight into the joint work underway to advance alignment, highlight how stakeholders can support the process, and offer an opportunity to ask questions.
