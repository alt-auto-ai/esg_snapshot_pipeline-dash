This is a unique opportunity to hear from leading directors as they dissect the big issues facing boards today.
Our board will delve into a thought-provoking case study which will explore themes and challenges faced in contemporary governance. You are also encouraged to join in the conversation with the opportunity to ask questions of the board and challenge their decisions.
The Perth Biggest Boardroom Lunch is a unique professional development opportunity for you and your fellow board members. You will take away practical learnings and continue the conversation back in your own boardroom.
This is a popular event in the AICD calendar and with limited seats available, early booking is essential.
