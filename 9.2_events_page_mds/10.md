**Agentic AI is moving fast in the workplace. Stay ahead of the curve. Join our free webinar launching our latest White Paper on Governing in the age of agentic AI.**
60 percent of Australian businesses surveyed say they are already deploying agentic AI technology and over 90 percent are using or making plans to adopt agentic AI within the next six months. Gartner claims that over 40 percent of agentic AI projects will be cancelled by the end of 2027 due to escalating costs, unclear business value propositions and inadequate risk and governance frameworks. 
Join us for a free webinar launching **Governing in the age of Agentic AI white paper** - a major thought‑leadership initiative sponsored by Diligent, Mallesons, the University of Melbourne (CAIDE), SEEK and EthicAI. The White paper examines the technical, legal, regulatory and ethical challenges AI agents raise, and offer practical guidance on how to effectively oversee, govern, and manage increasingly autonomous AI agents in the workplace as and their usage becomes more widespread.
Footnotes
The paper offers practical guidance on:
  * The technical, ethical, legal, and regulatory foundations to ensure you stay ahead of the curve.
  * What goes into developing an Agentic AI oversight, governance, and risk management framework.
  * Best-practice governance and oversight deployment strategy through a use case of a multi-agent digital taskforce.


This webinar will unpack the key findings from the research and discuss their implications for organisations navigating an increasingly complex AI landscape.
## Speaker Highlights:
**Katriel Healy, Co‑Founder, EthicAI**
Katriel Healy is a People & Culture leader with over a decade of experience supporting Boards and Executive Leadership Teams navigate change, align people strategy to business performance, and build resilient, purpose-led cultures.
Drawing on her background in organisational development, advertising, and psychology, she designs and delivers people strategies that connect employee experience to organisational objectives. She has led large-scale transformation initiatives across workforce redesign, commercial restructuring, DE&I, HR compliance, employee engagement, and leadership development. A certified Organisational Coach, Katriel is passionate about supporting leaders to bring out the best in themselves and their teams. 
**Fernando Mourão, Head of Responsible AI, Seek**
Fernando Mourão is Head of Responsible AI at SEEK, where he transforms responsible AI principles into operational reality for enterprise-scale deployments. With a decade of experience across AI, data science, and recommender systems, Fernando combines deep technical expertise with strategic governance leadership. His academic foundation, a PhD in Computer Science and executive education from MIT Sloan School of Management, informs his pragmatic approach to bridging AI strategy, governance, and execution. Beyond SEEK, Fernando serves on the Governance Institute of Australia's AI Governance Expert Advisory Panel, contributing to industry-wide best practices. His leadership has earned recognition as a finalist in the 2025 Australian Financial Review AI Awards (Ethics and Responsibility). An AIGP-certified professional with 50+ academic publications, Fernando is dedicated to advancing responsible AI from the frameworks to the front lines, championing governance that keeps pace with innovation while building trust and delivering business value. **
