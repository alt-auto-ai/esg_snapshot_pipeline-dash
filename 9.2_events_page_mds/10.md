Topic
Cities
This half-day event is dedicated to knowledge sharing and discussions on pathways to improve paratransit services in developing cities.
Centered around the theme: “Advancing the Sector: From Understanding to Action,” this year’s event will combine plenary presentations, a roundtable and interactive workshops to collectively reflect on key themes for the industry.
### Objectives:
  * Take stock of the latest trends and evidence on paratransit across regions
  * Identify concrete action areas
  * Strengthen collaboration between key stakeholders


### Who should attend:
  * City and national government officials working on urban mobility
  * Civil society and researchers
  * Industry representatives
  * Technical and financial partners involved in the sector
