# Melbourne Director Download - Geopolitical Disruption and Resilience Planning
30th April 2026 
Melbourne, VIC
#### In today's volatile landscape, boards must anticipate and navigate macro-level disruptions, including shifting trade alliances, regulatory rollbacks, regional conflicts, and social unrest. 
#### Given the speed and scope of these global challenges, reactive governance models are no longer viable.

Resilience planning, stakeholder mapping, and geopolitical risk assessments should be proactive and regular agenda items, not just emergency topics. A forward-thinking approach is crucial for building a resilient organization that can withstand and adapt to the complex geopolitical forces shaping our world.
