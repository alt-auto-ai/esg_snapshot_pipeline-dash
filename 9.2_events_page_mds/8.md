#### This year’s study highlights:
  * Increased governance workload– with implications for the role of the non-executive director and their relationship with the CEO and management 
  * Evolving discussions around director remuneration and board succession planning
  * Profitability challenges for many sub-sectors
  * Challenges and opportunities in measuring organisational purpose
  * Heightened accountability for care governance, quality and client outcomes
  * Emerging governance risks and opportunities across cyber security, artificial intelligence and climate governance

Have you ever imagined what it would be like to be an unobtrusive observer in the boardrooms where Australia's most influential decisions are made? This is a unique opportunity to hear from leading directors as they dissect the big issues facing boards today.
Our board will delve into a thought-provoking case study which will explore themes and challenges faced in contemporary governance. You are also encouraged to join in the conversation with the opportunity to ask questions of the board and challenge their decisions.
The Brisbane Biggest Boardroom Lunch is a unique professional development opportunity for you and your fellow board members. You will take away practical learnings and continue the conversation back in your own boardroom.
