### [Joint Communiqué of Tenth African Union-United Nations Annual Conference](https://press.un.org/en/2026/sg2267.doc.htm)
On 13 May 2026, the Chairperson of the African Union Commission, Mahmoud Ali Youssouf and the Secretary-General of the United Nations, António Guterres, convened the tenth African Union–United Nations Annual Conference at the African Union Headquarters in Addis Ababa. They reaffirmed their commitment to the strategic partnership between the United Nations and the African Union as a cornerstone for advancing peace and security, human rights, sustainable development and accelerating progress on African Union Agenda 2063 and the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. They agreed that cooperation between the two organizations rests on collective coordinated action to address complex global and regional challenges. They expressed deep concern over the multiplication of the prevailing global and continental challenges, including armed conflicts, humanitarian crises, poverty and inequalities, climate change and disregard for international law and shared principles.
