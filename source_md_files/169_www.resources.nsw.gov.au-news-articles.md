Login changes for TMS and NSW Resources Portal
A new login experience is coming for all Titles Management System and NSW Resources Portal (formerly ‘Regulator’ portal) accounts to strengthen security and simplify your experience. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) will soon be required for all accounts and login options will be extended to include Google, Microsoft and Apple. The changes will include new Term of Use. Find out more, including how to set up MFA.
